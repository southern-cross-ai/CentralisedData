House debates

Thursday, 21 November 2013

Questions without Notice

Fishing Industry

2:18 pm

Kevin Hogan (Page, National Party) | Hansard source

My question is to the Minister for Agriculture. I remind the minister that today is World Fisheries Day, to recognise and celebrate the sustainability of Australian fisheries. What impact has the carbon tax had on this iconic Australian industry? And how will the government's plans restore the future prospects of Australian fisheries?

2:19 pm

Barnaby Joyce (New England, National Party, Minister for Agriculture) | Hansard source

I thank the honourable member for his question. With a seat that includes Ballina and Yamba, he has a very keen understanding of the importance of the fishing industry.

It is also important because one of the inflictions on that industry has come about by the Labor Party, in their desire to single-handedly cool the planet with a tax. We all know that if Labor taxes cooled the planet the place would be an icebox, but it is quite clear that the Labor Party had decided that they would inflict this on fishermen.
