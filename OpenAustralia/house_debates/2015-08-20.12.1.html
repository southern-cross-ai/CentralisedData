House debates

Thursday, 20 August 2015

Committees

National Capital and External Territories Committee; Membership

Russell Broadbent (McMillan, Liberal Party) | Hansard source

I have received advice from the Chief Government Whip that he has nominated Mrs BK Bishop to be a member of the Joint Standing Committee on the National Capital and External Territories in place of Mrs Griggs.

9:52 am

Michael McCormack (Riverina, National Party, Parliamentary Secretary to the Minister for Finance) | Hansard source

I ask leave of the House to move a motion for the appointment of a member to the Joint Standing Committee on the National Capital and External Territories.

Leave granted.

I move:

That Mrs Griggs be discharged from the Joint Standing Committee on the National Capital and External Territories and that, in her place, Mrs B. K. Bishop be appointed a member of the committee.

Question agreed to.
