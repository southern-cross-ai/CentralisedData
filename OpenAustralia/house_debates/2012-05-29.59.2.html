House debates

Tuesday, 29 May 2012

Questions without Notice: Additional Answers

Carbon Pricing

3:07 pm

Anthony Albanese (Grayndler, Australian Labor Party, Leader of the House) | Hansard source

Madam Deputy Speaker, I seek the indulgence of the chair to add to an answer.

Ms Anna Burke (Chisholm, Deputy-Speaker) | Hansard source

The minister may proceed.

Anthony Albanese (Grayndler, Australian Labor Party, Leader of the House) | Hansard source

Today I was asked by the Chief Government Whip about the impact on domestic airlines of the price on carbon, and I quoted a media release from Ian Vanderbeek from Aeropelican and Brindabella Airlines . Just to put some of this in context, I will table the AEC return from 2009-10 disclosing a donation to the National Party of $13,200 and the AEC return from 2010-2011 from the National Party, indicating a donation to the National Party of Australia of a further $8,000.

3:08 pm

Warren Truss (Wide Bay, National Party, Leader of the Nationals) | Hansard source

I seek leave to table a press release from Rex Airlines announcing the cancellation of the Griffith-Melbourne service because of the carbon tax—

Ms Anna Burke (Chisholm, Deputy-Speaker) | Hansard source

The Leader of the Nationals will resume his seat. Leave is not granted.

Honourable members interjecting —

Order! Question time has concluded.
