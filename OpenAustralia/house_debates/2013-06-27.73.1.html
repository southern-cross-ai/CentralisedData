2:52 pm

Kevin Rudd (Griffith, Australian Labor Party, Prime Minister) | Hansard source

I thank the honourable member for his question. Any industrial accident in Australia is one accident too many. Any industrial death in Australia is one death too many. Each year we have thousands of such deaths across Australia and those four that the honourable member referred to were a source of enormous grief to their families and loved ones. As for the insulation program, the honourable member will be familiar with the extensive debate which occurred in this chamber in the previous parliament.

Tony Abbott (Warringah, Liberal Party, Leader of the Opposition) | Hansard source

Speaker, on a point of order: the Prime Minister was asked to release all documents and correspondence between himself and the former minister and he should respond to that.

Ms Anna Burke (Speaker) | Hansard source

Order! The Leader of the Opposition will resume his seat. The Deputy Prime Minister will resume his seat. The Prime Minister has the call and is being relevant to the question.
