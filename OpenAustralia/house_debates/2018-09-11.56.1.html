Questions without Notice

Minister for Home Affairs

2:28 pm

Shayne Neumann (Blair, Australian Labor Party, Shadow Minister for Immigration and Border Protection) | Hansard source

My question is for the Minister for Home Affairs . In question time on 27 March, when asked whether he could categorically rule out any personal connection between himself and the intended employers of the au pairs, the minister answered, 'Yes, and I don't know these people'. How can the minister expect the parliament to believe he didn't know the intended employer of the Italian au pair when an email he sent to the minister, titled 'Call required', begins, 'Peter, long time between calls'?
