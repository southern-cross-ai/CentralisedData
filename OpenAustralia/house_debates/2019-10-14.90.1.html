House debates

Monday, 14 October 2019

Questions without Notice

Houston, Brian

2:50 pm

Richard Marles (Corio, Australian Labor Party, Shadow Minister for Defence) | Hansard source

My question is to the Prime Minister. Did the Prime Minister or his office seek to have Brian Houston invited to the White House ?

Scott Morrison (Cook, Liberal Party, Prime Minister) | Hansard source

As the member would know, the invitations to the White House dinner were issued by the White House. That's a matter for the White House. I can tell you, Mr Speaker, I think the Australian people are more interested in who Jamie Clements was inviting to lunch and who he was inviting to dinner, on behalf of the New South Wales Labor Party, and how much cash was in the wine bag.

Tony Smith (Speaker) | Hansard source

The Deputy Leader of the Opposition.

Richard Marles (Corio, Australian Labor Party, Shadow Minister for Defence) | Hansard source

My point of order is on relevance.

Tony Smith (Speaker) | Hansard source

Yes, I made that point. The Prime Minister's indicated he has concluded his answer.
