Questions without Notice: Additional Answers

Goods and Services Tax

3:14 pm

Barnaby Joyce (New England, National Party, Minister for Agriculture) | Hansard source

Madam Speaker, I would like to add to an answer from today.

Mrs Bronwyn Bishop (Speaker) | Hansard source

The minister is seeking the call for—

Honourable members interjecting —

There will be silence! The Leader of the Opposition will desist. The Minister for Agriculture is seeking the call for what purpose?

Barnaby Joyce (New England, National Party, Minister for Agriculture) | Hansard source

I would like to add to an answer I gave today. Today I stated that the Labor Party currently has an agriculture policy, but it is actually from Hawker Britton . It probably still does not have a policy. On the National Farmers' Federation , I did not realise that the NFF is writing agriculture policy for the Labor Policy.

Mrs Bronwyn Bishop (Speaker) | Hansard source

The member is out of order. This is not adding to an answer.

Barnaby Joyce (New England, National Party, Minister for Agriculture) | Hansard source

It is not a policy; it is a comment.

Mrs Bronwyn Bishop (Speaker) | Hansard source

This is not adding to an answer.
