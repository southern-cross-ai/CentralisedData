Questions to the Speaker

Questions in Writing

I will write to the minister as required under standing order 105(b).

3:17 pm

Andrew Southcott (Boothby, Liberal Party, Shadow Parliamentary Secretary for Primary Healthcare) | Hansard source

Harry Jenkins (Speaker) | Hansard source

I will do as required under standing order 105(b).

3:18 pm

Paul Fletcher (Bradfield, Liberal Party) | Hansard source
