House debates

Wednesday, 13 June 2007

Notices

The following notices were given:

Peter McGauran (Gippsland, National Party, Deputy Leader of the House) | Hansard source

to present a bill for an act to amend the Wheat Marketing Act 1989, and for other purposes. (Wheat Marketing Amendment Bill 2007)

Philip Ruddock (Berowra, Liberal Party, Attorney-General) | Hansard source

to present a bill for an act to implement the Australian Government ’s response to recommendations made by the Inquiry into Certain Australian Companies in relation to the United Nations Oil-for- Food Programme , and for other purposes. (International Trade Integrity Bill 2007)

to present a bill for an act to amend the law in relation to Judges’ pensions, and for related purposes. (Judges’ Pensions Amendment Bill 2007)

to present a bill for an act to amend the Telecommunications (Interception and Access) Act 1979, and for other purposes. (Telecommunications (Interception and Access) Amendment Bill 2007)

Peter Lindsay (Herbert, Liberal Party, Parliamentary Secretary to the Minister for Defence) | Hansard source

to move:

·              That, in accordance with the provisions of the Public Works Committee Act 1969, it is expedient to carry out the following proposed work which was referred to the Parliamentary Standing Committee on Public Works and on which the committee has duly reported to Parliament: National Towers Program Stage 1 for Airservices Australia at Adelaide, Canberra, Melbourne and Rockhampton airports.

to move:

·              That, in accordance with the provisions of the Public Works Committee Act 1969, it is expedient to carry out the following proposed work which was referred to the Parliamentary Standing Committee on Public Works and on which the committee has duly reported to Parliament: Lavarack Barracks Redevelopment Stage 4, Townsville, Qld.

to move:

·              That, in accordance with the provisions of the Public Works Committee Act 1969, it is expedient to carry out the following proposed work which was referred to the Parliamentary Standing Committee on Public Works and on which the committee has duly reported to Parliament: Defence Force School of Signals Redevelopment at Simpson Barracks , Watsonia, Vic.
