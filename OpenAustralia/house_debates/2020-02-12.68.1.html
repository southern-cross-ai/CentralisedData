Questions without Notice

Employment

2:30 pm

Jim Chalmers (Rankin, Australian Labor Party, Shadow Treasurer) | Hansard source

My question is to the Treasurer. Can the Treasurer confirm that, under the Morrison government, annual wages growth in every single quarter has been lower than in any quarter under the last Labor government?

The reality is that under Labor the real minimum wage went down in three out of the six years they were in government. Under us, real minimum wages have gone up every year.
