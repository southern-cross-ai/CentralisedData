House debates

Tuesday, 13 September 2011

Bills

Indigenous Affairs Legislation Amendment Bill (No. 2) 2011; Consideration in Detail

7:31 pm

Julie Collins (Franklin, Australian Labor Party, Parliamentary Secretary for Community Services) | Hansard source

I present a supplementary explanatory memorandum to the bill and I move:

That on (1)   Schedule 2, page 16 (lines 1 to 18), omit the Schedule.

7:32 pm

Kevin Andrews (Menzies, Liberal Party, Shadow Minister for Families, Housing and Human Services) | Hansard source

I will not detain the House for long. I simply repeat what I said in my remarks in the second reading debate, and that is that the coalition was opposed to this schedule and therefore we support the amendment to have it withdrawn.

Question agreed to.

Bill, as amended, agreed to.
