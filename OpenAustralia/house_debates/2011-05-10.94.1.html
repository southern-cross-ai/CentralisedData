Questions in Writing

Prime Minister: Expenditure on Residences (Question No. 235)

Jamie Briggs (Mayo, Liberal Party, Chairman of the Scrutiny of Government Waste Committee) | Hansard source

asked the Prime Minister, in writing, on 3 March 2011:

What is the breakdown of costs of all capital works, acquisitions, and contractual services at (a) The Lodge , (b) Kirribilli House , (c) her apartment in Canberra, and (d) her home in Altona, since 23 June 2010.

Julia Gillard (Lalor, Australian Labor Party, Prime Minister) | Hansard source

I am advised that the answer to the honourable member's question is as follows:

(a) The Lodge

(b) Kirribilli House

(c) Private Canberra residence – Nil.

(d) Private Altona residence – Nil.
