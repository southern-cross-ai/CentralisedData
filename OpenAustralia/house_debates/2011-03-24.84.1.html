Bennelong Electorate: Armenian Community

1:53 pm

John Alexander (Bennelong, Liberal Party) | Hansard source

Bennelong is fortunate to have an active Armenian community, proudly displaying their great heritage, including a mayor and a local councillor. An issue of great importance to this community is the lack of appropriate recognition of the genocide by the Ottoman Empire , tragically linking our two nations, as Anzac troops landed in Gallipoli at the same time just a short distance away. The world has turned many times since then and the Turkish people welcome us back each year to commemorate a tragedy that has formed such an important part of our national legend. I look forward to the day that the Turkish and Armenian people can build a similar bridge and come to terms with their own tragedy.
