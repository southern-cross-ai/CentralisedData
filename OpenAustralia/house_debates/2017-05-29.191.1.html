Yeronga State School Fete

4:43 pm

Graham Perrett (Moreton, Australian Labor Party) | Hansard source

On Saturday I went to the Yeronga State School fete and helped out on the barbeque stall. On behalf of the organising committee, I want to thank many of the volunteers connected with that wonderful event, which is held only every two years. In particular I thank Sarah, Kate, Gretta, Leesa, Amy, Andrea, Faith, Megan, Sandra, Kristen and Sara.
