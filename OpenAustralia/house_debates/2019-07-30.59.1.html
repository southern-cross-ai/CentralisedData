House debates

Tuesday, 30 July 2019

Questions without Notice

Pensions and Benefits

2:34 pm

Anthony Albanese (Grayndler, Australian Labor Party, Leader of the Opposition) | Hansard source

My question is addressed to the Minister for Government Services. Have any victims of the Townsville flood received debt notices under the government's robo-debt program?

2:35 pm

Stuart Robert (Fadden, Liberal Party, Minister for the National Disability Insurance Scheme) | Hansard source

Let me thank the Leader of the Opposition for his question, and let me say, at present, under the income compliance program, no victims in the four postcodes around Townsville have received a debt notice.
