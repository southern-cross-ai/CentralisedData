Kingsway Christian College

1:48 pm

Luke Simpkins (Cowan, Liberal Party) | Hansard source

I would like to mention Kingsway Christian College today. During the election campaign I had the honour of attending a couple of sessions with their senior students to talk about their responsibilities as potential college captains and deputy college captains. I was fortunate enough to hear them make a number of speeches that I could give them a little bit of feedback on. They then made speeches to the whole school and four students were voted into the highest positions. I would just like to mention that the college captains for 2011 are Tarbie van Tonder and Paul Jones , and the vice-captains are Kelsey Hall and Ross Conradie.
