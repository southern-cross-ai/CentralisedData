House debates

Thursday, 17 July 2014

Condolences

Lance Corporal Todd Chidgey

2:00 pm

Mrs Bronwyn Bishop (Speaker) | Hansard source

I report that the order of the day relating to the Prime Minister's motion of condolence in connection with the death of Lance Corporal Todd Chidgey has been debated in the Federation Chamber and is returned to the House. I present a certified copy of the motion. I understand it is the wish of the House to consider the matter immediately. The question is that the motion moved by the honourable the Prime Minister be agreed to. As a mark of respect, I ask all present to signify their approval by rising in their places, and that includes the people in the gallery.

Question agreed to, honourable members standing in their places.

I thank the House and those others present.
