House debates

Wednesday, 17 March 2021

Condolences

Hurford, Hon. Christopher John, AO; Reference to Federation Chamber

2:11 pm

Peter Dutton (Dickson, Liberal Party, Minister for Home Affairs) | Hansard source

by leave—I move:

That the resumption of the debate on the Prime Minister's motion of condolence in connection with the death of the Hon. Christopher John Hurford AO be referred to the Federation Chamber.

Question agreed to.

Tony Smith (Speaker) | Hansard source

As a mark of respect, I ask all present to rise in their places.

Honourable members and those in the gallery having stood in their places—

I thank the House.
