Thursday, 21 June 2012

Statements by Members

Horse Racing

1:54 pm

Ken O'Dowd (Flynn, National Party) | Hansard source

Mr Deputy Speaker Scott, a well-known lad from your electorate, Peter Moody , steps out at Ascot on Saturday night with his Australian champion, Black Caviar . We expect a big run from this horse. It will be a little different over there than with his 21 wins out of 21 starts in Australia. He will be running uphill, probably on a heavy track. The distance is about 1,200 metres, which is right up his alley. Let's hope he can—

Ewen Jones (Herbert, Liberal Party) | Hansard source

She!

Ken O'Dowd (Flynn, National Party) | Hansard source

Yes, it is a she—sorry! We are expecting great things and if you get $1.50 you should take it because I think it is pretty good odds.

Ewen Jones (Herbert, Liberal Party) | Hansard source

Get on!

Ken O'Dowd (Flynn, National Party) | Hansard source

Yes, get on! Go, you good thing!

Bruce Scott (Maranoa, National Party) | Hansard source
