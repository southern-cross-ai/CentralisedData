Report summarising the petitions and ministerial responses being presented.

T he committee met in private session in the 47th Parliament on 2 August 2023.

1. The committee resolved to present the following 2 petitions in accordance with standing order 207:

Petitions certified on 2 August 2023

From 401 petitioners—regarding the undergrounding of power lines in the Apollo Bay network ( PN 0564)

From 55 petitioners—requesting an end to the indefinite detention of refugees (PN0566)
