Questions without Notice

Renewable Energy

2:33 pm

Colin Boyce (Flynn, Liberal National Party) | Hansard source

Mr Speaker, my question is to the Minister for Climate Change and Energy. The minister stated in a speech on 24 September 2022 that Australia will need to install 22,000 solar panels every day and 40 wind turbines every month by 2030 to reach the government's 82 per cent renewable energy target. It has been over 240 days since this speech. Can the minister provide an update on how many solar panels and wind turbines have been installed in this time frame?
