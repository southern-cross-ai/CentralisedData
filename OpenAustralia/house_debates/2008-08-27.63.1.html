House debates

Wednesday, 27 August 2008

Questions to the Speaker

Questions in Writing

3:33 pm

Andrew Southcott (Boothby, Liberal Party, Shadow Minister for Employment Participation and Apprenticeships and Training) | Hansard source

Mr Speaker, under standing order 105(b), could you write to the Minister for Employment Participation and ask him to respond to the following questions in writing, which have not been answered within 60 days: Nos 179, 180, 181, 182, 183, 184, 185, 186, 187, 188, 189, 190, 191, 199, 200, 201, 202. Could you also write to the Minister for Infrastructure, Transport, Regional Development and Local Government and ask him to respond to questions Nos 172 and 204. Could you also write to the Minister for Education about question in writing No. 193 and, in her capacity as Minister for Employment and Workplace Relations, question No. 203.

Harry Jenkins (Speaker) | Hansard source

I will take action as required under standing order 105(b).

3:34 pm

Chris Pearce (Aston, Liberal Party) | Hansard source

Mr Speaker, could I also seek your assistance under standing order 105(b). Could you please write to the Treasurer and ask him to respond to question in writing No. 7. It is now over five months since I asked that question. There are also question No. 14 to the Minister for Families, Housing, Community Services and Indigenous Affairs and a question to the Prime Minister, which is now over three months old, question No. 90.

Harry Jenkins (Speaker) | Hansard source

I will take action as required under standing order 105(b).
