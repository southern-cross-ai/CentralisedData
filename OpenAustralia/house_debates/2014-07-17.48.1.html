Carbon Pricing

1:56 pm

Sarah Henderson (Corangamite, Liberal Party) | Hansard source

This is a great day for Corangamiteâ€”for Colac, Geelong, Bannockburn and Winchelsea. Today's vote by the Senate to repeal the carbon tax is great news for Corangamite families and small businesses.
