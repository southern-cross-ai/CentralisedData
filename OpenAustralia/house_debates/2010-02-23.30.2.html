House debates

Tuesday, 23 February 2010

Documents

3:37 pm

Anthony Albanese (Grayndler, Australian Labor Party, Leader of the House) | Hansard source

On indulgence, before I present the documents I acknowledge that 50 years ago today a momentous occasion occurred. Fifty years ago to the day both the Minister for Veterans’ Affairs and the Parliamentary Secretary for Defence Support and Parliamentary Secretary for Water were born. On behalf of everyone in the House, I wish them a happy 50th birthday.

Julia Gillard (Lalor, Australian Labor Party, Deputy Prime Minister) | Hansard source

Are they twins?

Anthony Albanese (Grayndler, Australian Labor Party, Leader of the House) | Hansard source

‘Are they twins?’ the Deputy Prime Minister asks. Mr Speaker, documents are presented as listed in the schedule circulated to honourable members. Details of the documents will be recorded in the Votes and Proceedings .
