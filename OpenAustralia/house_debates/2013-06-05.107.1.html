House debates

Wednesday, 5 June 2013

Personal Explanations

3:42 pm

Christopher Pyne (Sturt, Liberal Party, Shadow Minister for Education, Apprenticeships and Training) | Hansard source

I wish to make a personal explanation.

Ms Anna Burke (Speaker) | Hansard source

Does the honourable member claim to have been misrepresented?

Christopher Pyne (Sturt, Liberal Party, Shadow Minister for Education, Apprenticeships and Training) | Hansard source

Yes.

Ms Anna Burke (Speaker) | Hansard source

Please proceed.

Christopher Pyne (Sturt, Liberal Party, Shadow Minister for Education, Apprenticeships and Training) | Hansard source

In question time today the member for Ballarat said that I had opposed the amendments to the Australian Education Bill. That is false.

3:43 pm

Tony Abbott (Warringah, Liberal Party, Leader of the Opposition) | Hansard source

I wish to make a personal explanation.

Ms Anna Burke (Speaker) | Hansard source

Does the honourable member claim to have been misrepresented?

Tony Abbott (Warringah, Liberal Party, Leader of the Opposition) | Hansard source

Yes.

Ms Anna Burke (Speaker) | Hansard source

Please proceed.

Tony Abbott (Warringah, Liberal Party, Leader of the Opposition) | Hansard source

In question time today, and on other occasions this week, the Prime Minister has claimed that I have sought no intelligence briefings. I have had multiple briefings from the Department of Defence . I have had multiple briefings from ASIO , including from the Director-General. I have been briefed by the Australian Federal Police , by Border Protection Command and, on a number of occasions, by Customs. I have had numerous briefings on intelligence and defence—

Ms Anna Burke (Speaker) | Hansard source

Order! The member needs to show where he has been misrepresented.

Tony Abbott (Warringah, Liberal Party, Leader of the Opposition) | Hansard source

This is the misrepresentation! It is a massive misrepresentation of the facts. And most recently I had an ASIS briefing.
