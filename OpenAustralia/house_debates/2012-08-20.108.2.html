House debates

Monday, 20 August 2012

Personal Explanations

4:34 pm

Tony Abbott (Warringah, Liberal Party, Leader of the Opposition) | Hansard source

I wish to make a personal explanation.

Ms Anna Burke (Chisholm, Deputy-Speaker) | Hansard source

Does the Leader of the Opposition claim to have been misrepresented?

Tony Abbott (Warringah, Liberal Party, Leader of the Opposition) | Hansard source

Most grievously.

Ms Anna Burke (Chisholm, Deputy-Speaker) | Hansard source

The Leader of the Opposition has the call.

Tony Abbott (Warringah, Liberal Party, Leader of the Opposition) | Hansard source

Repeatedly today in question time the Prime Minister claimed that I intended to cut funding to public schools. I said no such thing; I intend no such thing; I would do no such thing. There is only one hit list. It is the Prime Minister's hit list under which one in three schools right around Australia will have their funding cut as the Gonski review stands, even if fully funded.

Ms Anna Burke (Chisholm, Deputy-Speaker) | Hansard source

The Leader of the Opposition will resume his seat. He has indicated where he has been misrepresented.
