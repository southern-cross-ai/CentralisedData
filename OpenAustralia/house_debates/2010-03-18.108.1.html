House debates

Thursday, 18 March 2010

Leave of Absence

4:49 pm

Martin Ferguson (Batman, Australian Labor Party, Minister for Resources and Energy) | Hansard source

I move:

That leave of absence be given to every Member of the House of Representatives :

(1)
from the determination of this sitting of the House to the date of its next sitting; and
(2)
if the House sits on 26 March 2010, from the determination of that sitting to the date of its next sitting.

Question agreed to.
