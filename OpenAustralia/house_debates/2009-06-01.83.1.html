(d)

This will enable a greater participation in the number of budget bills that we have before the chamber that need to be carried this week. It is envisaged that we will also be sitting later tomorrow night and later on Wednesday night. We will endeavour to put in place systems which maximise participation whilst ensuring that disruption of activities such as the party room meetings that occur traditionally on Tuesday morning can also proceed. I thank the House for their cooperation in anticipation of support for this motion.
