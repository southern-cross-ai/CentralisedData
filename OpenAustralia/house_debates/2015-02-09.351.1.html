Grievance Debate

Employment (Question No. 614)

Kelvin Thomson (Wills, Australian Labor Party) | Hansard source

asked the Minister for Industry and Science, in writing, on 14 November 2014:

Will the Minister consider attending a forum on the issues of economic growth, manufacturing, unemployment and job creation in 2015 should I arrange one; if so, what dates or timeframes would suit.

Ian Macfarlane (Groom, Liberal Party, Minister for Industry and Science) | Hansard source

The answer to the honourable member's question is as follows:

Requests for meetings, or my attendance at events, should be put in writing to:

minister.invitations@industry.gov.au.
