My question is to the Prime Minister. Given that the Prime Minister has made a previous statement to the House that she has had private discussions with the member for Dobell, and her reference to media reports that she spoke to the member for Dobell on 28 April about his status as a member of parliament, is it the case that the issue of the payment of the member for Dobell's legal fees was raised with her by the member for Dobell in that conversation on 28 April?

2:29 pm

Julia Gillard (Lalor, Australian Labor Party, Prime Minister) | Hansard source

No, it was not.
