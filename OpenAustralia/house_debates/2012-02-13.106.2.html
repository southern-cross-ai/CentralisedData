House debates

Monday, 13 February 2012

Personal Explanations

3:49 pm

Tony Crook (O'Connor, National Party) | Hansard source

Mr Speaker, I wish to make a personal explanation.

Peter Slipper (Speaker) | Hansard source

Does the honourable member claim to have been misrepresented?

Tony Crook (O'Connor, National Party) | Hansard source

Yes, Mr Speaker.

Peter Slipper (Speaker) | Hansard source

Please proceed.

Tony Crook (O'Connor, National Party) | Hansard source

Today in question time I asked a question of the Prime Minister regarding native title compensation. The Prime Minister stated that she had not heard from the Premier of Western Australia on this issue and for the record I would like say that the Prime Minister received a letter from the premier on 14 April 2011, which she subsequently replied to on 29 May.

Peter Slipper (Speaker) | Hansard source

The member for O'Connor will resume his seat. In a personal explanation he has to show where he personally has been misrepresented, but he has made his point.
