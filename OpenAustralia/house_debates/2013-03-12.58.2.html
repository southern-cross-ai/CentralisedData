Auditor-General's Reports

Report Nos 22, 23, 24, 25 and 26 of 2012-13

3:15 pm

Ms Anna Burke (Speaker) | Hansard source

I present the Auditor-General’s Audit reports 2012-13 entitled Audit report No. 22, Performance audit—Administration of the Tasmanian Forests Intergovernmental Agreement Contractors Voluntary Exit Grants Program— Department of Agriculture , Fisheries and Forestry ; No. 23, Performance audit—The Australian Government Reconstruction Inspectorate’s conduct of value for money reviews of flood reconstruction projects in Victoria—Department of Regional Australia , Local Government , Arts and Sport ; No. 24, Performance audit—The preparation and delivery of the natural disaster recovery work plans for Queensland and Victoria—Department of Regional Australia, Local Government, Arts and Sport ; No. 25, Performance audit—Defence’s implementation of audit recommendations— Department of Defence — Defence Materiel Organisation , and No. 26, Performance audit—Remediation of the lightweight torpedo replacement project—Department of Defence—Defence Materiel Organisation .

Ordered that the reports be made parliamentary papers.
