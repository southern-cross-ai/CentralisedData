Statements by Members

Keswick and Hampstead Barracks

1:56 pm

Kate Ellis (Adelaide, Australian Labor Party) | Hansard source

I would like to take this opportunity to raise my concerns with a Defence proposal to close a number of barracks in South Australia , including two in my electorateâ€”the Keswick and Hampstead barracks. This has caused a lot of concern within the community, particularly for Lieutenant Colonel Murray Alexander of the Defence Reserves Association and Jock Statton, the president of the South Australian RSL . There are some very real concerns about the impact any such closure would have on recruitment and retention, particularly amongst reservists. Today I call on the Minister for Defence to conduct a formal study consulting with all of these stakeholders before any decision is made so that we can all be clear about what the consequences of any such cost-cutting measure would be.
