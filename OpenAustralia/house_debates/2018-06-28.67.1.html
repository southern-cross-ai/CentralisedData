Thursday, 28 June 2018

Questions without Notice

Turnbull Government

2:08 pm

Chris Bowen (McMahon, Australian Labor Party, Shadow Treasurer) | Hansard source

My question is to the Prime Minister. Has the government reached any agreements with One Nation within the last 48 hours?

2:09 pm

Malcolm Turnbull (Wentworth, Liberal Party, Prime Minister) | Hansard source

I thank the honourable member and I congratulate him on his recent appointment as the shadow minister for small business. Of course, there are many ways to create a small business. Most people start off with no business at all and build it up. Others—and I think the shadow minister would fall into this category—start with a large business and turn it into a small one. That's exactly what he will do with Australia's economy and Australia's businesses—
