Perth NAIDOC Ball

1:57 pm

Alannah Mactiernan (Perth, Australian Labor Party) | Hansard source

Last Friday I was privileged to be invited to celebrate the life spirit of WA's Aboriginal community at the NAIDOC ball at the Perth convention centre. Over 1,100 frocked up to show their pride in their Aboriginal heritage and to celebrate their achievements and their determination to secure a place in the sun for their families. It was literally a brilliant finale to a successful NAIDOC Week .
