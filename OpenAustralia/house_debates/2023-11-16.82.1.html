Questions without Notice

Roads

2:26 pm

Llew O'Brien (Wide Bay, National Party) | Hansard source

My question is to the Minister for Infrastructure, Transport, Regional Development and Local Government . Data obtained from the Queensland government shows that almost half of the Bruce Highway is rated just two safety stars out of five. Can the minister guarantee that life-saving projects along the Bruce Highway, like the four-lane Tiaro Bypass, will proceed according to the same scope and timelines committed to in the government's October 2022 budget?
