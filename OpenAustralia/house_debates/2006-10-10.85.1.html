House debates

Tuesday, 10 October 2006

Notices

The following notices were given:

Mark Vaile (Lyne, National Party, Deputy Prime Minister) | Hansard source

to present a bill for an act to amend the Protection of the Sea (Prevention of Pollution from Ships) Act 1983, and for related purposes. (Maritime Legislation Amendment (Prevention of Pollution from Ships) Bill 2006)

Kevin Andrews (Menzies, Liberal Party, Minister Assisting the Prime Minister for the Public Service) | Hansard source

to present a bill for an act to amend the law relating to long service leave, and for related purposes. ( Long Service Leave (Commonwealth Employees) Amendment Bill 2006)

Gary Nairn (Eden-Monaro, Liberal Party, Special Minister of State) | Hansard source

to present a bill for an act to amend the Parliamentary Superannuation Act 2004, and for related purposes. (Parliamentary Superannuation Amendment Bill 2006)

Andrew Robb (Goldstein, Liberal Party, Parliamentary Secretary to the Minister for Immigration and Multicultural Affairs) | Hansard source

to present a bill for an act to amend the law relating to migration, and for related purposes. (Migration Amendment (Border Integrity) Bill 2006)
