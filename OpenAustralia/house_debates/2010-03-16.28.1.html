House debates

Tuesday, 16 March 2010

Personal Explanations

3:39 pm

Tony Abbott (Warringah, Liberal Party, Leader of the Opposition) | Hansard source

Mr Speaker, I wish to make a personal explanation.

Harry Jenkins (Speaker) | Hansard source

Does the honourable member claim to have been misrepresented?

Tony Abbott (Warringah, Liberal Party, Leader of the Opposition) | Hansard source

Yes, most grievously by a very un-prime-ministerial Prime Minister in question time today.

Harry Jenkins (Speaker) | Hansard source

Please proceed.

Tony Abbott (Warringah, Liberal Party, Leader of the Opposition) | Hansard source

In response to misrepresentations by the Prime Minister I simply refer him to page 179 of budget paper No. 2 of 2003-2004:

The funding for 2003-04 to 2007-08 represents an increase of $10 billion over the last agreements.

I seek leave to table this truth-telling budget paper.

Leave not granted.

3:40 pm

Christopher Pyne (Sturt, Liberal Party, Shadow Minister for Education, Apprenticeships and Training) | Hansard source

Mr Speaker, I wish to make a personal explanation.

Harry Jenkins (Speaker) | Hansard source

Does the honourable member claim to have been misrepresented?

Christopher Pyne (Sturt, Liberal Party, Shadow Minister for Education, Apprenticeships and Training) | Hansard source

I do.

Harry Jenkins (Speaker) | Hansard source

Please proceed.

Christopher Pyne (Sturt, Liberal Party, Shadow Minister for Education, Apprenticeships and Training) | Hansard source

In question time today the Minister for Education claimed that the opposition opposed the My School website. In an interview with Fran Kelly on 3 February 2010, in fact, I said;

… on its own, the My Schools website is a good idea, but it needs more.

I seek leave to table the transcript.

Leave not granted.
