Committees

Treaties Committee; Membership

3:14 pm

Tony Smith (Speaker) | Hansard source

I have received advice from the Chief Government Whip and the Chief Opposition Whip nominating members to be members of the Joint Standing Committee on Treaties.

by leave—I move:

That Mr Crewther, Ms M. M. H. King, Ms Marino, Mr O’Dowd, Mr Robert, Ms Templeman, Mr Vasta, Mr Wallace and Mr J . H. Wilson be appointed members of the Joint Standing Committee on Treaties.

Question agreed to.
