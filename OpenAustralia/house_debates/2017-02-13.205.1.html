Monday, 13 February 2017

Private Members' Business

Centrelink (Question No. 629)

Susan Templeman (Macquarie, Australian Labor Party) | Hansard source

asked the Minister for Human Services, in writing, on 24 November 2016

In (a) the electoral division of Macquarie, and (b) New South Wales , how many people who are 55 years of age and over are (i) registered with Centrelink, and (ii) eligible for Medicare.

Alan Tudge (Aston, Liberal Party, Minister for Human Services) | Hansard source

The answer to the honourable member's question is as follows:

Centrelink Customer over 55yrs in receipt of a main income support payment (1)

(1) Main Income Support payments ( ISP ) were utilised as a customer base to minimise the risk of potential over counting of customers.

(2) Numbers have been rounded.

Total Number of eligible Medicare Enrolment holders over 55yrs (3)

(3) Eligible Medicare enrolment includes customers who are enrolled and have a current status.
