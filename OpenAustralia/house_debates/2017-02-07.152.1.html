Griffith Electorate: Australia Day Awards

4:51 pm

Terri Butler (Griffith, Australian Labor Party) | Hansard source

I rise to advise that on the weekend over the break we held the ceremony for the 2017 Griffith Australia Day Awards, the 18th annual Griffith Australia Day Awards. They were instigated by my predecessor as the member for Griffith, Kevin Rudd . I want to express my thanks to Dr Bill Glasson, who honoured me by agreeing to serve as the chair of the Australia Day awards committee this year, and I also thank the other members of the committee: Penny McClelland, Terry Wahry, Desley Graham and Alex Skinner.

The recipients of the Griffith Australia Day Awards included: Lois Angus, who is in her 80s and has been a long-term volunteer at the Cannon Hill Community Sports Club and the Cannon Hill Ladies Bowling Club;
