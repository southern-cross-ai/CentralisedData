House debates

Wednesday, 4 June 2008

Personal Explanations

3:22 pm

Christopher Pyne (Sturt, Liberal Party, Shadow Minister Assisting the Shadow Minister for Immigration and Citizenship) | Hansard source

Mr Speaker, I wish to make a personal explanation.

Harry Jenkins (Speaker) | Hansard source

Does the honourable member claim to have been misrepresented?

Christopher Pyne (Sturt, Liberal Party, Shadow Minister Assisting the Shadow Minister for Immigration and Citizenship) | Hansard source

Yes.

Harry Jenkins (Speaker) | Hansard source

Please proceed.

Christopher Pyne (Sturt, Liberal Party, Shadow Minister Assisting the Shadow Minister for Immigration and Citizenship) | Hansard source

In question time the Minister for Housing and Minister for the Status of Women implied that members of the former government trivialised domestic violence. This is utterly untrue. It is an outrageous slur.

Harry Jenkins (Speaker) | Hansard source

Order! The member must show where he has been misrepresented. The member for Sturt will resume his seat.
