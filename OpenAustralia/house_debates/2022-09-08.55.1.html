Statements by Members

Chisholm Electorate: Sports

1:43 pm

Carina Garland (Chisholm, Australian Labor Party) | Hansard source

Today I have the great pleasure to highlight excellence in my wonderful Chisholm community. Two of our fantastic local football clubs celebrated grand finals wins across the weekend. I extend a warm congratulations to the Surrey Park Panthers under-14s and under-13 Blacks, and the Waverley Blues seniors and reserve teams, who all took home the premiership for their respective divisions. I've had the great pleasure of visiting both clubs across the last few months, so I can say with certainty and pride that they are incredible local sporting groups who enrich our community. They have endured the brunt of the pandemic and come back stronger, all with the support of their dedicated volunteers.

Congratulations once again to the Surrey Park Panthers and the Waverley Blues. Go, Panthers! Go, Blues!
