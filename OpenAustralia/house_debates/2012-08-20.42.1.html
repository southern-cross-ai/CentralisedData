Statements by Members

Youth Services

1:59 pm

Geoff Lyons (Bass, Australian Labor Party) | Hansard source

I recently attended Beacon events at schools in my electorate of Bass. Beacon is a not-for-profit organisation aimed at providing brighter and positive futures for our youth. The schools involved in these events in the great electorate of Bass were Brooks High School , the city campus of Kings Meadows High School , Queechy High School and Winnaleah District High School. These young adults make a commitment to further education, training or employment. I commend this program and look forward to this generation making a positive difference for themselves and their communities.

Ms Anna Burke (Chisholm, Deputy-Speaker) | Hansard source
