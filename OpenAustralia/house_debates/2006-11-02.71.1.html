Questions without Notice

Interest Rates

2:15 pm

Kim Beazley (Brand, Australian Labor Party, Leader of the Opposition) | Hansard source

My question is to the Prime Minister and it follows the first question I asked him. Does the Prime Minister accept any personal responsibility for any of the last seven back-to-back rate hikes?

John Howard (Bennelong, Liberal Party, Prime Minister) | Hansard source

As leader of the government, I accept responsibility for the aggregate state of the Australian economy—which is magnificent.
