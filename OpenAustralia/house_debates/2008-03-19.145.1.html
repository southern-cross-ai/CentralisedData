House debates

Notices

The following notices were given:

to present a bill for an act to amend the law relating to aviation, and for related purposes. (Civil Aviation Legislation Amendment (1999 Montreal Convention and Other Measures) Bill 2008)

to present a bill for an act to give effect to the International Convention on Civil Liability for Bunker Oil Pollution Damage, and for related purposes. (Protection of the Sea (Civil Liability for Bunker Oil Pollution Damage) Bill 2008)

to present a bill for an act to amend the Sydney Airport Demand Management Act 1997, and for related purposes. (Sydney Airport Demand Management Amendment Bill 2008)

Ms Anna Burke (Chisholm, Deputy-Speaker) | Hansard source
