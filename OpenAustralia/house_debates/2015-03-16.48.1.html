Nola Marino (Forrest, Liberal Party) | Hansard source

I also thank the employers who encouraged their workers to be part of the emergency volunteer groups and the families who support ed them. These services are the difference between life and death in rural and regional areas. Firefighters protect our property and frequently put their own lives at risk, making personal sacrifices to do so. As a nation, we are eternally grateful to this outstanding group of volunteers.
