Statements by Members

Renewable Energy

4:35 pm

Barnaby Joyce (New England, National Party, Shadow Minister for Veterans' Affairs) | Hansard source

I rise today to talk about the campaign that is against people losing their property rights by reason of transmission lines and having their environment destroyed by reason of wind factories and solar factories, against having a power price that is going through the roof while reliability is going through the floor and against having overseas companies so the money goes overseas. Basically, we understand the swindle and how we're getting done over. We've now set up a national group. We've got people from Clarke Creek . We've got people from Tasmania, from Victoria and from New South Wales . I'd like to commend the people who have put their hands up to be part of the executive of that group: Grant Piper from Coolah, Hamilton Gerrand from Gippsland and Katie McCallum from Kilkivan in Queensland.

This goes to show you that a spread of people across our nation is now forming together. They're getting coordinated and they're getting the capacity to have real political effect.
