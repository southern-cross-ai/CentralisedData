Statements by Members

Aston Electorate: Health Care

1:59 pm

Alan Tudge (Aston, Liberal Party) | Hansard source

I have previously spoken in this House about the government's $107 million cuts to Victorian hospitals which are having a direct impact on my electorate. Five palliative-care beds from Wantirna Health have had to close as a result of Prime Minister Gillard's decision to cut health funds in my electorate. I have since discovered that the emergency department at the Angliss Hospital in the electorate of La Trobe , next door to mine, may also have to reduce its hours. They have already cut five surgical beds from that hospital. At the same time that they are cutting $107 million from hospitals they are investing $110 million in carbon tax promotion. Where are their priorities?
