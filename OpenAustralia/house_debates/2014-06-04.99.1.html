House debates

Wednesday, 4 June 2014

Personal Explanations

4:14 pm

Andrew Leigh (Fraser, Australian Labor Party, Shadow Assistant Treasurer) | Hansard source

Mr Deputy Speaker, I wish to make a personal explanation.

Russell Broadbent (McMillan, Liberal Party) | Hansard source

Does the honourable member claim to have been misrepresented?

Andrew Leigh (Fraser, Australian Labor Party, Shadow Assistant Treasurer) | Hansard source

I do, most grievously.

Russell Broadbent (McMillan, Liberal Party) | Hansard source

Please proceed.

Andrew Leigh (Fraser, Australian Labor Party, Shadow Assistant Treasurer) | Hansard source

In question time today the Minister for Health quoted from a newspaper article I wrote over a decade ago while at university and suggested that I now support a GP co-payment. This is incorrect. I encourage the health minister to do as I have done and speak with health experts, who overwhelmingly oppose the tax and will persuade him against it. Rather than talking about what I wrote while at university the government might start talking about what it said in last year's election.
