House debates

Tuesday, 12 March 2013

Documents

Presentation

3:15 pm

Anthony Albanese (Grayndler, Australian Labor Party, Leader of the House) | Hansard source

():

Documents are presented as listed in the schedule circulated to honourable members. Details of the documents will be recorded in the Votes and Proceedings and I move:

That the House take note of the following documents:

Australian Competition and Consumer Commission —Reports for 2011-12—Report 1: Telecommunications competitive safeguards; Report 2: Changes in the prices paid for telecommunications services in Australia.

Australian Meat and Live-stock Industry Act 1997—

Live-stock mortalities for exports by sea—Report for the period 1 July to 31 December 2012.

Report on the funding agreement between the Commonwealth of Australia and Australian Livestock Export Corporation Limited for 2011-12.

Dairy Produce Act 1986 Statutory funding agreement with Dairy Australia Limited—Report for 2011-12.

Interactive Gambling Act 2001 Prohibition on interactive gambling advertisements—Report for 2012.

Law Enforcement —Joint Statutory Committee—Inquiry into Commonwealth unexplained wealth legislation and arrangements—Government response.

Local Government ( Financial Assistance ) Act 1995 Report on the operation of the Act for 2009-10.

Regional Australia —House of Representatives Standing Committee—Report into certain matters relating to the proposed Murray-Darling Basin Plan—Status of Government response.

Treaties—

Bilateral

Text, together with national interest analysis Agreement between the Government of Australia and the Government of the United Arab Emirates on cooperation in the peaceful uses of nuclear energy ( Abu Dhabi , 31 July 2012).

Text, together with national interest analysis and annexure

Agreement between the Government of Australia and the Government of the Kingdom of Belgium relating to air services (Canberra, 23 November 2012).

Agreement between the Government of Australia and the Government of the Solomon Islands relating to air services (Canberra, 21 August 2012).

Text, together with national interest analysis and annexures Agreement between the Government of Australia and the Government of the Republic of the Philippines relating to air services (Canberra, 24 October 2012).

Multilateral Text, together with national interest analysis

Agreement on the establishment of the Global Green Growth Institute (Rio de Janeiro, 20 June 2012).

International Labour Organization Convention No. 129: Convention concerning labour inspection in agriculture (Geneva, 25 June 1969).

International Labour Organization Convention No. 138: Convention concerning minimum age for admission to employment (Geneva, 26 June 1973).

Debate adjourned.
