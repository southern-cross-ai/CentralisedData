Thursday, 14 September 2023

Questions without Notice

Aviation Industry

2:39 pm

David Littleproud (Maranoa, National Party, Shadow Minister for Agriculture) | Hansard source

My question is to the Minister for Infrastructure, Transport, Regional Development and Local Government . I refer to a discussion the minister had with Qantas executive Vanessa Hudson regarding the Qatar application for extra flights, prior to the minister's decision being made. What was the content of that discussion?

Ms Catherine King (Ballarat, Australian Labor Party, Minister for Infrastructure, Transport, Regional Development and Local Government) | Hansard source

I haven't had any discussions with Vanessa Hudson about the Qatar decision at all.
