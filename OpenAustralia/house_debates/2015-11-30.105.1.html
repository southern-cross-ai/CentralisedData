Questions without Notice

Special Minister of State

3:06 pm

Mark Dreyfus (Isaacs, Australian Labor Party, Shadow Attorney General) | Hansard source

My question is, again, to the Special Minister of State . I refer to the minister's answers in question time last week and today. On 29 March 2012 James Ashby sent a text message to the minister which included pages of the Speaker's official diary. The minister sent a text back which said, 'Can that be emailed James it is hard to read Mal.brough2@bigpond.com' The initial copy he received may have been blurry, but isn't it crystal clear the minister should resign?
