Questions without Notice

Carbon Pricing

Louise Markus (Macquarie, Liberal Party) | Hansard source

My question is to the Treasurer. Will the Treasurer confirm that under Professor Garnaut's proposal a typical single-income family with two or three children and one partner earning $80,000 per annum will get no compensation for the government's carbon tax?
