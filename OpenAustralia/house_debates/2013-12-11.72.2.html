Wednesday, 11 December 2013

Distinguished Visitors

2:40 pm

Mrs Bronwyn Bishop (Speaker) | Hansard source

I advise the House that we have in the gallery the former member for Barker, Mr Secker; the former Western Australian senator, Ross Lightfoot ; and a ministerial delegation of the government of Papua New Guinea , whom we particularly welcome. We also have present the former Victorian senator, Kay Patterson , and we have the APEC 7th Delegation from the Philippines. All of those people are made very welcome here today.

Honourable members: Hear, hear!
