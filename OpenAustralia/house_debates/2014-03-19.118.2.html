House debates

Wednesday, 19 March 2014

Documents

Presentation

3:21 pm

Christopher Pyne (Sturt, Liberal Party, Minister for Education) | Hansard source

Documents are tabled in accordance with the list circulated to honourable members earlier today. Full details of the documents—

Ms Plibersek interjecting —

Mrs Bronwyn Bishop (Speaker) | Hansard source

And you will leave the chamber under 94(a).

Christopher Pyne (Sturt, Liberal Party, Minister for Education) | Hansard source

You will be named if you do not be careful!

Mrs Bronwyn Bishop (Speaker) | Hansard source

Indeed.

The member for Sydney then left the chamber.

Mr Pyne interjecting —

Dr Chalmers interjecting —

The Leader of the House will desist, and the member for Rankin will leave under 94(a).

The member for Rankin then left the chamber .

Mr Tony Burke (Watson, Australian Labor Party, Shadow Minister for Finance) | Hansard source

Madam Speaker, I believe it is a serious reflection on the chair for the Leader of the House to be threatening who will be named. He should withdraw that comment.

Mrs Bronwyn Bishop (Speaker) | Hansard source

I can assure the Manager of Opposition Business that the only person who will make a decision about whether someone is to be named is me.

Christopher Pyne (Sturt, Liberal Party, Minister for Education) | Hansard source

If it will assist the House, Madam Speaker, I am happy to withdraw.

Mrs Bronwyn Bishop (Speaker) | Hansard source

Thank you.
