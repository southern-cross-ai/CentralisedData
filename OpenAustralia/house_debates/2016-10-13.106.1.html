House debates

Thursday, 13 October 2016

Personal Explanations

3:12 pm

Andrew Leigh (Fenner, Australian Labor Party, Shadow Assistant Treasurer) | Hansard source

Mr Speaker, I seek your permission to make a personal explanation.

Tony Smith (Speaker) | Hansard source

Does the member for Fenner claim to have been misrepresented?

Andrew Leigh (Fenner, Australian Labor Party, Shadow Assistant Treasurer) | Hansard source

I do, most grievously. In an answer to an earlier question, the Minister for Revenue and Financial Services said that I had voted against the government's multinational tax law. This is not correct. Neither I nor any of my House of Representatives colleagues voted against the government's multinational tax law. Given the events of the last 24 hours, I am surprised the minister did not check the Hansard.

Tony Smith (Speaker) | Hansard source

The member for Fenner will resume his seat.
