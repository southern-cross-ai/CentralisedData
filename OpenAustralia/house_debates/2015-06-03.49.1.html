Statements by Members

Petrie Electorate: Sport

1:59 pm

Luke Howarth (Petrie, Liberal Party) | Hansard source

I rise to quickly talk about a great sporting club in my electorate, the Redcliffe Dolphins rugby league club. They employ hundreds of local people and provide a fantastic rugby league team to the Queensland cup and they have 800 juniors in their junior program.

Mrs Bronwyn Bishop (Speaker) | Hansard source
