Adjournment

Adelaide: Traffic Congestion

10:29 pm

Andrew Southcott (Boothby, Liberal Party, Shadow Parliamentary Secretary for Primary Healthcare) | Hansard source

In the remaining time, I would like to speak on one of my favourite topics, which is traffic congestion on South Road . Three weeks ago residents of Adelaide woke up to the announcement splashed on the front page of the Advertiser that the state government had come up with a new South Road plan. Now, less than three weeks after that was released, we see that the numbers do not stack up. Only $20 million has been put by the federal government towards that, with $5 million from the state government, and nothing will really get going until 2017. This is a fraud on the South Australian public.

Ms Anna Burke (Speaker) | Hansard source

Order! It being 10:30 pm the debate is interrupted.
