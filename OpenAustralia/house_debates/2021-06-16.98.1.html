Questions without Notice

COVID-19: Vaccination

2:39 pm

Mark Butler (Hindmarsh, Australian Labor Party, Deputy Manager of Opposition Business in the House of Representatives) | Hansard source

Michael McCormack (Riverina, National Party, Leader of the Nationals) | Hansard source

I'll ask the Minister for Health to answer that question.
