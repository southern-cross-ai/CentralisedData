Darling Range RSL: Memorial Day Service

6:53 pm

Sharryn Jackson (Hasluck, Australian Labor Party) | Hansard source

I would like to thank the Darling Range RSL for their Remembrance Day service at the Darling Range War Memorial on Wednesday last weekâ€”and especially for the cuppa at the morning tea served afterwards.
