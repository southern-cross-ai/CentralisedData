House debates

Monday, 15 February 2021

Questions without Notice

Workplace Relations

2:26 pm

Mr Tony Burke (Watson, Australian Labor Party, Shadow Minister for the Arts) | Hansard source

My question is to the Minister for Industrial Relations . The minister says that paying at least the minimum wage to every worker is complicated. What exactly is complicated about workers being paid at least the minimum wage?

2:27 pm

Christian Porter (Pearce, Liberal Party, Attorney-General) | Hansard source

Minimum wage applies. There should never be any excuse and it's unlawful to not pay the minimum wage. That is a very well-known principle. If the member is asking questions about people to whom the minimum wage doesn't apply, I'm sure he would be able to clarify that in a further question.
