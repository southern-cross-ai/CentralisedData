House debates

Wednesday, 14 August 2024

Questions without Notice

Visitor Visas

3:03 pm

Keith Wolahan (Menzies, Liberal Party) | Hansard source

My question is to the Minister for Immigration. Minister, since 7 October last year, has any individual from the Gaza war zone applied for a visa and been rejected on the basis of their affiliation with, or membership of, the listed terrorist organisation, Hamas?

3:04 pm

Mr Tony Burke (Watson, Australian Labor Party, Minister for Employment and Workplace Relations) | Hansard source

The statistics on visas applied for and rejected are as follows. From 7 October to 12 August, which are the statistics I have available, 2,922 visas were granted; 7,111 were rejected.
