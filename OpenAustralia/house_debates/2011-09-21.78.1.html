Wednesday, 21 September 2011

Distinguished Visitors

Harry Jenkins (Speaker) | Hansard source

Before calling the member for North Sydney —by way of omission—earlier in the day we had the Lord Mayor of Brisbane in the gallery, Councillor Graham Quirk. He was here with a delegation of mayors from South-East Queensland. If he had been in the chamber I would have given him a warm welcome and I apologise to those who pointed out to me that he was in the gallery.
