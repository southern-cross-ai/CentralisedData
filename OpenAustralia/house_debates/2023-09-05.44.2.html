Distinguished Visitors

Work Exposure in Government Program, NPY Emerging Leaders Program, City of Greater Dandenong Council

2:23 pm

Milton Dick (Speaker) | Hansard source

I'm pleased to inform the House that present in the gallery today are a group of students participating in the Work Exposure in Government program, the WEX program. On behalf of the House, welcome to question time. I look forward to meeting you all after question time.

Also present in the gallery are a group of young NPY emerging leaders from the NPY region in the Northern Territory , in the member for Lingiari's electorate. A big welcome to you.

I also welcome Councillor Jim Memeti and Councillor Richard Lim OAM from the City of Greater Dandenong in the member for Holt's electorate. Welcome.

Honourable members: Hear, hear!
