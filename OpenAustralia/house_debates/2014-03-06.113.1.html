House debates

Thursday, 6 March 2014

Questions without Notice

Commission of Audit Report

3:01 pm

Chris Bowen (McMahon, Australian Labor Party, Shadow Treasurer) | Hansard source

My question is to the Treasurer. Given the Treasurer has had the Commission of Audit's interim report for weeks, when will the Treasurer release the report to the public? Will the Treasurer release it before the Western Australian Senate election on 5 April?

Joe Hockey (North Sydney, Liberal Party, Treasurer) | Hansard source

Thank you very much. We have received the interim report. We have not received the final report.

Opposition Members:

Opposition members interjecting —

Joe Hockey (North Sydney, Liberal Party, Treasurer) | Hansard source

Oh, you have shot me in the heart! What a harrowing hit from Labor.

Mrs Bronwyn Bishop (Speaker) | Hansard source

Order! I think we will have a little decorum returned.

Joe Hockey (North Sydney, Liberal Party, Treasurer) | Hansard source

We have had it now for about three weeks—that is about right. The Labor Party when they were in government held Gonski for 60 days—most of it against his will—and they held the Henry report for 130 days and then did not even implement it, apart from the mining tax. So I would say, please, we go through these things in a careful, methodical manner. We consider these things very carefully and, quite frankly, if the Labor Party paid the same attention to detail that we did, perhaps they would still be in government.
