Consideration of Legislation

5:42 pm

Greg Hunt (Flinders, Liberal Party, Minister for the Environment) | Hansard source

I move:

That the resolution agreed to on 24 June 2014 relating to the consideration of the Clean Energy Legislation (Carbon Tax Repeal) Bill 2013 (No. 2) and 10 related bills be varied to exclude the Clean Energy Finance Corporation (Abolition) Bill 2014 and to make the further consideration of that bill an order of the day for the next sitting.
