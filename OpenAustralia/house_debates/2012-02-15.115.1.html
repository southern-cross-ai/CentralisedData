Committees

Selection Committee; Report

7:00 pm

Peter Slipper (Speaker) | Hansard source

I present the report of the Selection Committee No.43 relating to consideration of committee and delegation business on Monday, 27 February 2012. The report will be printed in today's Hansard and the determinations of the committee will appear on tomorrow's Notice Paper . Copies of the report have been placed on the table.
