Tuesday, 10 May 2011

Questions in Writing

Regional Development Australia Fund (Question No. 299)

Bob Baldwin (Paterson, Liberal Party, Shadow Minister for Tourism) | Hansard source

asked the Minister for Regional Australia , Regional Development and Local Government , in writing, on 21 March 2011:

In respect of the Regional Development Australia Fund and the Australian Standard

Geographical Classification—Remoteness Areas ( RA ), will there be a limit by (a) number, (b) dollar value, or (c) share of available funding, to the projects funded in (i) non-capital city RA1, (ii) capital city RA1, (iii) RA2 , (iv) RA3 , (v) RA4, and (vi)RA5.

Simon Crean (Hotham, Australian Labor Party, Minister for Regional Australia, Regional Development and Local Government) | Hansard source

The answer to the honourable member’s question is as follows:

Projects to be funded will be selected in accordance with the assessment process, eligibility criteria and selection criteria set out in the Regional Development Australia Fund Guidelines.
