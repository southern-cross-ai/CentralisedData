Statements by Members

Parliament House: Christmas Carols

2:00 pm

Christopher Pyne (Sturt, Liberal Party, Leader of the House) | Hansard source

Tonight in the Members Hall, the Canberra Choral Society will be singing Christmas carols for the benefit of the members, staff and others who inhabit the building, including Laura Tingle. I am sure we would much prefer to hear Laura Tingle singing than her husband, Alan Ramsey , who probably does not have nearly as nice a voice!

Tony Smith (Speaker) | Hansard source
