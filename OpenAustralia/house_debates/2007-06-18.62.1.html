House debates

Monday, 18 June 2007

Questions to the Speaker

Parliamentary Behaviour

3:25 pm

Anthony Albanese (Grayndler, Australian Labor Party, Manager of Opposition Business in the House) | Hansard source

I have a question to you, Mr Speaker. My question to you is: how many members from the opposition side of the House were ejected from parliament today and how many were warned? On the other side, how many were ejected and how many were warned?

Tony Abbott (Warringah, Liberal Party, Leader of the House) | Hansard source

Mr Speaker, on a point of order: that question is plainly a reflection on the chair by a member who regularly reflects on the chair, and this member should be dealt with.

Anthony Albanese (Grayndler, Australian Labor Party, Manager of Opposition Business in the House) | Hansard source

Mr Albanese interjecting

David Hawker (Speaker) | Hansard source

The member for Grayndler has been warned. If he wants me to answer his question before he adds his name to that list, he might listen. I say to the member for Grayndler: the answer is quite simple. He should refer to the Hansard.

3:26 pm
