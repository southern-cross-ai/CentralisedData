Tuesday, 17 October 2017

Personal Explanations

3:12 pm

Ann Sudmalis (Gilmore, Liberal Party) | Hansard source

I wish to make a personal explanation.

Tony Smith (Speaker) | Hansard source

Does the member for Gilmore claim to have been misrepresented?

Ann Sudmalis (Gilmore, Liberal Party) | Hansard source

Yes.

Tony Smith (Speaker) | Hansard source

The member for Gilmore may proceed.

Ann Sudmalis (Gilmore, Liberal Party) | Hansard source

Yesterday in the House the member for Whitlam—whom I cannot locate at this moment—spoke about job losses in HMAS Albatross and said that he had reliable documentation to refute my statement that what he was saying was completely untrue. I have confidential material directly from the base that refutes exactly what the member for Whitlam said, and he should apologise publicly.

Mr Stephen Jones interjecting —

Tony Smith (Speaker) | Hansard source

I thank the member for Gilmore. The member for Whitlam will cease interjecting .

Mrs Sudmalis interjecting —

The member for Gilmore has made her personal explanation. She won't interject.

Mrs Sudmalis interjecting —

The member for Gilmore will cease interjecting !
