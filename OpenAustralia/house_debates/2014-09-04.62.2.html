House debates

Thursday, 4 September 2014

Ministerial Arrangements

1:59 pm

Mrs Bronwyn Bishop (Speaker) | Hansard source

I call the honourable the Acting Prime Minister .

2:00 pm

Warren Truss (Wide Bay, National Party, Leader of the Nationals) | Hansard source

Thank you, Madam Speaker—

An opposition member: Where are they?

An opposition member: They've all gone on leave.

If you wait a moment, you will hear.

Mrs Bronwyn Bishop (Speaker) | Hansard source

There will be silence on my left!

Warren Truss (Wide Bay, National Party, Leader of the Nationals) | Hansard source

I inform the House that the Prime Minister and the Minister for Trade and Investment will be absent from question time today while they conduct a series of trade and security related meetings in India. I will answer questions on their behalf.

Opposition members interjecting —

Mrs Bronwyn Bishop (Speaker) | Hansard source

There will be silence! I cannot hear what the Acting Prime Minister is saying.

Warren Truss (Wide Bay, National Party, Leader of the Nationals) | Hansard source

Opposition members interjecting —

Mrs Bronwyn Bishop (Speaker) | Hansard source

Acting Prime Minister —

Warren Truss (Wide Bay, National Party, Leader of the Nationals) | Hansard source

The Treasurer will answer questions—

Mrs Bronwyn Bishop (Speaker) | Hansard source

Acting Prime Minister , would you—

Warren Truss (Wide Bay, National Party, Leader of the Nationals) | Hansard source

on behalf of the Minister for Small Business —

An opposition member: He can't hear either. Don't stop him when he's on a roll!

Mrs Bronwyn Bishop (Speaker) | Hansard source

Warren Truss (Wide Bay, National Party, Leader of the Nationals) | Hansard source
