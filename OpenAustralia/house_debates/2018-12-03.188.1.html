Canberra Electorate: Local Sporting Champions

4:16 pm

Gai Brodtmann (Canberra, Australian Labor Party, Shadow Assistant Minister for Cyber Security and Defence) | Hansard source

Canberra lays claim to producing some of the world's greatest sporting stars. This year I have had the honour of presenting 63 awards to our local sporting champions. I know we will be seeing many of these names up in the bright lights on the world stage before too long.
