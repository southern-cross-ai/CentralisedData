4:05 pm

Jason Wood (La Trobe, Liberal Party) | Hansard source

It's with great pleasure that Minister Alan Tudge and I visited the Buddhist Vihara in Berwick, otherwise known as the Sri Lankan temple, where we made a $500,000 grant announcement for the Multicultural Education Resource Centre. First of all, can I say it was absolutely amazing to be greeted by all the young girls dancing and being so proud of Sri Lankan culture.
