House debates

Wednesday, 29 November 2006

Questions to the Speaker

Department of Parliamentary Services: Cabcharge

3:51 pm

Kelly Hoare (Charlton, Australian Labor Party) | Hansard source

Mr Speaker, I have a question to you. Can you please confirm to the House that the Department of Parliamentary Services has recently determined that Hansard staff finishing work late at night can no longer access a cabcharge to get home, as has been past practice? Can you explain why this decision has been taken and whether the department owes Hansard staff a duty of care to ensure their safe return home after working late into the night?

David Hawker (Speaker) | Hansard source

I thank the member for Charlton for her question. I had hoped to be in a position to answer it for her now. I will get an answer to her as soon as I can.
