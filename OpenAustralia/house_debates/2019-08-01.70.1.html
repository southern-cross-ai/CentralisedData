House debates

Thursday, 1 August 2019

Questions without Notice

Minister for Energy and Emissions Reduction

2:50 pm

Mark Butler (Hindmarsh, Australian Labor Party, Shadow Minister for Climate Change and Energy) | Hansard source

My question is to the Minister for Energy and Emissions Reduction. I refer to the minister's previous answers and his statement that he has disclosed interests in accordance with the rules. Can the minister confirm that he has never disclosed his interest in an entity called JRAT to the parliament?

2:51 pm

Angus Taylor (Hume, Liberal Party, Minister for Energy and Emissions Reduction) | Hansard source

All my interests are declared in accordance with the rules.

Tony Smith (Speaker) | Hansard source

The member for Hindmarsh is seeking to table a document?

Mark Butler (Hindmarsh, Australian Labor Party, Shadow Minister for Climate Change and Energy) | Hansard source

Thank you, Mr Speaker. I seek leave to table a media report in which the minister confirms that he has not disclosed that and should have.

Tony Smith (Speaker) | Hansard source

The member for Hindmarsh will resume his seat. I've made it very clear that I'm not even going to ask the Leader of the House for leave on publications that are widely available.
