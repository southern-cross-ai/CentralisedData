House debates

Thursday, 15 June 2006

Questions to the Speaker

House of Representatives Resolution

3:34 pm

Michael Hatton (Blaxland, Australian Labor Party) | Hansard source

Mr Speaker, I seek to assist you in your determination in relation to the argument put previously by the member for Aston. I simply want to point out that I was enforcing the gag motion that had been moved on that bill. I had previously indicated that there was an absolute five-minute limit for the parliamentary secretary to speak. He continued to speak, and I indicated that I was gagging him, as all other members had been gagged by that motion. Even if, Mr Speaker, you sought to agree with him that that should be scrubbed out of the record—

Mrs Bronwyn Bishop (Mackellar, Liberal Party) | Hansard source

Mr Speaker, I rise to take a point of—

David Hawker (Speaker) | Hansard source

Order! I am listening to the member for Blaxland.

Michael Hatton (Blaxland, Australian Labor Party) | Hansard source

I will finish on this before the member for Mackellar—

David Hawker (Speaker) | Hansard source

The member for Mackellar will resume her seat. I am listening to the member for Blaxland.

Michael Hatton (Blaxland, Australian Labor Party) | Hansard source

Even if you did agree with him, and seek to scrub from the record what I said, his restatement of it in the House would involve a serial scrubbing out, which would reinforce the stupidity of what he did.

David Hawker (Speaker) | Hansard source

I thank the member for Blaxland. As I said to the member for Aston, I will investigate the points raised. I thank the member for Blaxland for his explanation.
