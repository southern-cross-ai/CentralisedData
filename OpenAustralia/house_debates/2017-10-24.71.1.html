Questions without Notice

Law Enforcement

3:20 pm

Michael Keenan (Stirling, Liberal Party, Minister for Justice) | Hansard source

What would be preferable is if they looked at their position, realised that the status quo is not acceptable and supported the reform that we are making to make sure that child sex offenders spend time in prison.

Malcolm Turnbull (Wentworth, Liberal Party, Prime Minister) | Hansard source
