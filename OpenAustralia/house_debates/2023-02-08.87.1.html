House debates

Wednesday, 8 February 2023

Questions without Notice

Donations to Political Parties

3:01 pm

Paul Fletcher (Bradfield, Liberal Party, Shadow Minister for Government Services and the Digital Economy) | Hansard source

My question is to the Prime Minister. The Prime Minister expelled CFMMEU boss John Setka from the Labor Party after Mr Setka was convicted for harassing his wife, saying, 'His values aren't the same as Labor's values.' Yet, following a period where the CFMMEU has continued with its notorious violence, thuggery and intimidation towards women, Labor has accepted a $4.3 million donation from the CFMMEU at this election. Prime Minister, what values does this Labor government share with the CFMMEU?

Sussan Ley (Farrer, Liberal Party, Shadow Minister for Women) | Hansard source

You should give that money back.

Milton Dick (Speaker) | Hansard source

The Deputy Leader of the Opposition. The Prime Minister will answer the question at the end.

Anthony Albanese (Grayndler, Australian Labor Party, Prime Minister) | Hansard source

I did take action to ensure that John Setka was expelled from the Labor Party. That's called leadership.
