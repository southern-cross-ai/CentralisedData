House debates

Tuesday, 22 May 2007

Personal Explanations

3:22 pm

Sharon Bird (Cunningham, Australian Labor Party) | Hansard source

Mr Speaker, I wish to make a personal explanation.

David Hawker (Speaker) | Hansard source

Does the honourable member claim to have been misrepresented?

Sharon Bird (Cunningham, Australian Labor Party) | Hansard source

Twice, by the Treasurer.

David Hawker (Speaker) | Hansard source

Please proceed.

Sharon Bird (Cunningham, Australian Labor Party) | Hansard source

Firstly, I indicate that I am a very proud member of the Transport Workers Union of Australia and have been a member of the CPSU , the New South Wales Teachers Federation and whichever union covered my workplace at any point in time. Secondly, the Treasurer indicated that I was the member for Hunter. I am the member for Cunningham, and, if he had answered my question from two years ago asking whether he had ever visited my electorate, he might well know that.
