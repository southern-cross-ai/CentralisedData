House debates

Thursday, 20 September 2012

Committees

Publications Committee; Report

3:23 pm

Chris Hayes (Fowler, Australian Labor Party) | Hansard source

I present the report from the Publications Committee sitting in conference with the Publications Committee of the Senate. Copies of the report are being placed on the table. I seek leave to move that the report be agreed to.

Ms Anna Burke (Chisholm, Deputy-Speaker) | Hansard source

Is leave granted?

Christopher Pyne (Sturt, Liberal Party, Shadow Minister for Education, Apprenticeships and Training) | Hansard source

He did not seek leave. He said he was tabling a report.

Ms Anna Burke (Chisholm, Deputy-Speaker) | Hansard source

He asked for leave at the end.

Christopher Pyne (Sturt, Liberal Party, Shadow Minister for Education, Apprenticeships and Training) | Hansard source

Is he going to give a speech to it? This has not even been raised with us.

Ms Anna Burke (Chisholm, Deputy-Speaker) | Hansard source

It is on the notice. Leave has been granted. The member for Fowler has the call.

Christopher Pyne (Sturt, Liberal Party, Shadow Minister for Education, Apprenticeships and Training) | Hansard source

Are you giving a speech?

Ms Anna Burke (Chisholm, Deputy-Speaker) | Hansard source

Order! That is not for you to determine. The member for Fowler has the call.

Chris Hayes (Fowler, Australian Labor Party) | Hansard source

I move:

That the report be agreed to.

Question agreed to.
