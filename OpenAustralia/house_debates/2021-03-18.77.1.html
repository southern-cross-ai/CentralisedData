Thursday, 18 March 2021

Questions without Notice

Members of Parliament: Staff

2:24 pm

Ms Catherine King (Ballarat, Australian Labor Party, Shadow Minister for Infrastructure, Transport and Regional Development) | Hansard source

My question is again to the Prime Minister. On Monday, Brittany Higgins stood at the front of this parliament and said:

I watched as the Prime Minister of Australia publicly apologised to me through the media, while privately his media team actively undermined and discredited my loved ones.

How can the Prime Minister hear a serious allegation from Brittany Higgins about appalling actions by his own staff and refuse to even check if it is true?

Scott Morrison (Cook, Liberal Party, Prime Minister) | Hansard source

I've already addressed this matter. I refer the member to my earlier answers.
