House debates

Thursday, 29 March 2007

Committees

Members’ Interests Committee; Report

11:09 am

Steven Ciobo (Moncrieff, Liberal Party) | Hansard source

In accordance with standing order 220, on behalf of the Committee of Members’ Interests, I present the report on the operations of the committee for 2006, together with the minutes of proceedings.

Ordered that the report be made a parliamentary paper.

by leave— Can I put on the record my sincere thanks to the member for Scullin, as deputy chair of the committee, as well as to other members of the committee. I and on behalf of the committee sincerely thank the registrar, Mr Bernard Wright, and assistant to the committee, Mrs Laraine Brennan. I thank them for the good work they have done throughout the past year; it has been a pleasure working with them. As required by resolutions of the House I present copies of notifications of alterations of interests received during the period 8 December 2006 to 28 March 2007.
