House debates

Tuesday, 16 August 2011

Questions without Notice

Member for Dobell

3:26 pm

Mrs Bronwyn Bishop (Mackellar, Liberal Party, Shadow Minister for Seniors) | Hansard source

My question is to the Prime Minister. I refer the Prime Minister to her statement that she retains complete confidence in the member for Dobell but that she had not undertaken a thorough investigation into the allegations surrounding that member. Has she now conducted an investigation of her own into the allegations surrounding the member for Dobell and is she satisfied that her confidence in the member for Dobell is warranted?

3:27 pm

Julia Gillard (Lalor, Australian Labor Party, Prime Minister) | Hansard source

I thank the member for the question. It gives me the opportunity to say I have complete confidence in the member for Dobell. I think he is doing a fine job representing the people of his constituency in this place and raising their concerns in this parliament, as is appropriate for a local member. I look forward to him continuing to do that job for a very long, long, long time to come.
