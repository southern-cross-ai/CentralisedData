Tuesday, 11 March 2008

Therapeutic Goods Amendment (Poisons Standard) Bill 2008

Report from Main Committee

Bill returned from Main Committee with amendments; certified copy of the bill and schedule of amendments presented.

Ordered that this bill be considered immediately.

Main Committee amendments—

(1)
Schedule 1, item 3, page 4 (line 9), omit “that Division”, substitute “that Act”.
(2)
Schedule 1, item 3, page 4 (line 22), omit “that Division”, substitute  “that Act”.

Harry Jenkins (Speaker) | Hansard source

The question is that the amendments be agreed to.

Question agreed to.

Bill, as amended, agreed to.
