Morton Electorate: Harmony Week

4:14 pm

Graham Perrett (Moreton, Australian Labor Party) | Hansard source

I rise to mention the wonderful ceremony I participated in on Saturday, a citizenship ceremony, as part of Harmony Week celebrations. It took place at the Centre for Interfaith & Cultural Dialogue, at Griffith University , where we were hosted by Dr Brian Adams. It was a wonderful ceremony with people from over 20 countries and 60-plus new Australians. Definitely, the best part of my job as a politician is to welcome people to Australia, to confirm that commitment to a strong multicultural Australia and the zest and economic opportunity that also comes from new Australians.

It was ably hosted by the MC , Lewis Lee OAM , who was conducting his 30th citizenship ceremony.
