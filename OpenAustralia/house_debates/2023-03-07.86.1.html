Statements

Personal Explanations

3:21 pm

Russell Broadbent (Monash, Liberal Party) | Hansard source

I wish to make a personal explanation.

Milton Dick (Speaker) | Hansard source

Do you claim to have been misrepresented?

Russell Broadbent (Monash, Liberal Party) | Hansard source

Most grievously.

Milton Dick (Speaker) | Hansard source

You may proceed.

Russell Broadbent (Monash, Liberal Party) | Hansard source

During question time, the Prime Minister said that I supported the government's policy on superannuation. That is an inaccurate statement to the House. I did not support their policy; in fact, I have a number of changes I'd like to make to it. More importantly, members of parliament are disliked for representing issues around an individual that are not correct. I was very interested to say that, as a country, we should be having a discussion about issues. That was my point. Thank you.
