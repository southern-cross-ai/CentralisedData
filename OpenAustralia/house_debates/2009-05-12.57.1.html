Tuesday, 12 May 2009

Auditor-General’S Reports

Reports Nos 27, 28, 29, 30 and 31 of 2008-09

3:56 pm

Harry Jenkins (Speaker) | Hansard source

—I present the Auditor-General’s Audit reports Nos 27, 28, 29, 30 and 31 of 2008-09 entitled No. 27—Performance audit: management of the M113 armoured personnel carrier upgrade project— Department of Defence ; No. 28—Performance audit: quality and integrity of the Department of Veterans’ Affairs income support records—Department of Veterans’ Affairs ; No. 29—Performance audit: delivery of projects on the AusLink National Network— Department of Infrastructure , Transport, Regional Development and Local Government ; No. 30—Performance audit: management of the Australian government’s action plan to eradicate trafficking in persons—Attorney-General’s Department; Department of Immigration and Citizenship ; Australian Federal Police ; Department of Families, Housing, Community Services and Indigenous Affairs and No. 31—Performance audit: Army Reserve forces—Department of Defence .

Ordered that the reports be made parliamentary papers.
