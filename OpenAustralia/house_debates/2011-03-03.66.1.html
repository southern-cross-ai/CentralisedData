Statements by Members

Braddon Electorate: Penguin Sustainable Living Centre; Get Communities Working

1:59 pm

Sid Sidebottom (Braddon, Australian Labor Party) | Hansard source

I want to congratulate the Penguin Sustainable Living Centre, which on Saturday, through the Get Communities Working fund, received something like $60,000 in support. I congratulate this government on its Get Communities Working fund, from which $900,000 has come to my electorate of Braddon. Some 22 projects have already been undertaken and are going very well. I would like to particularly thank the O group for the work they have done in administering this program.
