House debates

Monday, 20 August 2012

Personal Explanations

3:09 pm

Andrew Southcott (Boothby, Liberal Party, Shadow Parliamentary Secretary for Primary Healthcare) | Hansard source

Madam Deputy Speaker, I wish to make a personal explanation.

Ms Anna Burke (Chisholm, Deputy-Speaker) | Hansard source

Does the honourable member claim to have been misrepresented?

Andrew Southcott (Boothby, Liberal Party, Shadow Parliamentary Secretary for Primary Healthcare) | Hansard source

Yes.

Ms Anna Burke (Chisholm, Deputy-Speaker) | Hansard source

Please proceed.

Andrew Southcott (Boothby, Liberal Party, Shadow Parliamentary Secretary for Primary Healthcare) | Hansard source

On three occasions, the Leader of the House has made an assertion that last week, in tabling the electricity bills from the Belair Hotel, I tabled something other than that which I said I would. I was very clear. I sought leave to table the electricity accounts of January 2012 and July 2012 of the Belair Hotel, and that is exactly what I tabled. Hansard will confirm that and the Table Office will confirm that. The assertion by the Leader of the House is quite simply false.

Mr Albanese interjecting —

I am just responding—

Ms Anna Burke (Chisholm, Deputy-Speaker) | Hansard source

No. The member for Boothby cannot respond to interjections. The Leader of the House is not assisting the chair.

Anthony Albanese (Grayndler, Australian Labor Party, Leader of the House) | Hansard source

I am trying to help, Madam Deputy Speaker, as always!

Ms Anna Burke (Chisholm, Deputy-Speaker) | Hansard source

You are not.
