House debates

Monday, 29 October 2012

Statements on Indulgence

United Nations Security Council; Reference to Federation Chamber

2:18 pm

Anthony Albanese (Grayndler, Australian Labor Party, Leader of the House) | Hansard source

by leave—I move:

That further statements on Australia's seat on the United Nations Security Chamber be referred to the Federation Chamber.

Mr Pyne interjecting —

Do you want to come?

Christopher Pyne (Sturt, Liberal Party, Shadow Minister for Education, Apprenticeships and Training) | Hansard source

I'm on the wrong side!

Ms Anna Burke (Chisholm, Deputy-Speaker) | Hansard source

And you might be on the wrong side of the chamber very quickly.

Honourable members interjecting —

I mean on the wrong side of the whole chamber, if you are not careful.

Question agreed to.
