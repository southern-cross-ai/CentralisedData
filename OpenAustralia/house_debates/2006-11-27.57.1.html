Personal Explanations

3:28 pm

Bob McMullan (Fraser, Australian Labor Party) | Hansard source

Mr Speaker, I wish to make a personal explanation.

David Hawker (Speaker) | Hansard source

Does the honourable member claim to have been misrepresented?

Bob McMullan (Fraser, Australian Labor Party) | Hansard source

Yes, Mr Speaker, on more occasions than I can deal with here.

David Hawker (Speaker) | Hansard source

Please proceed.

Both those statements are complete fabrications made without any attempt to contact me to verify them.
