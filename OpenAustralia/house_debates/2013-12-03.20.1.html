House debates

Tuesday, 3 December 2013

Questions without Notice

Education Funding

2:10 pm

Bill Shorten (Maribyrnong, Australian Labor Party, Leader of the Opposition) | Hansard source

My question is to the Prime Minister. Under Labor's school agreements, states are required to invest one extra dollar for every two extra federal dollars. What is the rate of co-contribution from the states under the panicked deals that your government announced yesterday?

Opposition Members:

Opposition members interjecting —

Mrs Bronwyn Bishop (Speaker) | Hansard source

The Prime Minister has the call, but I would remind those on my left that, having listened to the Leader of the Opposition's question, if you want to hear the answer then we need some quiet.

Tony Abbott (Warringah, Liberal Party, Prime Minister) | Hansard source
