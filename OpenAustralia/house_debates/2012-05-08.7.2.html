House debates

Tuesday, 8 May 2012

Ministerial Arrangements

2:22 pm

Julia Gillard (Lalor, Australian Labor Party, Prime Minister) | Hansard source

I table for the information of the House a revised ministry list dated 8 May 2012.

The document read as follows—

SECOND GILLARD MINISTRY

8 May 2011

Each box represents a portfolio. Cabinet Ministers are shown in bold type. As a general rule, there is one department in each portfolio. However, there is a Department of Veterans' Affairs in the Defence portfolio. The title of a department does not necessarily reflect the title of a minister in all cases.

I inform the House that the Treasurer will be absent from question time today—I think everybody can guess why—and the Assistant Treasurer will answer questions on his behalf.
