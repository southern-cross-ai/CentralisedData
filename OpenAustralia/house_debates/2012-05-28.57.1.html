House debates

Monday, 28 May 2012

Questions without Notice

Enterprise Migration Agreements

2:12 pm

Scott Morrison (Cook, Liberal Party, Shadow Minister for Immigration and Citizenship) | Hansard source

My question is to the Minister for Immigration and Citizenship. When did the minister first inform the Prime Minister or her office that a decision with respect to the enterprise migration agreement for the Roy Hill project was imminent?

2:13 pm

Chris Bowen (McMahon, Australian Labor Party, Minister for Immigration and Citizenship) | Hansard source

There was, of course, substantial consultation between my office and other ministerial offices and a detailed brief was provided to the Prime Minister's office early in the week of 21 May.

Scott Morrison (Cook, Liberal Party, Shadow Minister for Immigration and Citizenship) | Hansard source

Madam Deputy Speaker, I have a supplementary question. Given the answer the minister has just given, are reports correct that he did not inform the Prime Minister or her office that this deal had been struck until she had returned from Chicago last Wednesday?

Chris Bowen (McMahon, Australian Labor Party, Minister for Immigration and Citizenship) | Hansard source

I refer to the answer I provided a few moments ago. The arrangement with Roy Hill was not struck until later that week. The detailed brief was provided to the Prime Minister's office early in the week of 21 May.

Ms Anna Burke (Chisholm, Deputy-Speaker) | Hansard source

I am watching the member for Mayo, for future reference.
