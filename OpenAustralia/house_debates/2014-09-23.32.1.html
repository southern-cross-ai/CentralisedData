Hughes Electorate: Georges River

1:59 pm

Craig Kelly (Hughes, Liberal Party) | Hansard source

Two hundred and nineteen years ago, Bass and Flinders were the first Europeans to sail down the Georges River . Since then, we have had an enormous increase in our prosperity, but unfortunately our Georges River has declined. Therefore, last week, I was very pleased to launch the Georges River Combined Councils' Committee's Aboriginal Riverkeepers Team. This is a team that will undertake bush regeneration across the Georges River catchment. It is a $1.78 million scheme funded by the federal government.
