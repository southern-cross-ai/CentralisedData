House debates

Monday, 2 June 2014

Questions without Notice

Veterans

2:10 pm

Gai Brodtmann (Canberra, Australian Labor Party, Shadow Parliamentary Secretary for Defence) | Hansard source

My question is to the Prime Minister. In 2012 the Prime Minister told the national RSL conference:

If it’s inadequate just to lift Centrelink pensions by the Consumer Price Index , it’s even less fair to apply solely that index to those who have risked their lives for our country. Loyalty goes both ways.

Why is the Prime Minister breaking his promise not to change pensions, by applying the consumer price index to the pensions of 140,463 veterans?

2:11 pm

Tony Abbott (Warringah, Liberal Party, Prime Minister) | Hansard source

Again I would say to the member opposite that we made a commitment that DFRDB and DFRB pensions would be indexed by the higher rate, a commitment which the former government made in opposition but failed to deliver on in government. We made that commitment in opposition and we have delivered on it in government.

Mark Dreyfus (Isaacs, Australian Labor Party, Shadow Attorney General) | Hansard source

Loyalty goes both ways!

Mrs Bronwyn Bishop (Speaker) | Hansard source

The member for Isaacs is warned.
