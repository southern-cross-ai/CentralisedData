Distinguished Visitors

Jenkins, Ms Kate, Hartland, Ms Kerri, Brighton, Ms Meg

9:01 am

Milton Dick (Speaker) | Hansard source

I'd like to acknowledge that present in the gallery for this important occasion are Kate Jenkins, the Sex Discrimination Commissioner and author of the Set the standard report; Kerri Hartland, the independent expert chair of the Parliamentary Leadership Taskforce; Meg Brighton, head of the Parliamentary Workplace Support Service; and, importantly, their important supporting staff. On behalf of the House I extend a warm welcome to you all.

Honourable members: Hear, hear!
