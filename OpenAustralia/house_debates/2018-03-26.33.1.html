House debates

Monday, 26 March 2018

Committees

Select Committee on Regional Development and Decentralisation; Membership

12:08 pm

Ian Goodenough (Moore, Liberal Party) | Hansard source

I have to report to the House that, on 8 March 2018, the Speaker received advice from the Chief Government Whip nominating Mr Drum to be a member of the Select Committee on Regional Development and Decentralisation. In accordance with standing order 229(b), as the House was not expected to sit for several weeks, the appointment became effective at that date. I now call the minister to move a motion to resolve membership of the committee.

12:09 pm

Darren Chester (Gippsland, National Party, Minister for Veterans' Affairs) | Hansard source

I move:

That Mr Drum be appointed a member of the Select Committee on Regional Development and Decentralisation.

Question agreed to.
