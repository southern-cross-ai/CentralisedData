1:57 pm

Ewen Jones (Herbert, Liberal Party) | Hansard source

Before the Canberra Raiders take on Souths, the mighty Cowboys will carry the weight of the nation as they go in against bloody Manly Warringah. It will be the mighty North Queensland Cowboys . We already have a Cowboys flag flying out the front of the member for Brisbane's office, and on Monday morning I expect to see a flag flying out the front of the office of the member for Warringah, the Leader of the Opposition, as the mighty Cowboys go marching forward.
