House debates

Monday, 12 October 2015

Committees

Membership

12:02 pm

Russell Broadbent (McMillan, Liberal Party) | Hansard source

Mr Speaker has received advice from the Chief Government Whip nominating changes to the membership of certain committees.

Darren Chester (Gippsland, National Party, Assistant Minister for Defence) | Hansard source

by leave—I move:

Standing Committee on Economics—Mr Alexander and Dr Hendy be discharged from the committee.

Standing Committee on Education and Employment—Mr E. T. Jones be discharged from the committee.

Standing Committee on the Environment—Mr Hawke be discharged from the committee.

Standing Committee on Health—Mr Wyatt be discharged from the committee.

Standing Committee on Procedure—Mr Wyatt be discharged from the committee.

Selection Committee—Mr Buchholz and Mr Nikolic be discharged from the committee and Mr E. T. Jones and Mr Whiteley be appointed members of the committee.

Standing Committee on Tax and Revenue—Mr Alexander be discharged from the committee.

Joint Standing Committee on Electoral Matters—Mr Hawke be discharged from the committee.

Joint Standing Committee on Foreign Affairs, Defence and Trade—Mr Hawke and Mr Wyatt Roy be discharged from the committee.

Parliamentary Joint Committee on Human Rights —Mr Wyatt be discharged from the committee and Mr Pasin be appointed a member of the committee.

Joint Standing Committee on the National Disability Insurance Scheme—Mr Brough and Mr Irons be discharged from the committee.

Joint Committee of Public Accounts and Audit—Dr Hendy and Mr Wyatt be discharged from the committee.

Joint Standing Committee on Treaties—Mr Wyatt Roy be discharged from the committee and Mr Taylor be appointed a member of the committee.

Question agreed to.
