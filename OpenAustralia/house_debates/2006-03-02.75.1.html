Thursday, 2 March 2006

Documents

3:12 pm

Tony Abbott (Warringah, Liberal Party, Leader of the House) | Hansard source

Documents are tabled as listed in the schedule circulated to honourable members. Details of the documents will be recorded in the Votes and Proceedings . I move:

That the House take note of the following documents:Foreign Affairs, Defence and Trade—Joint Standing Committee—Report—Australia’s human rights dialogue process—Government response.Migration Act 1958—Section 91Y—Protection visa processing taking more than 90 days—Report for the period 1 July to 31 October 2005.Public Accounts and Audit—Joint Statutory Committee—Report 403—Access of Indigenous Australians to law and justice services, June 2005—Government response.Superannuation (Government Co-contribution for Low Income Earners) Act—Quarterly report on the operation of the Act—1 October to 31 December 2005

Debate (on motion by Ms Gillard ) adjourned.
