Report summarising the petitions and ministerial responses being presented.

The committee met in private session in the 47th Parliament on 13 September 2023 and 18 October 2023.

1. The committee resolved to present the following three petitions in accordance with standing order 207:

Petition certified on 13 September 2023

From 11291 petitioners—regarding a moratorium on offshore wind turbine developments off the Illawarra coastline (EN5444)

Petitions certified on 18 October 2023

From 641 petitioners—regarding access to treatment for patients on opioid agonist medications ( PN 0571)

From 150 petitioners—requesting that residency or a visa extension be granted for Mr Peter Hodgson (PN0572)
