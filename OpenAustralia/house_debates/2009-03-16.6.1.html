House debates

Monday, 16 March 2009

Committees

Climate Change, Water, Environment and the Arts Committee; Membership

Harry Jenkins (Speaker) | Hansard source

I have received advice from the Chief Government Whip that he has nominated Mr Murphy to be a member of the Standing Committee on Climate Change , Water, Environment and the Arts in place of Mrs D’Ath.

12:02 pm

Greg Combet (Charlton, Australian Labor Party, Parliamentary Secretary for Climate Change) | Hansard source

by leave—I move:

That Mrs D’Ath be discharged from the Standing Committee on Climate Change , Water, Environment and the Arts and that, in her place, Mr Murphy be appointed a member of the committee.

Question agreed to.
