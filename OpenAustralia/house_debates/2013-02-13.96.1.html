House debates

Wednesday, 13 February 2013

Personal Explanations

3:14 pm

Steve Irons (Swan, Liberal Party) | Hansard source

Madam Speaker, I wish to make a personal explanation.

Ms Anna Burke (Speaker) | Hansard source

Does the honourable member claim to have been misrepresented?

Steve Irons (Swan, Liberal Party) | Hansard source

I certainly do.

Ms Anna Burke (Speaker) | Hansard source

Please proceed.

Steve Irons (Swan, Liberal Party) | Hansard source

During question time today the Treasurer, in response to my question about the Gateway project, said that I did not support the Gateway project. I know this is a target-rich environment but the fact is, in 2010, the coalition made a commitment to the Gateway project, which I heartily supported.
