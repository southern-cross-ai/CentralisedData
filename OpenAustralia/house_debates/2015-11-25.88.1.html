House debates

Wednesday, 25 November 2015

Questions without Notice

Parliament: Integrity

2:56 pm

Mark Dreyfus (Isaacs, Australian Labor Party, Shadow Attorney General) | Hansard source

My question is to the Special Minister of State . I refer to the minister's answer yesterday. How does the minister respond to the following words contained in an AFP search warrant, which I quote in part:

Between 23 and March and 13 April 2012, Malcolm Thomas Brough … counselled and procured James Hunter Ashby, to access restricted data, namely the former Speaker of the House of Representatives … official diary, contrary to Section 478.1 of the Criminal Code 1995 …

Mr Ewen Jones interjecting —

Tony Smith (Speaker) | Hansard source

The member for Herbert will cease interjecting .

Mr Ewen Jones interjecting —

The member for Herbert is warned.

2:57 pm

Mal Brough (Fisher, Liberal Party, Special Minister of State) | Hansard source

I refer the member for Isaacs to my statement of the 18th. I have nothing further to add, and what you are referring to are of course allegations.
