House debates

Tuesday, 15 June 2021

Ministerial Arrangements

2:09 pm

Michael McCormack (Riverina, National Party, Leader of the Nationals) | Hansard source

I inform the House that the Prime Minister will be absent from question time today and for the remainder of the week, as he is in the United Kingdom for the G7-plus summit. I will answer questions on his behalf. The Minister for Veterans' Affairs and Minister for Defence Personnel will also be absent from question time today, and the Minister for Defence will answer questions on his behalf.
