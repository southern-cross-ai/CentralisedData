House debates

Monday, 29 October 2012

Personal Explanations

3:10 pm

Sharman Stone (Murray, Liberal Party) | Hansard source

I wish to make a personal explanation.

Ms Anna Burke (Chisholm, Deputy-Speaker) | Hansard source

Does the member for Murray claim to have been misrepresented?

Sharman Stone (Murray, Liberal Party) | Hansard source

Most grievously.

Ms Anna Burke (Chisholm, Deputy-Speaker) | Hansard source

The member for Murray has the call.

Sharman Stone (Murray, Liberal Party) | Hansard source

Thank you. A short time ago, in answer to a question from his own side, the minister for the environment suggested that I was advocating additional water be taken off irrigators, to wit, referring to the Friday announcement of an extra 450 gigalitres. I certainly have never advocated and will never advocate for an additional 450 gigalitres to be taken from irrigators, and I certainly have said in the past that there should not be any water further purchased on a non-strategic basis from irrigators. Any additional water in the past should have been found through irrigation investment and environmental works and measures.

3:11 pm

Most grievously, Madam Speaker.
