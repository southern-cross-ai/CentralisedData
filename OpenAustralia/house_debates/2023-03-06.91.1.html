Monday, 6 March 2023

Questions without Notice

Income Tax

2:43 pm

Zoe McKenzie (Flinders, Liberal Party) | Hansard source

My question is to the Prime Minister. Before the election the Prime Minister said he would stand by the legislated stage 3 tax cuts. Will the Prime Minister rule out any changes to the stage 3 tax cuts, or will this be the next broken promise from this tricky Prime Minister?

2:44 pm

Anthony Albanese (Grayndler, Australian Labor Party, Prime Minister) | Hansard source

I thank the member for Flinders for her question. We've made our priority very clear, and so have the Liberals. We now have the first commitment from the Leader of the Opposition, which is on tax issues.

Milton Dick (Speaker) | Hansard source

The member for Bowman is warned.

Anthony Albanese (Grayndler, Australian Labor Party, Prime Minister) | Hansard source

If successful at the next election—with the support, I assume of the member for Flinders if she's successful—he will introduce better tax concessions for those people who have $100 million in their super account.

Milton Dick (Speaker) | Hansard source
