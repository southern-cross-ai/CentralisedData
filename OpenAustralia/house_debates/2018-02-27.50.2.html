2:22 pm

Mr Tony Burke (Watson, Australian Labor Party, Manager of Opposition Business (House)) | Hansard source

My question is to the Deputy Prime Minister. This morning the former Deputy Prime Minister, the member for New England, refused to rule out a return to the job. How long does the current Deputy Prime Minister plan to stay in his role?

Tony Smith (Speaker) | Hansard source

The Leader of the House, on a point of order?
