House debates

Monday, 27 March 2006

Main Committee

Dick Adams (Lyons, Australian Labor Party) | Hansard source

I advise the House that the Deputy Speaker has fixed Tuesday, 28 March, at 4 pm, as the time for the next meeting of the Main Committee, unless an alternative day or hour is fixed.

6:31 pm

Kerry Bartlett (Macquarie, Liberal Party) | Hansard source

by leave—I move:

That unless otherwise ordered, for the Main Committee meeting on Tuesday, 28 March 2006, the order of business be as follows:

(a)
Members’ statements, each for no longer than 3 minutes, with the item of business continuing for 30 minutes irrespective of suspensions for divisions in the House;
(b)
an 80 minute adjournment debate (with each Member speaking for no longer than 10 minutes); and
(c)
orders of the day, government business.

Question agreed to.
