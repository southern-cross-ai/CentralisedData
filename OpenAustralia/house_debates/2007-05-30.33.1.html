House debates

Wednesday, 30 May 2007

Questions without Notice

Nuclear Energy

2:27 pm

Kevin Rudd (Griffith, Australian Labor Party, Leader of the Opposition) | Hansard source

My question is again to the Prime Minister. I refer to the Prime Minister’s answer to my previous question where he said that the government was disposed to having a taxpayer funded advertising campaign on the nuclear energy option for Australia. Prime Minister, don’t the Australian people have a right to know whether this campaign will be conducted before the next election and how much it will cost?

John Howard (Bennelong, Liberal Party, Prime Minister) | Hansard source

My understanding is that rolled into both climate change and energy was the $52 million over five years. That was outlined in the budget papers, so I do not know where the nondisclosure is.
