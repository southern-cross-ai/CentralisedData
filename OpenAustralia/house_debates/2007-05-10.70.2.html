House debates

Thursday, 10 May 2007

Questions to the Speaker

Questions in Writing

3:13 pm

Steve Georganas (Hindmarsh, Australian Labor Party) | Hansard source

Mr Speaker, under standing order 105(b) I ask that you write to the relevant ministers and seek reasons why the following questions in writing have not been answered: 3799, 3800, 4905, 4908, 5321 and 5396.

David Hawker (Speaker) | Hansard source

I thank the member for Hindmarsh and I will follow up his request.

Sharon Bird (Cunningham, Australian Labor Party) | Hansard source

Mr Speaker, under the same standing order I ask you to write to the Treasurer regarding question No. 507 from 8 February 2005—not difficult, just asking whether he has visited my electorate—and 1139 on 10 May 2005 to the Prime Minister regarding family impact statements in cabinet submissions.

David Hawker (Speaker) | Hansard source

I thank the member for Cunningham and I will follow up her request.
