House debates

Tuesday, 4 March 2014

Questions without Notice

Assistant Minister for Health: Staffing

3:07 pm

Ms Catherine King (Ballarat, Australian Labor Party, Shadow Minister for Health) | Hansard source

My question is to the Prime Minister. I refer to the Prime Minister's statement during question time last Wednesday that the Assistant Minister for Health has done nothing wrong. If this is the case, will the Prime Minister release the document detailing how the minister's former chief of staff's conflict of interest was to be managed? If not, why not?

Tony Abbott (Warringah, Liberal Party, Prime Minister) | Hansard source

This government, like all governments, respects the confidentiality of this process and it is not in the business of revealing personal information about people.
