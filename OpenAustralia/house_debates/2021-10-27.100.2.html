House debates

Wednesday, 27 October 2021

Statements

Personal Explanation

3:11 pm

Brian Mitchell (Lyons, Australian Labor Party) | Hansard source

[by video link] Mr Speaker, I wish to make a personal explanation.

Tony Smith (Speaker) | Hansard source

Does the honourable member claim to have been misrepresented?

Brian Mitchell (Lyons, Australian Labor Party) | Hansard source

I do.

Tony Smith (Speaker) | Hansard source

You may proceed.

Brian Mitchell (Lyons, Australian Labor Party) | Hansard source

Thank you, Mr Speaker. In yesterday's MPI the member for Lyne accused myself and other members of hypocrisy for criticising the BBRF while welcoming projects in our own electorates. My criticism went to the fact that 90 per cent of the grant money under the scheme went to coalition seats. That's a fact illustrated by my seat receiving $4 million and Mallee receiving $38 million, which is about on point. So far from hypocrisy, it's simply fact and truth.
