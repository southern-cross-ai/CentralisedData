House debates

Tuesday, 13 May 2008

National Library of Australia; Advisory Council on Australian Archives

Membership

Dick Adams (Lyons, Australian Labor Party) | Hansard source

Mr Speaker has received advice that Mr MJ Ferguson has resigned as a member of the Council of the National Library of Australia.

5:35 pm

Mike Kelly (Eden-Monaro, Australian Labor Party, Parliamentary Secretary for Defence Support) | Hansard source

by leave—I move:

That:

(a)
in accordance with the provisions of the National Library Act 1960 , this House elects Mr Melham to be a member of the Council of the National Library of Australia and to continue as a member for a period of three years from this day; and
(b)
in accordance with the provisions of section 10 of the Archives Act 1983 , this House appoints Mr Somlyay as a member of the Advisory Council on Australian Archives for a period of three years.

Question agreed to.
