House debates

Wednesday, 13 September 2006

Questions to the Speaker

Parliamentary Language

3:11 pm

Chris Bowen (Prospect, Australian Labor Party) | Hansard source

Mr Speaker, I refer you to page 188 of House of Representatives Practice , which states:

It is not acceptable to raise points of order concerning proceedings earlier in the day or concerning proceedings of a previous day.

This is a ruling that you have enforced previously, Mr Speaker. Can I ask: when is it acceptable for a member to raise a point of order regarding something which has happened earlier in the day, as the Leader of the House just did, and when is it not?

David Hawker (Speaker) | Hansard source

The member for Prospect would be aware I am not going to provide commentary on rulings. The point is that the Leader of the House asked for a withdrawal and a withdrawal was given.
