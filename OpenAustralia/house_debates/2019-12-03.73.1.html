Questions without Notice

Minister for Energy and Emissions Reduction

2:57 pm

Mr Tony Burke (Watson, Australian Labor Party, Shadow Minister for the Arts) | Hansard source

My question is to the Minister for Energy and Emissions Reduction. Yesterday the minister's office provided a response to claims that he's been misleading the parliament ever since his first speech, when he claimed that Naomi Wolf was living a few doors down the corridor when, in fact, she was on the other side of the Atlantic Ocean . Given the minister has been misleading the parliament since the day he arrived, is he proud of his consistency and that he has continued his career as he began it?

Tony Smith (Speaker) | Hansard source
