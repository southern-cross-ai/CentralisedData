Ministerial Arrangements

2:07 pm

Julia Gillard (Lalor, Australian Labor Party, Prime Minister) | Hansard source

I table for the information of the House a revised ministry list reflecting changes to the ministry made on 21 February. The changes reflect the appointment of Senator the Hon. Kate Lundy as Parliamentary Secretary for Immigration and Multicultural Affairs . I seek leave to have the document incorporated into Hansard .

Leave granted.
