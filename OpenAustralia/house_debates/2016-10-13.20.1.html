Bills

VET Student Loans (Charges) Bill 2016; Second Reading

10:35 am

Karen Andrews (McPherson, Liberal Party, Assistant Minister for Vocational Education and Skills) | Hansard source

I move:

That this bill be now read a second time.

As outlined in the second reading speech for the VET Student Loans Bill 2016, the VET Student Loans (Charges) Bill 2016 enables the Commonwealth to charge approved VET Student Loans program providers an annual charge.

I commend the bill.

Debate adjourned.
