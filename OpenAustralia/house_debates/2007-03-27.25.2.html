House debates

Tuesday, 27 March 2007

Personal Explanations

3:16 pm

Anthony Albanese (Grayndler, Australian Labor Party, Manager of Opposition Business in the House) | Hansard source

Mr Speaker, I wish to make a personal explanation.

David Hawker (Speaker) | Hansard source

Does the honourable member claim to have been misrepresented?

Anthony Albanese (Grayndler, Australian Labor Party, Manager of Opposition Business in the House) | Hansard source

Yes, on a couple of occasions.

David Hawker (Speaker) | Hansard source

Please proceed.

Anthony Albanese (Grayndler, Australian Labor Party, Manager of Opposition Business in the House) | Hansard source

Firstly, today in question time, the Minister for Employment and Workplace Relations suggested that I, as the local member, and others on this side of the House had not been interested in Tristar. I asked questions on 10 August 2006 and again in November 2006. I gave speeches on 9 August 2006, on 17 August 2006—

Tony Abbott (Warringah, Liberal Party, Leader of the House) | Hansard source

Mr Speaker, this is not a personal explanation. This is an argument.

David Hawker (Speaker) | Hansard source

The member for Grayndler must show where he has been personally misrepresented.

Anthony Albanese (Grayndler, Australian Labor Party, Manager of Opposition Business in the House) | Hansard source

I am. I and others on this side have taken up the issue of Tristar workers consistently—a fact the government ignored until Alan Jones took up the case in January this year.

David Hawker (Speaker) | Hansard source

The member has shown where he has been misrepresented.

Anthony Albanese (Grayndler, Australian Labor Party, Manager of Opposition Business in the House) | Hansard source

Secondly, last night in a speech in the chamber, the member for Tangney gave a speech in which he defended his proposed climate change solution of having an orbital shadecloth in the sky to protect—

David Hawker (Speaker) | Hansard source

Order! The member will come to where he claims to have been misrepresented.

Anthony Albanese (Grayndler, Australian Labor Party, Manager of Opposition Business in the House) | Hansard source

He stated:

The member for Grayndler, in similar fashion to Labor itself, would not have any idea about actually thinking through issues or thinking of new ideas ...

And that I had ‘a lack of critical or logical thinking’—

Government Members:

Government members interjecting —

Anthony Albanese (Grayndler, Australian Labor Party, Manager of Opposition Business in the House) | Hansard source

I am quoting him.

Tony Abbott (Warringah, Liberal Party, Leader of the House) | Hansard source

Mr Speaker, this is an argument that is being developed by the member for Grayndler. He is not showing where he has been misrepresented and, plainly, he has no intention of doing so.

David Hawker (Speaker) | Hansard source

Order! Before I call the member for Grayndler, he must show where he has been personally misrepresented.

Anthony Albanese (Grayndler, Australian Labor Party, Manager of Opposition Business in the House) | Hansard source

Mr Speaker, the member for Tangney said that I had ‘a lack of critical or logical thinking’ with regard to climate change, because I do not support a shadecloth in the sky—

David Hawker (Speaker) | Hansard source

The member will resume his seat!
