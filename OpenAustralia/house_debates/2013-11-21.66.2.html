House debates

Thursday, 21 November 2013

Questions without Notice

Commonwealth Scientific and Industrial Research Organisation

2:03 pm

Bill Shorten (Maribyrnong, Australian Labor Party, Leader of the Opposition) | Hansard source

My question is to the Minister for Industry. I refer the minister to the CSIRO , an organisation which is central to Australia's future prosperity for scientific achievement and innovation. Minister, why are 600 staff to face the Abbott axe?

Joe Hockey (North Sydney, Liberal Party, Treasurer) | Hansard source

They were your cuts.

Ian Macfarlane (Groom, Liberal Party, Minister for Industry) | Hansard source

As the Leader of the Opposition knows, management decisions on the CSIRO are decisions by the CSIRO.
