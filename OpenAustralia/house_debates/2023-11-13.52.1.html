Monday, 13 November 2023

Statements by Members

Western Australian Governor's Prayer Breakfast

1:34 pm

Sam Lim (Tangney, Australian Labor Party) | Hansard source

I stand before you today with a heart full of gratitude and hope, having had the privilege of being a guest of my local Mount Pleasant Baptist Church for the recent WA Governor's Prayer Breakfast. This event was not just a gathering of individuals of Christian faith. It was a profound experience that really touched me. On that day I walked into a room filled with people from diverse backgrounds, coming together with a common goal: to reflect, pray and share in the spirit of hope and faith. It was the first thing on my agenda, having flown back from sitting in parliament only the night before. It is always heartwarming for me to attend events with my fellow Christian brothers and sisters, to share in their belief, welcoming me in, even though I am a Buddhist.

The Governor's Prayer Breakfast was a testament to the power of togetherness and really highlighted the importance of prayers and spiritual reflection. The speakers were so inspiring. Their stories of faith and resilience and the impact of prayer on their lives was so deep. They reminded us that, in the face of adversity, one's faith and prayer can be a source of strength and comfort.

I would like to extend my heartfelt thanks to Pastor Gary from Mount Pleasant Baptist Church and to everyone who attended.
