Questions without Notice

Donations to Political Parties

2:45 pm

Mark Butler (Hindmarsh, Australian Labor Party, Shadow Minister for Climate Change and Energy) | Hansard source

My question is to the Prime Minister. I refer to evidence to the Independent Commission Against Corruption that the Minister for Energy and Emissions Reduction was involved in laundering illegal donations through the Liberal Party front organisation known as the Free Enterprise Foundation. What action has the Prime Minister taken to investigate the minister's conduct?
