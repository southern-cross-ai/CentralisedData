Monday, 19 June 2023

Statements by Members

Marillanca, Mr Victor

1:31 pm

David Smith (Bean, Australian Labor Party) | Hansard source

I rise to pay tribute to the significant achievements of my constituent and friend Victor Marillanca. This month, 24 June marks the 47th anniversary of his radio program Cone cci on Latino Americana , a Spanish-language program which brings news and information to the Spanish-speaking community in Canberra. It was inaugurated in 1976, making Victor the longest-serving presenter of a Spanish-language program on community radio in Australia.

Victor has led a remarkable life. He came to Australia as a refugee from the Pinochet regime in Chile. Later this year we will recognise the 50th anniversary of the terrible coup. His political activism for justice and democracy in Chile resulted in his persecution by the regime.

Since arriving in Australia, Victor has dedicated himself to service to his community. His service has been rightly recognised. In 2021, the Mexican government recognised Victor with the Ohtli Award, one of the highest awards the Mexican government can present to non-citizens of Mexico. He has provided enormous support for the Latin American diaspora. Additionally, Victor will receive life membership of the Australian Labor Party at the ACT conference later in July this year. Victor's record of service to the community is exemplary, and I'm pleased to be able to pay tribute to him.
