The minister has told us he is interested in the triple bottom-line approach when it comes to the Murray-Darling Basin Plan. But, no, this particular modelling is based on relaxed constraints. What does that mean? It means you ignore the fact that there are roads, bridges, railway lines, houses, towns and the Barmah Choke.
