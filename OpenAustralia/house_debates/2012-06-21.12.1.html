House debates

Thursday, 21 June 2012

Committees

Social Policy and Legal Affairs Committee; Report

9:53 am

Graham Perrett (Moreton, Australian Labor Party) | Hansard source

I apologise for interrupting the debate and commend the member for Fremantle on her contribution. I table the advisory report on the Australian Human Rights Commission Amendment (National Children's Commissioner) Bill 2012 and a dissenting report, and I seek leave to make a very short statement.

Leave granted.

This advisory report on behalf of the House Standing Committee on Social Policy and Legal Affairs and the Australian Human Rights Commission explores the future contribution of the National Children's Commissioner to Australian society. I look forward to speaking further on the report later.

9:54 am

Judi Moylan (Pearce, Liberal Party) | Hansard source

Likewise I apologise for interrupting the debate. On behalf of the coalition I seek leave to make a short statement on the dissenting report.

Leave granted.

I will speak on the dissenting report at a later date.

Debate adjourned.
