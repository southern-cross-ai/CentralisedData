Tuesday, 23 February 2021

Questions without Notice

Members of Parliament: Staff

2:46 pm

Richard Marles (Corio, Australian Labor Party, Deputy Leader of the Opposition) | Hansard source

My question is to the Prime Minister. Does the Prime Minister owe a duty of care to all ministerial staff?

Scott Morrison (Cook, Liberal Party, Prime Minister) | Hansard source

Both the codes that relate to the management of staff and my ministers and the Ministerial Code of Conduct set out all the obligations I have to my staff, and at all times I seek to abide by those.
