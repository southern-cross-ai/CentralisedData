House debates

Wednesday, 23 November 2011

Committees

Public Works Committee; Approval of Work

12:29 pm

Jason Clare (Blaxland, Australian Labor Party, Minister for Defence Materiel) | Hansard source

On behalf of the Minister for Regional Australia , Regional Development and Local Government , I move:

That, in accordance with section 5 of the Parliament Act 1974, the House approves the following proposals for works in the Parliamentary Zone which were presented to the House on 21 November 2011, namely: High Court of Australia south western forecourt landscape works, and installation of a raised concrete bench seat at the southern end of the Ceremonial Ramp at the entrance to the High Court of Australia.

Question agreed to.
