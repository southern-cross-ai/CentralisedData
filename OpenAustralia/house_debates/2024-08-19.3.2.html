Report summarising the petitions and ministerial responses being presented.

The committee met in private session in the 47th Parliament 3 July and 14 August 2024.

1. The committee resolved to present the following 2 petitions in accordance with standing order 207:

Petitions certified on 3 July 2024

From 7363 petitioners—requesting that independent local post offices be able to provide additional services to support communities (EN6326)

Petitions certified on 14 August 2024

From 16046 petitioners—requesting that independent local post offices be able to provide additional services to support communities ( PN 0615)
