They reach the table as one colourful character introduces his guest and says, 'Say hello to my little friend.' That little friend, of course, is ' Matthew Guy father', leader of the Libs in Victoria. But this isn't a crime novel, Speaker; it's a fundraising event for the Liberal Party. 'Tough on crime!' he shrieks to the public, while quietly munching lobsters with the mobsters. It's time for Matthew Guy to come clean, end the lies or resign.
