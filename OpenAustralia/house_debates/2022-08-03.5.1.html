9:14 am

Milton Dick (Speaker) | Hansard source

The question is that the bill now be read a third time.

I just note that there was no leave sought from the House.

I remind the member of the new standing order 85, under which leave is not required.

Andrew Wallace (Fisher, Liberal National Party) | Hansard source

any further questions necessary to complete the remaining stages of the bill to be put without delay.

Does that negate the requirement for leave to be sought? I'm just seeking clarification, that's all.

Milton Dick (Speaker) | Hansard source

Yes, it does.
