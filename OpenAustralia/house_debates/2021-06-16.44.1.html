House debates

Wednesday, 16 June 2021

Committees

Australia's Family Law System Joint Select Committee; Reporting Date

12:55 pm

Ross Vasta (Bonner, Liberal Party) | Hansard source

The Speaker has received the following message from the Senate informing the House that the Senate has agreed to the following resolutions:

That the time for the presentation of the final report of the Joint Select Committee on Australia's Family Law System be extended to 29 October 2021.

The Senate requests the concurrence of the House of Representatives in this resolution.

Ordered that the message be considered immediately.

Stuart Robert (Fadden, Liberal Party, Minister for Employment, Workforce, Skills, Small and Family Business) | Hansard source

I move:

That the House concur with the resolution of the Senate.

Question agreed to.
