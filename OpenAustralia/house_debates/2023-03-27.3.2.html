Report summarising the petitions and ministerial responses being presented.

T he committee met in private session in the 47th Parliament on 15 February 2023, 8 March 2023 and 22 March 2023.

1. The committee resolved to present the following 9 petitions in accordance with standing order 207:

From 18 petitioners—regarding funding for Trove (EN4791)

From 2627 petitioners—regarding the processing time of Parent Visaapplications (EN4798)

From 15 petitioners—regarding the access to Australia of vaccinated people without health insurance (EN4799)

From 748 petitioners—regarding the housing crisis action, regulation of rental increases and vacancy tax (EN4800)

From 26 petitioners—requesting to bring the Australian Rail Track Corporation under direct government control (EN4802)

From 23 petitioners—requesting routine screening for sepsis in Australia (EN4805)

From 117 petitioners—regarding Santos Gas Fracking and protecting the Surat Artesian Basin (EN4806)

From 65 petitioners—requesting to lower the cost of diesel (EN4807)

From 1 petitioner—regarding tick-borne infection testing, treatment and record-keeping in Australia ( PN 0556)
