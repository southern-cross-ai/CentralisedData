1:59 pm

Joanne Ryan (Lalor, Australian Labor Party) | Hansard source

I was six when, in 1967, the Australian public voted yes to the referendum question to count First Australians as Australian people in the census. To my people at home, I say: bring on the 'yes' vote, bring on the momentum; let's get this work done.
