House debates

Tuesday, 2 June 2009

Notices

The following notices were given:

Jenny Macklin (Jagajaga, Australian Labor Party, Minister for Families, Housing, Community Services and Indigenous Affairs) | Hansard source

to present a bill for an act to amend the law relating to family assistance, and for related purposes.

Nicola Roxon (Gellibrand, Australian Labor Party, Minister for Health and Ageing) | Hansard source

to present a bill for an act to amend the law in relation to private health insurance, and for related purposes.

Malcolm Turnbull (Wentworth, Liberal Party, Leader of the Opposition) | Hansard source

to move:

That the House expresses its concern that:

(1)
the Government will increase the national public debt by 2013 to an unprecedented $315 billion;
(2)
the Government’s interest bill on this debt, as yet undisclosed, will be a continuing burden on Australian families for future generations;
(3)
despite claims that the Government will pay off its debt by 2022, the Government has refused to provide any modelling to substantiate this claim; and
(4)
the high level of Government borrowing will place an ongoing and upward pressure on interest rates for Australian families.
