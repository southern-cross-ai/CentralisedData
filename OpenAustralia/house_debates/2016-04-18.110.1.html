House debates

Monday, 18 April 2016

Questions without Notice

Donations to Political Parties

3:08 pm

Mark Dreyfus (Isaacs, Australian Labor Party, Shadow Attorney General) | Hansard source

My question is to the Minister representing the Special Minister of State . I refer to the minister's previous answer. Will the minister report to the House before question time tomorrow on whether the Special Minister of State will direct the Australian Electoral Commission to report on whether any organisation that received donations from the Free Enterprise Foundation complied with all Commonwealth electoral laws?

Scott Morrison (Cook, Liberal Party, Treasurer) | Hansard source

I will consult with the Special Minister of State and I am sure he will report in accordance with the normal practice.
