House debates

Monday, 19 October 2015

Committees

Membership

7:47 pm

Steve Irons (Swan, Liberal Party) | Hansard source

I have received advice from the Chief Government Whip and the Chief Opposition Whip that they have nominated members to be members of certain committees.

Michael McCormack (Riverina, National Party, Assistant Minister to the Deputy Prime Minister) | Hansard source

by leave—I move:

That:

(1) Mrs McNamara be appointed a member of the Standing Committee on Health;

(2) Mr van Manen be discharged from the Standing Committee on Indigenous Affairs and that, in his place, Mr Entsch be appointed a member of the committee;

(3) Ms Marino, Mr Pitt, Mr van Manen and Mr Williams be appointed members of the Standing Committee on Infrastructure, Transport and Cities, and Mrs Prentice be appointed a supplementary member for the purpose of the committee’s inquiry into the High Speed Rail Planning Authority Bill 2015;

(4) Mrs Griggs be appointed a member of the Standing Committee of Privileges and Members’ Interests;

(5) Mr Alexander and Mr Goodenough be discharged from the Standing Committee on Tax and Revenue and that, in their place, Mr Hastie and Mr Irons be appointed members of the committee;

(6) Mr Baldwin and Mr Pitt be appointed members of the Joint Standing Committee on Foreign Affairs, Defence and Trade;

(7) Mr Sukkar be appointed a member of the Parliamentary Joint Committee on Human Rights ;

(8) Mr K . J. Andrews be appointed a member of the Joint Standing Committee on Migration;

(9) Mr Matheson be appointed a member of the Joint Standing Committee on the National Disability Insurance Scheme;

(10) Mrs B. K. Bishop and Dr Gillespie be appointed members of the Joint Committee of Public Accounts and Audit;

(11) Dr Jensen be discharged from the Joint Standing Committee on Treaties and that, in his place, Mrs Wicks be appointed a member of the committee;

(12)Mr Watts be appointed a member of the Standing Committee on Communications and the Arts;

(13)Dr Chalmers be discharged from the Standing Committee on Economics and that, in his place, Ms O’Neil be appointed a member of the committee;

(14)Mr Watts be discharged from the Standing Committee on Health and that, in his place, Mr Zappia be appointed a member of the committee;

(15)Mr Giles be discharged from the Standing Committee on Indigenous Affairs and that, in his place, Ms Claydon be appointed a member of the committee;

(16)Mr Thistlethwaite be appointed a member of the Standing Committee on Infrastructure, Transport and Cities;

(17)Dr Chalmers be discharged from the Joint Select Committee on Trade and Investment Growth and that, in his place, Ms T. M. Butler be appointed a member of the committee;

Question agreed to.
