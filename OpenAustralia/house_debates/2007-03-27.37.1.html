House debates

Tuesday, 27 March 2007

Committees

Privileges Committee; Australian Commission for Law Enforcement Integrity Committee; Membership

4:23 pm

Phillip Barresi (Deakin, Liberal Party) | Hansard source

Mr Speaker has received advice from the Chief Opposition Whip nominating members to be members of certain committees.

Sussan Ley (Farrer, Liberal Party, Parliamentary Secretary to the Minister for Agriculture, Fisheries and Forestry) | Hansard source

I move:

That:

(1)
Ms A. E. Burke be discharged from the Committee of Privileges and that, in her place, Ms A. L. Ellis be appointed a member of the committee; and
(2)
Ms Hall and Mr Wilkie be appointed members of the Parliamentary Joint Committee on the Australian Commission for Law Enforcement Integrity.

Question agreed to.
