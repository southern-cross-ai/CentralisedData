House debates

Monday, 21 November 2011

Documents

Presentation

Anthony Albanese (Grayndler, Australian Labor Party, Leader of the House) | Hansard source

Documents are presented in accordance with the list circulated to honourable members earlier today. Details of the documents will be recorded in the Votes and Proceedings and I move:

That the House take note of the following documents:

Australian Electoral Commission —Funding and disclosure report—Election 2010.

Australian Prudential Regulation Authority —Report for 2010-11.

Communications, Information Technology and the Arts—House of Representatives Standing Committee—Report—Digital television: Who’s buying it?—Government response.

Crimes Act 1914 Review of the operation of subdivision A of division 6 of part VIIC of the Act—Final report, September 2011.

Great Barrier Reef Marine Park Authority —Report for 2010-11.

Safe Work Australia—Report for 2010-11.

Sydney Airport Demand Management Act—Quarterly report on movement cap for Sydney airport for the period 1 July to 30 September 2011.

Telecommunications (Interception and Access) Act 1979 Report on the operation of the Act for 2010-11—Corrigendum.

Torres Strait Regional Authority —Report for 2010-11.

Debate adjourned.
