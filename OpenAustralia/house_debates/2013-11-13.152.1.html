House debates

Wednesday, 13 November 2013

Statements on Indulgence

Member for Griffith; Reference to Federation Chamber

9:16 pm

Mr Tony Burke (Watson, Australian Labor Party, Shadow Minister for Finance) | Hansard source

A large number of members of parliament would like to provide the gift to Kevin's family of some gentler words than those you referred to. With that in mind, by leave, I move:

That further statements by indulgence in relation to the resignation of Mr Rudd be permitted in the Federation Chamber.

Christopher Pyne (Sturt, Liberal Party, Minister for Education) | Hansard source

I second the motion.

Question agreed to.

House adjourned at 21:17
