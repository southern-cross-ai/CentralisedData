House debates

Wednesday, 22 August 2012

Personal Explanations

3:19 pm

Joe Hockey (North Sydney, Liberal Party, Shadow Treasurer) | Hansard source

Madam Deputy Speaker, I seek leave to make a personal explanation.

Ms Anna Burke (Chisholm, Deputy-Speaker) | Hansard source

Does the member for North Sydney claim to have been misrepresented?

Joe Hockey (North Sydney, Liberal Party, Shadow Treasurer) | Hansard source

Yes, I have been.

Ms Anna Burke (Chisholm, Deputy-Speaker) | Hansard source

Please proceed.

Joe Hockey (North Sydney, Liberal Party, Shadow Treasurer) | Hansard source

The Prime Minister today alleged that I and the coalition had claimed that there was a $70 billion hole. There is no $70 billion hole. I never claimed it, and it is another lie.

Ms Anna Burke (Chisholm, Deputy-Speaker) | Hansard source

The member will withdraw the allegation of lying.

Joe Hockey (North Sydney, Liberal Party, Shadow Treasurer) | Hansard source

I withdraw.

Ms Anna Burke (Chisholm, Deputy-Speaker) | Hansard source

I thank the member.

Andrew Robb (Goldstein, Liberal Party, Chairman of the Coalition Policy Development Committee) | Hansard source

Madam Deputy Speaker, I seek leave to make a personal explanation.

Ms Anna Burke (Chisholm, Deputy-Speaker) | Hansard source

Does the member for Goldstein claim to have been misrepresented?

Andrew Robb (Goldstein, Liberal Party, Chairman of the Coalition Policy Development Committee) | Hansard source

I most certainly have been.

Ms Anna Burke (Chisholm, Deputy-Speaker) | Hansard source

Please proceed.

Andrew Robb (Goldstein, Liberal Party, Chairman of the Coalition Policy Development Committee) | Hansard source

The Minister for Families, Community Services and Indigenous Affairs claimed that I had said that services would be cut to families under a coalition government, when I had said quite explicitly that the coalition government would look to slash the cost and duplication of Labor's regulations.

3:20 pm

John Cobb (Calare, National Party, Shadow Minister for Agriculture and Food Security) | Hansard source

Madam Deputy Speaker, I seek leave to make a personal explanation.

Ms Anna Burke (Chisholm, Deputy-Speaker) | Hansard source

Does the member for Calare claim to have been misrepresented?

John Cobb (Calare, National Party, Shadow Minister for Agriculture and Food Security) | Hansard source

Most grievously.

Ms Anna Burke (Chisholm, Deputy-Speaker) | Hansard source

Please proceed.

John Cobb (Calare, National Party, Shadow Minister for Agriculture and Food Security) | Hansard source

The Prime Minister, in answer to my question, indicated either that I misrepresented the facts or that my constituent was in effect not telling the truth. I have it right here. I am willing to table it, if the Prime Minister would like to inspect it.

Ms Anna Burke (Chisholm, Deputy-Speaker) | Hansard source

The member for Calare will resume his seat.
