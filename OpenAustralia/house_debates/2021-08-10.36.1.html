Statements by Members

Stirling Electorate: Surfing WA

1:59 pm

Vince Connelly (Stirling, Liberal Party) | Hansard source

Like so many Aussies, I'm a massive fan of our oceans. My family and I are always surfing, swimming and spearfishing every chance we get. It's a wonderful outcome that the election commitment I made, for $4 million from this Morrison government for a new headquarters, a new home, for Surfing WA, is now going ahead. The sod was turned last week.

Tony Smith (Speaker) | Hansard source

That's a very succinct speech.
