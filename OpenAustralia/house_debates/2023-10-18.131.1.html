House debates

Wednesday, 18 October 2023

Questions to the Speaker

Aluminium

3:26 pm

Dan Tehan (Wannon, Liberal Party, Shadow Minister for Immigration and Citizenship) | Hansard source

I have a question of you, Mr Speaker, where you might be able to assist. During question time, the Minister for Defence Industry misled the House. He said that aluminium rusts. That is just not true. I have an aluminium smelter in my electorate. I think that it would help the House—and I don't want to take it any further than necessary—if he could come back and correct the record that aluminium doesn't actually rust.

Milton Dick (Speaker) | Hansard source

I'll take that as a comment. I'm sure the minister will avail himself of your comments. I thank the member.
