House debates

Wednesday, 10 October 2012

Personal Explanations

3:12 pm

Tony Abbott (Warringah, Liberal Party, Leader of the Opposition) | Hansard source

Madam Speaker, I wish to make a personal explanation.

Ms Anna Burke (Chisholm, Deputy-Speaker) | Hansard source

Does the honourable member claim to have been misrepresented?

Tony Abbott (Warringah, Liberal Party, Leader of the Opposition) | Hansard source

Yes.

Ms Anna Burke (Chisholm, Deputy-Speaker) | Hansard source

Please proceed.

Tony Abbott (Warringah, Liberal Party, Leader of the Opposition) | Hansard source

Madam Speaker, in question time today the Minister for Climate Change and Energy Efficiency accused me of making misleading statements about the impact of the carbon tax. The statement that Whyalla would be wiped off the map was in fact first made by the state Secretary of the Australian Workers' Union , Mr Wayne Hanson. And, as for Gladstone, this very day Queensland Alumina has announced job losses in that city.

Ms Anna Burke (Chisholm, Deputy-Speaker) | Hansard source

The Leader of the Opposition will resume his seat. He needs to demonstrate where he has been misrepresented.
