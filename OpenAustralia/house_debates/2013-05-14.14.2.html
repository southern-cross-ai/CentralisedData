House debates

Tuesday, 14 May 2013

Committees

Membership

1:45 pm

Steve Georganas (Hindmarsh, Australian Labor Party) | Hansard source

Madam Speaker has received advice that members have been nominated to be members of certain committees.

Anthony Albanese (Grayndler, Australian Labor Party, Leader of the House) | Hansard source

by leave—I move:

That Members be appointed as members of certain committees in accordance with the list which has been placed on the table.

As the list is a lengthy one, I do not propose to read the list to the House. Details will be recorded in the Votes and Proceedings .

The list read as follows—

Aboriginal and Torres Strait Islander Affairs— Standing Committee :

Mr Neumann be discharged and that, in his place, Ms Saffin and be appointed a member of the committee;

Climate Change , Environment and the Arts—Standing Committee:

Mr Ciobo be appointed a supplementary member for the purpose of the committee's inquiry into the Australia Council Bill 2013 and the Australia Council (Consequential and Transitional Provisions) Bill 2013;

Cyber-Safety—Joint Select Committee:

Mr Danby be discharged from the committee;

Economics—Standing Committee:

Dr Leigh be discharged and that, in his place, Mr Bowen be appointed a member of the committee;

Education and Employment—Standing Committee:

Ms Rishworth be discharged and that, in her place, Mr Bowen be appointed a member of the committee, and Ms Marino be appointed a supplementary member for the purpose of the committee's inquiry into the Fair Work Amendment Bill 2013;

Electoral Matters—Joint Standing Committee:

Ms Rishworth be discharged and that, in her place, Ms Roxon be appointed a member of the committee;

Foreign Affairs, Defence and Trade—Joint Standing Committee:

Mr Danby be discharged and that, in his place, Mr Marles be appointed a member of the committee;

National Capital and External Territories—Joint Standing Committee:

Dr Leigh be discharged and that, in his place, Mr Crean be appointed a member of the committee;

Procedure—Standing Committee:

Mr Fitzgibbon be discharged from the committee and that, in his place, Mr Hayes be appointed a member of the committee;

Public Accounts and Audit— Joint Committee :

Mr Neumann be discharged and that, in his place, Mr Jenkins be appointed a member of the committee;

Publications Committee:

Mr Hayes and Dr Leigh be discharged and that, in their places, Mrs Elliot and Mr Fitzgibbon be appointed members of the committee;

Selection Committee:

Mr Fitzgibbon, Mr Husic and Ms Saffin be discharged and that, in their places, Mr Hayes, Mr Mitchell and Mr Perrett be appointed members of the committee;

Social Policy and Legal Affairs —Standing Committee:

Mr Neumann be discharged and that, in his place, Mr L. D. T. Ferguson be appointed a member of the committee, and Mrs B. K. Bishop be appointed a supplementary member for the purpose of the committee's inquiry into the Public Interest Disclosure Bill 2013;

Treaties—Joint Standing Committee:

Mrs Elliot be discharged from the and that, in her place, Mr Marles be appointed a member of the committee.

Question agreed to.
