House debates

Wednesday, 10 February 2016

Questions without Notice

Minister for Human Services

2:42 pm

Mark Dreyfus (Isaacs, Australian Labor Party, Shadow Attorney General) | Hansard source

My question is to the Minister for Human Services. I refer to the minister's trip to China and his answer yesterday that he would fully assist with the Parkinson inquiry. Given that it is not clear from his answer yesterday what information he has provided to Dr Parkinson, has the minister provided the Parkinson inquiry with a copy of the letter of appointment he presented to an official of a Chinese state-owned company? Will the minister also provide this letter to the parliament?

Stuart Robert (Fadden, Liberal Party, Minister for Veterans’ Affairs) | Hansard source
