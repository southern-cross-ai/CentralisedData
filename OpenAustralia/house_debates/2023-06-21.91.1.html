House debates

Wednesday, 21 June 2023

Statements

Personal Explanations

3:12 pm

Max Chandler-Mather (Griffith, Australian Greens) | Hansard source

I seek to make a personal explanation.

Milton Dick (Speaker) | Hansard source

Order! Does the member claim to be misrepresented?

Max Chandler-Mather (Griffith, Australian Greens) | Hansard source

I do.

Milton Dick (Speaker) | Hansard source

You may proceed.

Max Chandler-Mather (Griffith, Australian Greens) | Hansard source

The Prime Minister, in question time today, in quoting an article I wrote in Jacobin , said that the article said that 30,000 homes were being opposed. Those words do not at any point appear in the article. I would ask that the Prime Minister come back and withdraw those comments. The words you attributed to me do not appear in the article.

3:13 pm

Anthony Albanese (Grayndler, Australian Labor Party, Prime Minister) | Hansard source

I didn't do that, Mr Speaker. I seek leave to give a personal explanation.

Milton Dick (Speaker) | Hansard source

Does the Prime Minister claim to be misrepresented?

Anthony Albanese (Grayndler, Australian Labor Party, Prime Minister) | Hansard source

I refer to the member for Griffith and the comments that he just made in parliament. I gave direct quotes from his article. There were two direct quotes from his article which went to the campaign that he's running. I'm happy to give him leave to table his article because it exposes the political nature of the opposition to 30,000 public housing units, which this member is engaged with.

Milton Dick (Speaker) | Hansard source

The member for Macnamara will cease interjecting . He's not in his seat. Is the member for Griffith seeking to table a document?

Max Chandler-Mather (Griffith, Australian Greens) | Hansard source

I would love to. I don't have it.

Milton Dick (Speaker) | Hansard source

Resume your seat.

Honourable members interjecting —

Order! The House will come to order!

Opposition members interjecting —

Members on my left! The Prime Minister is seeking the call.
