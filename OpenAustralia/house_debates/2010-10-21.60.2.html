House debates

Thursday, 21 October 2010

Personal Explanations

3:25 pm

Don Randall (Canning, Liberal Party, Shadow Parliamentary Secretary for Local Government) | Hansard source

Mr Speaker, I wish to make a personal explanation.

Harry Jenkins (Speaker) | Hansard source

Does the honourable member claim to have been misrepresented?

Don Randall (Canning, Liberal Party, Shadow Parliamentary Secretary for Local Government) | Hansard source

Yes.

Harry Jenkins (Speaker) | Hansard source

Please proceed.

Don Randall (Canning, Liberal Party, Shadow Parliamentary Secretary for Local Government) | Hansard source

Earlier today I made a statement to the House about comments made at the doors this morning in relation to the banks lifting fees outside of the RBA ’s raising of fees. In doing so, I made comments regarding the fact that it was an idea of a lunatic nature. This was not attributed to anyone but the Greens—to the Greens’ policy—which I have clarified. When the media and the opposition have endeavoured to connect this to the member for North Sydney , it has been clarified that I have said, on the record, that this statement was made in relation to the Greens and that I said of the member for North Sydney that as the minister for financial services he did an excellent job in making sure that the banks were sound institutions.

Harry Jenkins (Speaker) | Hansard source

Order! The member is starting to debate, but I am sure the member for North Sydney is appreciating it.

Don Randall (Canning, Liberal Party, Shadow Parliamentary Secretary for Local Government) | Hansard source

I thank ninemsn for correcting the record online.

Harry Jenkins (Speaker) | Hansard source

The member for Canning has indicated where he was misrepresented.
