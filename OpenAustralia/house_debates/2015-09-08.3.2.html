Tuesday, 8 September 2015

Business

Suspension of Standing and Sessional Orders

12:01 pm

Christopher Pyne (Sturt, Liberal Party, Leader of the House) | Hansard source

I move:

That standing order 133 (deferred divisions on Mondays and Tuesdays) be suspended until 2 pm.

I believe that I have an agreement with the Manager of Opposition Business that that will be voted on without debate, so therefore I will not hold up the House with a terrific oration for 10 minutes about why that should occur. I simply move that that be suspended until 2 pm.

Question agreed to.
