House debates

Wednesday, 23 May 2012

Questions to the Speaker

Member for Dobell

3:22 pm

Tony Abbott (Warringah, Liberal Party, Leader of the Opposition) | Hansard source

) ( ): Madam Deputy Speaker, I have a question for you. Given that the tactics employed by the government are shutting down debate on the statement by the member of Dobell, will you undertake to stop the clock when points of order are taken and gag motions are moved, trying to facilitate proper debate in this House and to allow democracy in this parliament to work?

Ms Anna Burke (Chisholm, Deputy-Speaker) | Hansard source

Order! Whilst I think the answer will be no, I will refer the actual question to the Speaker as it is not within my authority to take questions on administration in the chair at this point of time.
