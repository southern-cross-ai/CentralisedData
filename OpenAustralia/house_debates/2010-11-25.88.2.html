Thursday, 25 November 2010

Questions to the Speaker

Member for Gippsland

3:34 pm

Joel Fitzgibbon (Hunter, Australian Labor Party) | Hansard source

In an action which reminded me of a boxer’s trainer throwing the white towel into the ring, during question time the member for Gippsland left the chamber in much of a hurry and hurled a World Cup scarf into the press gallery. While some people might think I am being a little bit conservative, particularly at this time of year, I would have thought that if the member for Gippsland had some sentiment to extend to the Leader of the Opposition he could have simply passed him a text message.

Harry Jenkins (Speaker) | Hansard source

There was no question in there and based on my—

Don Randall (Canning, Liberal Party, Shadow Parliamentary Secretary for Local Government) | Hansard source

Mr Randall interjecting

Harry Jenkins (Speaker) | Hansard source

The member for Canning is the greatest advocate for the reverse of what he wants to achieve when he does things like that, because I was about to say that, consistent with the way that I have reacted to abuses of the forms of the House, the Chief Government Whip should know much better than to do what he just did.

Opposition Members:

Opposition members interjecting —

Harry Jenkins (Speaker) | Hansard source

I think that I have just explained that if you want to advocate things sit there quietly and let me do my job.
