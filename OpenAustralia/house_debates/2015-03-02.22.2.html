House debates

Monday, 2 March 2015

Committees

Law Enforcement Committee; Membership

11:51 am

Russell Broadbent (McMillan, Liberal Party) | Hansard source

Mr Speaker has received advice from the Chief Government Whip that he has nominated Mr Kelly to be a member of the Parliamentary Joint Committee on Law Enforcement in place of Mr van Manen.

Bob Baldwin (Paterson, Liberal Party, Parliamentary Secretary to the Minister for the Environment) | Hansard source

by leave: I move:

That Mr van Manen be discharged from the Parliamentary Joint Committee on Law Enforcement and that, in his place, Mr Kelly be appointed a member of the committee.

Question agreed to.
