Anthony Albanese (Grayndler, Australian Labor Party, Leader of the House) | Hansard source

I move:

That the House, at its rising, adjourn until 12 noon tomorrow, 17 March 2009, unless the Speaker or, in the event of the Speaker being unavailable, the Deputy Speaker, fixes an alternative day or hour of meeting, and for government business to take precedence from 12 noon until 2 p.m. on that day.
