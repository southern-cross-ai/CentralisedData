House debates

Wednesday, 26 June 2013

Personal Explanations

3:30 pm

Graham Perrett (Moreton, Australian Labor Party) | Hansard source

Speaker, I wish to correct the record.

Ms Anna Burke (Speaker) | Hansard source

Does the honourable member claim to have been misrepresented?

Graham Perrett (Moreton, Australian Labor Party) | Hansard source

Yes, most grievously and with malice aforethought.

Ms Anna Burke (Speaker) | Hansard source

Please proceed.

Graham Perrett (Moreton, Australian Labor Party) | Hansard source

Today the parliamentary netball side played the press gallery. I am not sure what the collective noun is for journalists—a coven, a gaggle, a murder, or whatever—

Ms Anna Burke (Speaker) | Hansard source

The member for Moreton will get to his point.

Graham Perrett (Moreton, Australian Labor Party) | Hansard source

James Massola, Jacqueline Maley, Eliza Borrello, Lauren Gianoli, Leah Craven, Brihony Speed and many others have incorrectly tweeted that they won the netball game. But, as my colleague would correctly attest, we won the game three points to two.

Ms Anna Burke (Speaker) | Hansard source

I thank the member for Moreton for correcting the record.
