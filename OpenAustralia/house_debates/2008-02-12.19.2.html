Tuesday, 12 February 2008

Ministerial Arrangements

3:51 pm

Kevin Rudd (Griffith, Australian Labor Party, Prime Minister) | Hansard source

Mr Speaker, I have the honour to inform the House that, following the election held on 24 November 2007, the Governor-General commissioned me to form a government. Ministers and parliamentary secretaries were appointed on 3 December 2007. For the information of honourable members, I present a list of the full ministry. The document lists all ministers and parliamentary secretaries and the offices they hold. It shows those ministers who comprise the cabinet and provides details of representation arrangements in each chamber. I understand the document will be included in the Votes and Proceedings and Hansard .
