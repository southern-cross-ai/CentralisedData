House debates

Tuesday, 27 October 2009

Questions without Notice

Asylum Seekers

2:25 pm

Sussan Ley (Farrer, Liberal Party, Shadow Minister for Justice and Customs) | Hansard source

My question is to the Prime Minister. Will the Prime Minister inform the House who is making decisions regarding the Australian Customs vessel Oceanic Viking ? Who is issuing instructions to the Australian personnel on board?

Kevin Rudd (Griffith, Australian Labor Party, Prime Minister) | Hansard source

I thank the honourable member for her question. I would assume that, consistent with our normal arrangements, that would be done through the Customs agency of the Australian government.

Opposition Members:

Opposition members interjecting —

Kevin Rudd (Griffith, Australian Labor Party, Prime Minister) | Hansard source

That would be done through the normal agency of the Australian government—the customs and border protection arrangements of the government. That is where the command and control lies. I imagine it is an operational matter. That is where it is being processed.
