Tuesday, 25 August 2020

Questions to the Speaker

COVID 19: Western Australian Border Closure

3:16 pm

Anthony Albanese (Grayndler, Australian Labor Party, Leader of the Opposition) | Hansard source

Mr Speaker, I have a question, through you. While question time has been on, the Federal Court have handed down their judgement on WA border restrictions. They refuse to order a new trial, upon the application of the WA government, based upon—

Tony Smith (Speaker) | Hansard source

Leader of the Opposition—

Honourable Member :

An honourable member interjecting —

Tony Smith (Speaker) | Hansard source

I don't need any interjections. I think I could sit here for a long time and get to the same conclusion I'm at: this has got nothing to do with me.

Anthony Albanese (Grayndler, Australian Labor Party, Leader of the Opposition) | Hansard source

It has, to the extent that I'm saying that the Attorney-General might want to give a ministerial statement, either today or tomorrow, on the outcome of the WA—

Tony Smith (Speaker) | Hansard source

No, the Leader of the Opposition will need to resume his seat. The Leader of the Opposition is raising a matter that could really only have been raised in question time, and the Prime Minister has asked that further questions be placed on the Notice Paper . The Leader of the Opposition could do that or ask a question about it tomorrow.
