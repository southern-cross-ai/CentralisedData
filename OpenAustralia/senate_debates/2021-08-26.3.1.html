Senate debates

Thursday, 26 August 2021

Business

Consideration of Legislation

9:31 am

Michaelia Cash (Liberal Party, Deputy Leader of the Government in the Senate) | Hansard source

by leave—I move:

That—

(a) if consideration of the following bills has not concluded by 11.15 am, the questions on all remaining stages be put without debate:

i. Electoral Legislation Amendment (Counting, Scrutiny and Operational Efficiencies) Bill 2021,

ii. Electoral Legislation Amendment (Party Registration Integrity) Bill 2021, and

iii. Electoral Legislation Amendment (Electoral Offences and Preventing Multiple Voting) Bill 2021;

(b) paragraph (a) operate as a limitation of debate under standing order 142;

(c) the routine of business from 3.30 pm be consideration of the following bills:

i. Foreign Intelligence Legislation Amendment Bill 2021,

ii. Australian Organ and Tissue Donation and Transplantation Authority Amendment (Governance and Other Measures) Bill 2021,

iii. Customs Amendment (2022 Harmonized System Changes) Bill 2021, and Customs Tariff Amendment (2022 Harmonized System Changes) Bill 2021;

(d) if consideration of the bills in paragraph (c) has not concluded by 4.30 pm, the questions on all remaining stages be put without debate;

(e) paragraph (d) operate as a limitation of debate under standing order 142;

(f) divisions may take place after 4.30 pm for the purposes of the bills listed in paragraph (c) only; and

(g) the Senate adjourn without debate after consideration of the bills has concluded, or on the motion of a minister, whichever is earlier.

And I also move:

That the question be now put.

Scott Ryan (President) | Hansard source

The question is that the question be now put.

Rachel Siewert (Australian Greens) | Hansard source

Scott Ryan (President) | Hansard source

Question agreed to.
