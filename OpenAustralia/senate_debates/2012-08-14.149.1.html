(2) Subregulation 18(a) of the Parliamentary Entitlements Regulations 1997 (the Regulations) provides that the Attorney-General must:

(a) inform the Parliament of each decision to pay assistance under this Part [Part 3 of the Regulations], including reasons for the decision and any limits on expenditure. In order to comply with subregulation 18(a) a statement is tabled in both houses of Parliament when the Attorney-General approves a grant of assistance. A statement was tabled in Parliament for all of the grants approved in 2009-10, 2010-11 and 2011-12.
