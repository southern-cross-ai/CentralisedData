Senate debates

Monday, 9 February 2015

Parliamentary Representation

New South Wales

10:02 am

Stephen Parry (President) | Hansard source

I inform the Senate that I have received a letter from Senator Faulkner, resigning his place as a senator for the state of New South Wales. Pursuant to the provisions of section 21 of the Constitution, I have notified the Governor of New South Wales of the vacancy in the representation of that state caused by the resignation. I table the letter and a copy of my letter to the Governor of New South Wales.
