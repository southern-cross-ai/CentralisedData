Senate debates

Wednesday, 18 November 2009

Fair Work Amendment (State Referrals and Other Measures) Legislation

Order

4:00 pm

Mary Fisher (Liberal Party) | Hansard source

I move:

(1)
That there be laid on the table by the Minister representing the Minister for Education, Employment and Workplace Relations, no later than 3 pm on Thursday, 19 November 2009, copies of any bilateral intergovernmental agreement entered into, or final text agreed, between the Commonwealth Government and any referring state government or any territory government, in relation to the Fair Work Amendment (State Referrals and Other Measures) Bill 2009.
(2)
That a copy of the final text of each such agreement proposed to be entered into between the Commonwealth Government and any referring state government or any territory government on or after 3 pm on Thursday, 19 November 2009, be laid on the table within 24 hours of the final text being agreed.
(3)
That a copy of each such signed agreement entered into between the Commonwealth Government and any referring state government or any territory government on or after 3 pm on Thursday, 19 November 2009, be laid on the table within 24 hours of the agreement being signed.

Question agreed to.
