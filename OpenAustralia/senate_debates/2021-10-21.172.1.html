Thursday, 21 October 2021

Committees

Public Accounts and Audit Committee, Joint Standing Committee on Foreign Affairs, Defence and Trade; Government Response to Report

6:26 pm

Richard Colbeck (Liberal Party, Minister for Senior Australians and Aged Care Services) | Hansard source

I present two government responses to the following committee report: the 484th report of the Joint Committee on Public Accounts and Audit, relating to administration of government grants; and the Joint Standing Committee on Foreign Affairs, Defence and Trade report on Australia's defence relationships with Pacific island nations. In accordance with the usual practice, I seek leave to have the documents incorporated in Hansard .

Leave granted.

The documents read as follows—

1. The committee met in private session on Wednesday, 20 October 2021 at 7.20 pm.

2. The committee recommends that—

(a) the Aboriginal Land Rights ( Northern Territory ) Amendment (Economic Empowerment) Bill 2021 be referred immediately to the Finance and Public Administration Legislation Committee for inquiry and report by 25 November 2021 (see appendix 1 for a statement of reasons for referral);

(b) the provisions of the Aged Care and Other Legislation Amendment (Royal Commission Response No. 2) Bill 2021 be referred immediately to the Community Affairs Legislation Committee for inquiry and report by 16 November 2021 (see appendix 2 for a statement of reasons for referral);

(c) the provisions of the Corporations Amendment (Meetings and Documents) Bill 2021 be referred immediately to the Economics Legislation Committee for inquiry and report by 18 November 2021 (see appendix 3 for a statement of reasons for referral);

(d) the provisions of the Offshore Petroleum (Laminaria and Corallina Decommissioning Cost Recovery Levy) Bill 2021Treasury Laws Amendment (Laminaria and Corallina Decommissioning Cost Recovery Levy) Bill 2021 be referred immediately to the Economics Legislation Committee for inquiry and report by 18 November 2021 (see appendix 4 for a statement of reasons for referral); and

(e) the provisions of the Telstra Corporation and Other Legislation Amendment Bill 2021 be referred immediately to the Environment and Communications Legislation Committee for inquiry and report by 18 November 2021 (see appendix 5 for a statement of reasons for referral).
