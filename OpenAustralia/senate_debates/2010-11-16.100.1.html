NBN Co Limited—Report for 2009-10. Motion to take note of document moved by Senator Williams. Debate adjourned till Thursday at general business, Senator Williams in continuation.

Australian Law Reform Commission—Report No. 114—Family violence – A national legal response—

Volume 1, dated October 2010.

Volume 2, dated October 2010.

Motion to take note of documents moved by Senator Williams. Debate adjourned till Thursday at general business, Senator Williams in continuation.
