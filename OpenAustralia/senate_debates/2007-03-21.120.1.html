Senate debates

Wednesday, 21 March 2007

Sydney Harbour Bridge

3:40 pm

Steve Hutchins (Australian Labor Party) | Hansard source

I, and also on behalf of Senators Faulkner, George Campbell , Stephens and Foreshaw, move:

That the Senate notes:
(a)   the 75th anniversary of the opening of the Sydney Harbour Bridge ;
(b)   the efforts of the estimated 4 000-strong unionised workforce which contributed to the construction of the bridge over a period of 10 years from 1922 to 1932, whose legacy is now an icon around the world;
(c)   the significant role unions played in securing fair wages and working conditions for bridge workers; and
(d)   the 16 workers who lost their lives during construction of the bridge, Sydney Edward Addison, Francis Chilvers, Alfred Edmunds, Percy Poole, James Campbell , Robert Craig , Alexander Faulkner, Thomas McKeown , August Peterson, Nathanial Swandells, Henry Waters, Henry Webb , William Woods , Frederick Gillon, Robert Graham and Edward Shirley.

Question agreed to.
