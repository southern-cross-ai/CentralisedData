Senate debates

Tuesday, 20 June 2023

Questions without Notice

Ministerial Standards

1:59 pm

Jonathon Duniam (Liberal Party, Shadow Minister for Environment, Fisheries and Forestry) | Hansard source

My question is to the Minister for Finance, Senator Gallagher. Minister, in response to a question regarding whether a brief or any form of advice was ever provided to you or to your office on the claim or proposed settlement as envisaged by guidance note 7, you said:

From my memory, I did not receive a brief or any advice from my department. I think it was quite detailed in terms of any other advice that may have come to the office. I would just want the opportunity to check that.

Minister, have you checked whether you or your office received such a brief?

2:00 pm

Katy Gallagher (Australian Labor Party, Minister for the Public Service) | Hansard source

In terms of whether there was a brief or any form of advice provided to me or my office on the claim or the proposed settlement and as outlined, I think, by guidance note 7, I think was Senator Cash's question, I can confirm that the answer to the senator's question is no.

Sue Lines (President) | Hansard source

Senator Duniam, a first supplementary?

Jonathon Duniam (Liberal Party, Shadow Minister for Environment, Fisheries and Forestry) | Hansard source

In response to a question regarding whether you ever declared a conflict in relation to any aspect of the matter, including the proposed settlement, or if you ever sought advice in relation to a potential conflict, you said:

If it had come to me, I would have declared a conflict, but it didn't. It didn't cross my desk. I did not get a brief. If there is anything further that I can update the Senate about on that, I will.

Minister, is there anything further you can update the Senate on in relation to this issue?

2:01 pm

Katy Gallagher (Australian Labor Party, Minister for the Public Service) | Hansard source

No. I refer you to the answer I gave last week and the answer I've just given now. I did not receive any brief, including as envisaged by guidance note 7, so I had no conflict to declare.
