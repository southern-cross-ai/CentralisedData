Senate debates

Thursday, 16 May 2024

Documents

Attorney-General's Department, National Indigenous Australians Agency; Order for the Production of Documents

12:06 pm

Lidia Thorpe (Independent) | Hansard source

I seek leave to amend general business notice motions No. 519 and No. 520 in my name for today for the production of documents. I'd like to change the reporting date from 16 May to 17 May.

Leave granted.

I move the motions, as amended, together:

GENERAL BUSINESS NO TICE OF MOTION NO. 519

That there be laid on the table by the Minister representing the Attorney- General , by no later than midday on Friday, 17 May 2024, all documents and briefing materials prepared by the Attorney-General's Department for additional estimates in February 2024.

GENERAL BUSINESS NOTICE OF MOTION NO. 520

That there be laid on the table by the Minister representing the Minister for Indigenous Australians , by no later than midday on Friday, 17 May 2024, all documents and briefing materials prepared by the National Indigenous Australians Agency for additional estimates in February 2024.

Question agreed to.
