Senate debates

Thursday, 11 May 2023

Parliamentary Zone

Proposed Works

12:01 pm

Katy Gallagher (Australian Labor Party, Minister for the Public Service) | Hansard source

I ask that government business No. 2, standing in the name of Senator Chisholm, proposing the approval of works within the parliamentary zone, be taken as a formal motion.

Sue Lines (President) | Hansard source

Is there any objection to this motion being taken as formal?

Pauline Hanson (Pauline Hanson's One Nation Party) | Hansard source

Yes.

Sue Lines (President) | Hansard source

There is an objection.

Katy Gallagher (Australian Labor Party, Minister for the Public Service) | Hansard source

Pursuant to contingent notice, I move:

That so much of the standing orders be suspended as would prevent the motion being moved immediately and determined without amendment or debate.

Question agreed to.

I move:

That, in accordance with section 5 of the Parliament Act 1974 , the Senate approves the proposal by the National Capital Authority for capital works within the Parliamentary Zone relating to the John Gorton campus carpark.

Sue Lines (President) | Hansard source

The question is that government business No. 2 as moved by Senator Gallagher, standing in the name of Senator Chisolm, be agreed to.
