Committees

Privileges Committee; Report

10:17 am

Alan Ferguson (President) | Hansard source

As indicated at item 7 of today’s Order of Business, and with the concurrence of the Senate, I shall call on order of the day No. 6 relating to committee reports, which relates to the 131st report of the Committee of Privileges . There being no objection, it is so ordered.

The question is:

That the Senate:

(a)   agree to the recommendation at paragraph 40; and
(b)   endorse the finding at paragraph 41,

of the 131st report of the Committee of Privileges.

Question agreed to.
