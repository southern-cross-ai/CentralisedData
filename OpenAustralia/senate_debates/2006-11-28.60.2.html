Tuesday, 28 November 2006

Avoiding Dangerous Climate Change (Kyoto Protocol Ratification) Bill 2006 [No. 2]

First Reading

3:38 pm

Kim Carr (Australian Labor Party, Shadow Minister for Housing and Urban Development) | Hansard source

I move:

That the following bill be introduced: A Bill for an Act to ratify the Kyoto Protocol to the United Nations Framework Convention on Climate Change , and for related purposes.

Question agreed to.

I move:

That this bill may proceed without formalities and be now read a first time.

Question agreed to.

Bill read a first time.
