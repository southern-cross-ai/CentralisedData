Senate debates

Tuesday, 30 July 2019

Documents

Welfare Reform; Order for the Production of Documents

3:57 pm

Rachel Siewert (Australian Greens) | Hansard source

I move:

That there be laid on the table by the Minister for Families and Social Services , by 1 August 2019, a list of the people and organisations who form part of the Cashless Debit Card community reference groups, in each of the Cashless Debit Card trial sites.

Jonathon Duniam (Liberal Party, Assistant Minister for Forestry and Fisheries) | Hansard source

I seek leave to make a short statement.

Scott Ryan (President) | Hansard source

Leave is granted for one minute.

Jonathon Duniam (Liberal Party, Assistant Minister for Forestry and Fisheries) | Hansard source

Releasing a list of the people and organisations who form the CDC community reference groups may breach their privacy and place undue community pressure on individuals.

Scott Ryan (President) | Hansard source

The question is that motion No. 60 be agreed to.
