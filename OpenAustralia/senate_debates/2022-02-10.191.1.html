Senate debates

Thursday, 10 February 2022

Bills

Treasury Laws Amendment (Enhancing Superannuation Outcomes For Australians and Helping Australian Businesses Invest) Bill 2021; First Reading

5:36 pm

Simon Birmingham (Liberal Party, Minister for Finance) | Hansard source

I move:

That this bill be now read a first time.

Question agreed to.

Bill read a first time.

Slade Brockman (President) | Hansard source

I will now deal with the amendments circulated by the Australian Greens on sheet 1521. The question is that schedules 2, 3 and 4 stand as printed.

The Australian Greens oppose d schedules 2, 3 and 4 in the following terms—

(3) Schedule 2, page 4 (lines 1 to 10), to be opposed.

(4) Schedule 3, page 5 (lines 1 to 9), to be opposed.

(5) Schedule 4, page 6 (line 1) to page 7 (line 12), to be opposed.

Question agreed to.

5:37 pm

Nick McKim (Australian Greens) | Hansard source

by leave—In the interests of time, instead of calling a division—and I acknowledge the encouragement of the Chief Government Whip!—I simply ask the Australian Greens ' position opposing the schedules be recorded.

Jacqui Lambie (Jacqui Lambie Network) | Hansard source

by leave—I record my position as against.

Slade Brockman (President) | Hansard source

The question now is that amendments (1) and (2) on sheet 1521 be agreed to.

Australian Greens ' circulated amendments

(1) Clause 2, page 2 (table item 2), omit "Schedules 1 and 2", substitute "Schedule 1".

(2) Clause 2, page 2 (table item 3), omit "Schedules 3 to 6", substitute "Schedules 5 and 6".

Question negatived.

Nick McKim (Australian Greens) | Hansard source

by leave—I request that the Australian Greens ' position in favour of these amendments be recorded.

Jacqui Lambie (Jacqui Lambie Network) | Hansard source

by leave—I request my position in favour of these amendments be recorded.
