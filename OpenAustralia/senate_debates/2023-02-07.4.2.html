Senate debates

Tuesday, 7 February 2023

Parliamentary Representation

Thorpe, Senator Lidia

12:02 pm

Lidia Thorpe (Australian Greens) | Hansard source

President, I seek leave to make a one-minute statement about my new status as an Independent.

Leave granted.

I inform the Senate that yesterday I resigned as a member of the Australian Greens and will sit as an Independent senator for Victoria. I also inform the Senate that I should be designated as a whip for the purpose of standing order 24A relating to the selection of bills committee.
