Senate debates

Monday, 6 March 2023

Questions without Notice: Take Note of Answers

COVID-19: Vaccination

3:38 pm

Andrew McLachlan (Deputy-President) | Hansard source

NT (): Senator Roberts, there's 30 seconds remaining that hasn't been used. Would you like to move a motion to take note?

Malcolm Roberts (Pauline Hanson's One Nation Party) | Hansard source

Yes, I would, Chair. I move:

That the Senate take note of the answer given by the Minister for Finance (Senator Gallagher) to a question without notice asked by Senator Roberts today relating to COVID-19 vaccinations.

The Labor Party thinks it's easy to just fob off this debate about broken political promises. This is about something far bigger. This is about the future of our country and this very parliament. We have got people dying in their thousands from the injections, and it has not been approved by United States authorities and it has bypassed by the Department of Defence . What we want is a proper, fair royal commission into the COVID injections and the whole treatment of COVID.

Question agreed to.
