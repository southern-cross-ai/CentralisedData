Senate debates

Thursday, 8 September 2022

Answers to Questions on Notice

Question Nos 99 and 100

3:15 pm

James Paterson (Liberal Party, Shadow Minister for Cyber Security) | Hansard source

I seek an explanation from the minister representing the Minister for Home Affairs , Senator Watt, of the minister's failure to answer questions on notice 99 and 100, which are now overdue. In doing so, I acknowledge I gave him only about 10 minutes notice of my intention to do so, so I will not be surprised if he doesn't have the answers on him. I seek his assistance in achieving a timely resolution to these questions.

Murray Watt (Australian Labor Party, Minister for Agriculture, Fisheries and Forestry) | Hansard source

I thank Senator Paterson for the heads-up on this. I will, of course, seek an explanation from the minister and respond to the chamber.
