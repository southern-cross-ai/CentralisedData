Senate debates

Tuesday, 13 June 2006

Committees

Public Accounts and Audit Committee; Meeting

4:34 pm

Nigel Scullion (Country Liberal Party) | Hansard source

by leave—On behalf of the Joint Committee of Public Accounts and Audit, I move:

That the Joint Committee of Public Accounts and Audit be authorised to hold a public meeting during the sitting of the Senate on Wednesday, 14 June 2006, from 11.30 am to 12.30 pm, to take evidence for the committee’s review of Auditor-General’s reports.

Question agreed to.
