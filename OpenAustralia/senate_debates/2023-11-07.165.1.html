Anthony Chisholm (Australian Labor Party, Assistant Minister for Education) | Hansard source

The government cannot agree to the date set out in this motion. As we have said previously, we intend to publicly release the NDIS review report following the next meeting of the National Cabinet. If necessary, this will be done out of session before the first sitting day of 2024, as was set out in our amendment.
