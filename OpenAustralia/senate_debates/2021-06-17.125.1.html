Bills

Higher Education Support Amendment (Extending the Student Loan Fee Exemption) Bill 2021; Second Reading

12:54 pm

Carol Brown (Australian Labor Party, Shadow Assistant Minister for Infrastructure and Regional Tourism) | Hansard source

Labor will support the Higher Education Support Amendment (Extending the Student Loan Fee Exemption) Bill 2021. This legislation makes sensible amendments to extend the extension of the FEE - HELP loan fee. Under the current arrangements, students who require financial assistance through FEE-HELP to study at a private, non-university, higher-education provider must pay a 20 per cent loan fee on top of the loan, and of course Labor supports this bill because the benefits of this exemption flow to the students.

12:55 pm

Jonathon Duniam (Liberal Party, Assistant Minister for Forestry and Fisheries) | Hansard source

I commend the bill to the Senate.

Question agreed to.

Bill read a second time.
