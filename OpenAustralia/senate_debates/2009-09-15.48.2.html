Senate debates

Tuesday, 15 September 2009

Leave of Absence

3:38 pm

Bob Brown (Australian Greens) | Hansard source

I move:

That leave of absence be granted to Senator Wong from 15 to 18 September 2009.

Question agreed to.

I rise on a point of order. Deputy President , it would be very helpful to the Senate if, in moving such a motion as the minister just did, a reason were given for the absence of an important minister during that period.

It is not a point of order, Senator Brown. I cannot instruct them how to write their motions, or in what order the motions ought to be.

3:41 pm

Joe Ludwig (Australian Labor Party, Manager of Government Business in the Senate) | Hansard source

I seek leave to make a short statement.

Alan Ferguson (Deputy-President) | Hansard source

Leave is granted for two minutes.

Joe Ludwig (Australian Labor Party, Manager of Government Business in the Senate) | Hansard source

As I understand it the absence relates to parliamentary business. If I can seek further information from the minister I will convey your inquiry to her. That may help.
