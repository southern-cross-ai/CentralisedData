Senate debates

Thursday, 14 November 2013

Questions without Notice

Asylum Seekers

2:17 pm

Kate Lundy (Australian Labor Party) | Hansard source

My question is to the Assistant Minister for Immigration and Border Protection. Can the minister advise the Senate of the current capacity of the Blaydin Point Detention Centre in Darwin? Has the government made any decision to increase capacity of this centre?

2:18 pm

Michaelia Cash (Liberal Party, Assistant Minister for Immigration and Border Protection) | Hansard source

In relation to that specific question, I do not have the information on me but I will take it on notice and I will provide the senator with an answer.

Kate Lundy (Australian Labor Party) | Hansard source

Mr President , I ask a supplementary question. Thank you. Can the minister advise whether any detainees have been transferred from Christmas Island to the Blaydin facility since 7 September?

Michaelia Cash (Liberal Party, Assistant Minister for Immigration and Border Protection) | Hansard source

Again, I thank the senator for her question. In relation to operational matters, this government has made it very, very clear that we will not be commenting on operational matters.
