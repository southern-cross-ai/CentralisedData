Aviation Transport Security Amendment (Cargo) Bill 2015; Third Reading

1:04 pm

John Williams (National Party) | Hansard source

As no amendments to the bill have been circulated, I shall call the minister to move the third reading unless any senator requires that the bill be considered in Committee of the Whole .

Mitch Fifield (Liberal Party, Manager of Government Business in the Senate) | Hansard source

That this bill be now read a third time.

Catryna Bilyk (Australian Labor Party) | Hansard source

Oh please no!

Mitch Fifield (Liberal Party, Manager of Government Business in the Senate) | Hansard source

I see him shaking his head, but I do note that it was covered in the UK media, so it has had some wide coverage.

Question agreed to.

Bill read a third time.
