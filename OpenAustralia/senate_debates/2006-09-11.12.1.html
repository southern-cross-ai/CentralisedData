Senate debates

Monday, 11 September 2006

Distinguished Visitors

Paul Calvert (President) | Hansard source

Order! Before I call Senator Ferguson, I would like to draw the attention of honourable senators to the presence in the President’s Gallery of Lord Falconer of Thoroton, PC , Lord Chancellor and Secretary of State for Constitutional Affairs in the British government. I also note the presence of a parliamentary delegation from the National People’s Congress of China led by Mr Liu Zhenhua, Vice-Chairman of the Committee for Internal and Judicial Affairs. I welcome all of our visitors today to the Senate and particularly to Australia.

Honourable Senators:

Hear, hear!
