Senate debates

Thursday, 15 February 2018

Questions without Notice

Deputy Prime Minister

2:55 pm

Doug Cameron (Australian Labor Party, Shadow Minister for Human Services) | Hansard source

My question is to the Minister for Jobs and Innovation, Senator Cash. Given the minister has since 20 September 2017 been responsible for the Department of Industry , Innovation and Science, either in an acting capacity or an ongoing capacity, is the minister aware of any request of the secretary or any other senior officials in that department to appoint Deputy Prime Minister Joyce's former media adviser to a departmental role?

Michaelia Cash (Liberal Party, Minister for Jobs and Innovation) | Hansard source

The answer to the question is no.

Scott Ryan (President) | Hansard source

Senator Cameron, a supplementary question.

Doug Cameron (Australian Labor Party, Shadow Minister for Human Services) | Hansard source

Is the minister or her office aware of any approach to the secretary or any other senior officials in any other government agency regarding the appointment of Deputy Prime Minister Joyce's former media adviser to a public service role?

2:56 pm

Michaelia Cash (Liberal Party, Minister for Jobs and Innovation) | Hansard source

The answer in relation to myself is no; in relation to my office, not to my knowledge.

Doug Cameron (Australian Labor Party, Shadow Minister for Human Services) | Hansard source

Can the minister guarantee to the Senate that neither she nor her office is aware of any attempt to secure a job for Deputy Prime Minister Joyce's former media adviser in Geoscience Australia or any other government agency?

Michaelia Cash (Liberal Party, Minister for Jobs and Innovation) | Hansard source

In relation to myself, yes; in relation to my office, I'll take it on notice.
