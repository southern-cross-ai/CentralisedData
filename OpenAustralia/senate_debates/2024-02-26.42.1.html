Tasmania State Election

1:47 pm

Peter Whish-Wilson (Australian Greens) | Hansard source

The Tasmanian people are about to go back to the polls on 23 March after three years of unstable majority Liberal government. I want to address the elephant in the room here today. He's back! Yes, a vote for Jeremy Rockliff , the Premier of Tasmania , is a vote for Eric Abetz . A vote for this guy is really a vote for this guy.

Matt O'Sullivan (Liberal Party) | Hansard source

Senator Whish-Wilson, please take your seat. Props are not allowed. I warn you of that. Please continue.

What will that look like for the Tasmanian Liberal Party after the election?
