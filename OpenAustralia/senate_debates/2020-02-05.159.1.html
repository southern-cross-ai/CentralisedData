Australia Day Honours

4:23 pm

Dean Smith (Liberal Party) | Hansard source

(ix) Medal of the Order of Australia (OAM (M)): Warrant Officer Anthony O'Riley;

(x) Conspicuous Service Cross ( CSC ): Captain Daniel LeRaye; and

(b) acknowledges that these deserving Western Australians have made a significant contribution to their communities and ultimately Australia.

Question agreed to.
