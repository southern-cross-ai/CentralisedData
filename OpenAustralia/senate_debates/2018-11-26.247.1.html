Monday, 26 November 2018

Bills

Aboriginal Land Rights (Northern Territory) Amendment Bill 2017; In Committee

8:31 pm

Anne Ruston (Liberal Party, Assistant Minister for International Development and the Pacific) | Hansard source

by leave—I table a supplementary memorandum relating to the government amendments to this bill and move government amendments (1) and (2) on sheet EK 113:

(1) Clause 2, page 2 (table item 3, column 1), omit "Schedule 2", substitute "Schedules 2 and 3".

(2) Page 6 (after line 10), at the end of the Bill, add:

Schedule 3— Anthony Lagoon

Aboriginal Land Rights ( Northern Territory ) Act 1976

1 Part 4 of Schedule 1 (before the item relating to Bauhinia Downs)

Insert:

ANTHONY LAGOON

All that land in the Northern Territory comprising the whole of Northern Territory Portion 5010 delineated on Survey Plan S96/303 lodged with the Surveyor-General, Darwin, and containing an area of approximately 603.5 hectares.

Question agreed to.

Bill, as amended, agreed to.

Bill reported with amendments; report adopted.
