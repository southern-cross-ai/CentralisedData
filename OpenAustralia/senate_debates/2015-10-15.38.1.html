Senate debates

Thursday, 15 October 2015

Documents

Nous Group; Order for the Production of Documents

12:14 pm

Kim Carr (Australian Labor Party, Shadow Minister Assisting the Leader for Science) | Hansard source

I, and also on behalf of Senators Lambie, Lazarus and Siewert, move:

That there be laid on the table by the Minister for Education and Training, no later than 3.30 pm on Thursday, 15 October 2015, the following:

(a) a redacted copy of any reports delivered under the Nous Group contract, ‘Assessment of Stakeholder Views—Higher Education’ listed on the Austender website (contract number CN 3277481), omitting any reference to the views expressed by individual senators or their staff; and

(b) all other documentation related to the contract, including the contract itself and any correspondence between the Nous Group and the Minister or his department, omitting any reference to the views expressed by individual senators or their staff.

Gavin Marshall (Deputy-President) | Hansard source

The question is that general business notice of motion No. 907 be agreed to.
