Senate debates

Monday, 25 October 2010

Condolences

  • Hon. Kenneth Shaw Wriedt (9 speeches)
    The PRESIDENT (3.32 pm)—It is with deep regret that I inform the Senate of the death on 18 October 2010 of the Hon. Kenneth Shaw Wriedt, a senator for Tasmania from 1968 to 1980.
  • Dr Gareth Clayton (1 speech)
    It is with deep regret that I inform the Senate of the death on 1 July 2010, of Dr Gareth Clayton a member of the House of Representatives for the division of Isaacs, Victoria, from 1974 to 1975.
