Senate debates

Monday, 7 November 2016

Motions

United Nations Framework on the Convention of Climate Change

3:55 pm

Larissa Waters (Australian Greens) | Hansard source

At the request of Senator Di Natale, I move:

That there be laid on the table, by no later than 1 December 2016, by the Minister representing the Minister for Foreign Affairs, the modelling referred to by New Zealand in the United Nations Framework on the Convention of Climate Change session SB 145(2016) that details the Government's emission and removals projections for 2030.

3:56 pm

James McGrath (Liberal National Party, Assistant Minister to the Prime Minister) | Hansard source

I seek leave to make a short statement.

Gavin Marshall (Deputy-President) | Hansard source

Leave is granted for one minute.

James McGrath (Liberal National Party, Assistant Minister to the Prime Minister) | Hansard source

Australia's most recent projections update published in April this year show that Australia is on track to meet and beat our 2020 target by 78 million tonnes of carbon dioxide equivalent. The Department of the Environment and Energy is currently preparing updated projections of Australia's greenhouse gas emissions. Once finalised, they will be made public in the usual way.

Question agreed to.
