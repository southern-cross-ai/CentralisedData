Senate debates

Monday, 13 August 2007

Committees

Australian Crime Commission Committee; Meeting

3:39 pm

Stephen Parry (Liberal Party) | Hansard source

by leave—At the request of the Chair of the Parliamentary Joint Committee on the Australian Crime Commission , Senator Ian Macdonald, I move:

That the Parliamentary Joint Committee on the Australian Crime Commission be authorised to hold a public meeting during the sitting of the Senate today, from 5.30 pm, to take evidence for the committee’s inquiry into the future impact of serious and organised crime on Australian society.

Question agreed to.
