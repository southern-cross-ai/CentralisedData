Senate debates

Thursday, 11 March 2010

National Broadband Network

Order

9:39 am

Scott Ludlam (Australian Greens) | Hansard source

I move:

That there be laid on the table by the Minister for Broadband, Communications and the Digital Economy , no later than 10 am on Wednesday, 17 March 2010, a copy of the National Broadband Network Implementation Study.

Stephen Parry (Liberal Party) | Hansard source

by leave—I move:

That the words ‘and government response’ be added to the end of the motion.

Question agreed to.

Kerry O'Brien (Australian Labor Party) | Hansard source

by leave—The government opposes this motion on the understanding that it clearly has support of both the opposition and the Greens and therefore a majority of the chamber. We will not be calling a division.

Original question, as amended, agreed to.
