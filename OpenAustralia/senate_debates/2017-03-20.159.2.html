Senate debates

Notices

Postponement

5:14 pm

David Fawcett (Liberal Party) | Hansard source

by leave—I move:

That business of the Senate order of the day No. 1 relating to the presentation of the report of the Legal and Constitutional Affairs Legislation Committee on the provision of the Native Title Amendment (Indigenous Land Use Agreements) Bill 2017 be postponed until a later hour.

As the Clerk is suggesting, I will give an explanation here: the report has not physically been tabled, which is why it has to occur at a later hour. There is no skulduggery occurring there.

Peter Whish-Wilson (Australian Greens) | Hansard source

It is on its way; I understand.

Question agreed to.
