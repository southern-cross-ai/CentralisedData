Senate debates

Tuesday, 27 February 2007

Documents

Consideration

The following orders of the day relating to government documents were considered:

Wheat Export Authority—Report for 1 October 2005 to 30 September 2006. Motion to take note of document moved by Senator Moore. Debate adjourned till Thursday at general business, Senator Moore in continuation.

Migration Act 1958 Section 440A—Conduct of Refugee Review Tribunal (RRT) reviews not completed within 90 days—Report for the period 1 July to 31 October 2006. Motion to take note of document moved by Senator Moore. Debate adjourned till Thursday at general business, Senator Moore in continuation.

Surveillance Devices Act 2004 Report for 2005-06 on the operation of the Act. Motion to take note of document moved by Senator Moore. Debate adjourned till Thursday at general business, Senator Moore in continuation.

General business order of the day no. 10 relating to government documents was called on but no motion was moved.
