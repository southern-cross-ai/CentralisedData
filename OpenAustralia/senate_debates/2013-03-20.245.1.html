Senate debates

Wednesday, 20 March 2013

Committees

Environment and Communications References Committee; Report

9:16 pm

Simon Birmingham (Liberal Party, Shadow Parliamentary Secretary for the Murray Darling Basin) | Hansard source

I present the Environment and Communications References Committee's report The Australian Broadcasting Corporation's commitment to reflecting and representing regional diversity , together with the Hansard record of proceedings and documents presented to the committee.

Ordered that the report be printed.

I seek leave to move a motion in relation to the report. Leave granted.

I move:

That the Senate take note of the report.

I commend the contents of the report to the Senate and highlight the five recommendations which deal with the ABC's coverage of regional content. I note that this is a particularly emotive issue in some states. I seek leave to continue my remarks.

Leave granted; debate adjourned.
