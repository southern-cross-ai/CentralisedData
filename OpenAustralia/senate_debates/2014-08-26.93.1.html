Senate debates

Tuesday, 26 August 2014

Notices

Presentation

4:00 pm

David Leyonhjelm (Liberal Democratic Party) | Hansard source

I give notice of my intention to amend business of the Senate Notices of Motion Nos 1 and 2, standing in my name for today, proposing that certain primary industry regulations be disallowed, as follows:

(1) Primary Industries (Customs) Charges Amendment (2014 Measures No. 1) Regulation 2014, by inserting after "That", "items 1 to 6 (hard onions) and 7 and 8 (mangoes) of the Schedule of

(2) the Primary Industries (Excise) Levies Amendment (2014 Measures No. 1) Regulation 2014, by inserting after "That", "items 1 and 2 (mushrooms), 3 to 7 (hard onions) and 8 and 9 (mangoes) of the Schedule of';

I seek leave to make a short statement about the notice of motion I have just given.

Leave granted.

For the information of senators, I intend to amend the notices of the disallowance motion so that the items of the schedules relating to the different products—namely mushrooms, onions and mangos—are readily identifiable. This does not narrow the scope of the disallowance motions. I will move the amended motions on 24 September 2014.
