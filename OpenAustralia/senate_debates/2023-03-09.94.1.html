Statements by Senators

Queensland: Storms

1:59 pm

Nita Green (Australian Labor Party) | Hansard source

While parliament has been sitting this week, the top end of my home state, Queensland, has been experiencing heavy rain, showers and storms as a monsoon trough lingers over the tropics. Yesterday and overnight, the severe weather has been concentrated in the north-west Gulf Country , northern goldfields, Upper Flinders and Central West districts of the state. Today, as the trough moves further south, heavy rainfall will continue to impact Queensland. I want to thank the Minister for Emergency Management, Senator Murray Watt, who assures me that the government continues to monitor the situation closely.

Sue Lines (President) | Hansard source

Thank you, Senator Green. The time for two-minute statements has expired.
