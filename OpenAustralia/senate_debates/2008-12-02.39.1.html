Senate debates

Tuesday, 2 December 2008

MR Joern Utzon

3:47 pm

Anne McEwen (Australian Labor Party) | Hansard source

At the request of Senator Hutchins, I move:

That the Senate—
(a)
notes:
(i)
with sadness, the passing of the architect of the Sydney Opera House , Mr Joern Utzon, on 29 November 2008, and
(ii)
the immense contribution that he made to Australia’s architecture and international image;
(b)
commends the reconciliation between Mr Utzon and the New South Wales Government following their 1966 fallout; and
(c)
offers its condolences to his family, including his wife, children and grandchildren.

Question agreed to.
