STATEMENT OF REASONS FOR IN TRODUCTION AND PASSAGE IN THE 2015 AUTUMN SITTINGS

PUBLIC GOVERNANCE AND RESOURCES LEGISLATION AMENDMENT BILL (No. 1) 2015

Purpose of the Bill

The Bill would support the ongoing implementation of the Public Governance, Performance and Accountability Act 2013 (PGPA Act) which commenced on 1 July 2014. For example, the Bill would make:

    Public Governance, Performance and Accountability (Consequential and Transitional Provisions) Act 2014

Reasons for Urgency

A number of amendments contained in the Bill are required to be in place for the new financial year, commencing 1 July 2015. There are no administrative alternatives to implement the effect of these amendments and as a result, there would be several adverse results if the Bill is not passed by 1 July 2015. For example, the Bill would amend the:

    Financial Management and Accountability Act 1997
