Senate debates

Thursday, 14 September 2006

Committees

Selection of Bills Committee; Report

6:03 pm

The Acting Deputy President:

by leave—I move:

That the order of the Senate agreed to earlier today adopting the 10th report of 2006 of the Selection of Bills Committee be varied to provide that the Environment, Communications, Information Technology and the Arts Committee report on the Broadcasting Services Amendment ( Media Ownership ) Bill 2006 and three related bills by 6 October 2006.

Question agreed to.

Order! The time for consideration of general business having expired, the Senate will now proceed to consideration of committee reports, government responses and Auditor-General’s reports, as printed on page 5 of the Notice Paper .
