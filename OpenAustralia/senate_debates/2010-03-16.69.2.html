Senate debates

Tuesday, 16 March 2010

Parliamentary Zone

Proposal for Works

4:59 pm

Guy Barnett (Liberal Party, Chairman of the Scrutiny of Government Waste Committee) | Hansard source

In accordance with the provisions of the Parliament Act 1974, I present a proposal by the Department of Parliamentary Services for works within the Parliamentary Zone, to construct a vehicle storage facility near the Parliament House loading dock.

John Faulkner (Australian Labor Party, Vice-President of the Executive Council) | Hansard source

I seek leave to give notice of a motion in relation to the approval of the works proposal in the Parliamentary Zone.

Leave granted.

I give notice that on Thursday, 18 March 2010 I shall move:

That, in accordance with section 5 of the Parliament Act 1974, the Senate approves the proposal by the Department of Parliamentary Services to construct a vehicle storage facility near the Parliament House loading dock.
