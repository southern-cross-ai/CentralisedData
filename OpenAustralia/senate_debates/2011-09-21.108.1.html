Senate debates

Wednesday, 21 September 2011

Committees

Finance and Public Administration Legislation Committee; Reporting Date

Scott Ryan (Liberal Party, Shadow Parliamentary Secretary for Small Business and Fair Competition) | Hansard source

I seek leave to amend general business notice of motion 440 before seeking leave to have the motion taken as a formal motion.

Leave granted.

I amend the notice of motion by omitting '12 October 2011' and substituting '6 October 2011'. I now move:

That the time for the presentation of the final report of the Finance and Public Administration Legislation Committee on the exposure drafts of Australian privacy amendment legislation be extended to 6 October 2011.

Question agreed to.
