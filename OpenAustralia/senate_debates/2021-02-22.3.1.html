Senate debates

Monday, 22 February 2021

Business

Rearrangement

10:01 am

Simon Birmingham (Liberal Party, Minister for Finance) | Hansard source

by leave—I move:

That—

(a) a motion to take note of a statement made by the Minister for Defence (Senator Reynolds) on 18 February 2021 may be moved immediately;

(b) senators may speak to the motion for not more than 10 minutes each and the time limit for debate be 30 minutes; and

(c) following consideration of the motion, the routine of business till not later than 12.50 pm be general business orders of the day for the consideration of bills only, after which the Senate return to its routine of business.

Question agreed to.
