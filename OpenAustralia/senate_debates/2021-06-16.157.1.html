Senate debates

Wednesday, 16 June 2021

Bills

Ministerial Suitability Commission of Inquiry Bill 2021; First Reading

3:55 pm

Larissa Waters (Australian Greens) | Hansard source

I move:

That the following bill be introduced: A Bill for an Act to establish an inquiry into whether Christian Porter is a fit and proper person to be a Minister of State , and for related purposes. Ministerial Suitability Commission of Inquiry Bill 2021 .

Question agreed to.

I present the bill and move:

That this bill may proceed without formalities and be now read a first time.

Scott Ryan (President) | Hansard source

The question is that the bill being introduced by Senator Waters be read a first time.
