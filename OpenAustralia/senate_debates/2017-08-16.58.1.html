Senate debates

Wednesday, 16 August 2017

Questions without Notice

Deputy Prime Minister

2:11 pm

Don Farrell (Australian Labor Party, Shadow Minister for the Centenary of ANZAC) | Hansard source

My question is to the Minister representing the Deputy Prime Minister, Senator Nash. On 30 July, the Deputy Prime Minister said: 'I'm not a New Zealand er. I'm only too happy for anybody to go out and clarify that.' Given that we now know that the Deputy Prime Minister was, in fact, a citizen of New Zealand, why didn't he clarify his own status when he invited others to do so?

2:12 pm

Fiona Nash (National Party, Deputy Leader of the Nationals) | Hansard source

The senator would be aware that this is a matter that is now in front of the High Court. The Deputy Prime Minister has made a statement regarding this issue, and I would refer the senator to that statement.
