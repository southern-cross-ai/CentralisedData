I'm going to speak today about the ongoing and increasing scourge of deaths in workplaces across Australia. This year, 194 workers have tragically lost their lives in industrial accidents. That's more than four a week. That's more than one every two days in Australia. These horrible occurrences are particularly prominent in the construction, mining, transportation and horticulture industries. In particular, 16 people in the Seasonal Worker Program have died, a stark increase from the zero deaths in the program between 2017 and 2019. This year, 2021, marks the second year running that the rate of industrial deaths has risen. The Liberals and Nationals refuse to implement the reforms needed to ensure accountability and safety for our worksites, and tragically workers continue to die needlessly.
