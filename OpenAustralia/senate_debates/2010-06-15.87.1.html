Documents

Departmental and Agency Contracts

5:53 pm

David Bushby (Liberal Party) | Hansard source

I seek leave to move a motion relating to all documents listed on page 12 and 13 of the Order of Business under section 15(g).

Leave granted.

I move:

That the Senate take note of the documents.

I ask to have them all separately listed on the Notice Paper and seek leave to continue my remarks later.

Leave granted; debate adjourned.

I am advised only 15(d) and (e) will be reserved, so if there are other items under (a), (b) or (c) then you might wish to seek leave to take note of the reports and to continue your remarks.
