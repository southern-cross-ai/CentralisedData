4:03 pm

Ian Macdonald (Liberal Party) | Hansard source

In moving motion No. 779, relating to the Waltzing Matilda Centre in Winton and its destruction by fire earlier this year, I note that the Mayor of the Winton Shire is currently in this parliament building. I move:
