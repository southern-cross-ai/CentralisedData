Senate debates

Monday, 3 December 2018

Motions

Farmers' and Landholders' Rights

4:12 pm

Anne Ruston (Liberal Party, Assistant Minister for International Development and the Pacific) | Hansard source

I seek leave to make a short statement.

Sue Lines (Deputy-President) | Hansard source

Leave is granted for one minute.

Anne Ruston (Liberal Party, Assistant Minister for International Development and the Pacific) | Hansard source

The regulation of onshore mining activities is a state responsibility. The government recognises the importance of farmers' rights over their land but also the value and economic importance of secure rights to exploration and development. The government encourages states to balance all of these factors when legislating these matters.

Sue Lines (Deputy-President) | Hansard source

The question is that general business notice of motion No. 1271, standing in the names of Senators Hanson-Young and Waters be agreed to.
