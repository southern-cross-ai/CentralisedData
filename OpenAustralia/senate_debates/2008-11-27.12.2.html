Senate debates

Thursday, 27 November 2008

Parliamentary Zone

Approval of Works

9:38 am

Joe Ludwig (Australian Labor Party, Manager of Government Business in the Senate) | Hansard source

I move:

That, in accordance with section 5 of the Parliament Act 1974 , the Senate approves the proposal by the National Capital Authority for capital works within the Parliamentary Zone, for the construction of a new cooling plant enclosure.

Question agreed to.
