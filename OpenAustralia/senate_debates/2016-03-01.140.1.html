Senate debates

Tuesday, 1 March 2016

Committees

Environment and Communications References Committee, Foreign Affairs, Defence and Trade References Committee; Membership

5:25 pm

Zed Seselja (Liberal Party) | Hansard source

The President has received letters requesting changes in the membership of committees.

Mathias Cormann (Liberal Party, Minister for Finance) | Hansard source

I move:

That senators be discharged from and appointed to committees as follows:

Environment and Communications References Committee—

Appointed—

Substitute member: Senator Brown to replace Senator Bullock for the committee's inquiry into large capacity fishing vessels

Participating member: Senator Bullock

Foreign Affairs, Defence and Trade References Committee—

Appointed—

Substitute member: Senator Whish-Wilson to replace Senator Ludlam for the committee's inquiry into the Australian Defence Force resistance to interrogation training

Participating member: Senator Ludlam.

Question agreed to.
