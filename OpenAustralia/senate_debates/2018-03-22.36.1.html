12:08 pm

James McGrath (Liberal National Party, Assistant Minister to the Prime Minister) | Hansard source

by leave—I move:

That senators be discharged from and appointed to committees as follows:

Community Affairs References Committee—

Appointed—Substitute members:

Senator Polley to replace Senator Keneally for the committee's inquiry into aged care assessment and accreditation

Senator O'Neill to replace Senator Watt for the committee's inquiry into mental health services in rural and remote Australia

Participating members: Senators Keneally and Watt

Economics Legislation Committee—

Appointed—Substitute member: Senator Rhiannon to replace Senator Whish-Wilson for the committee's inquiry into the National Housing Finance and Investment Corporation Bill 2018 and a related bill

Economics References Committee—

Appointed—Substitute member: Senator Hanson-Young to replace Senator Whish-Wilson for the committee's inquiry into a national radioactive waste management facility at Kimba and Hawker, South Australia

Question agreed to.
