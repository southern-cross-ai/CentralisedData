Senate debates

Thursday, 5 December 2019

Business

Consideration of Legislation

12:08 pm

Anne Ruston (Liberal Party, Minister for Families and Social Services) | Hansard source

I move:

That the provisions of paragraphs (5) to (8) of standing order 111 not apply to the following bills, allowing them to be considered during this period of sittings:

(1) Australian Crime Commission Amendment ( Special Operations and Special Investigations) Bill 2019

(2) Farm Household Support Amendment (Relief Measures) Bill (No. 2) 2019

(3) Interactive Gambling Amendment (National Self-exclusion Register) Bill 2019

(4) National Self-exclusion Register (Cost Recovery Levy) Bill 2019

(5) Special Recreational Vessels Bill 2019.

12:09 pm

Rachel Siewert (Australian Greens) | Hansard source

Mr President , can I ask that a number of the bills be put separately? We have a different opinion on them.

Scott Ryan (President) | Hansard source

Yes. Which bills would you like considered separately?

Rachel Siewert (Australian Greens) | Hansard source

We are supportive of the Farm Household Support Amendment (Relief Measures) Bill (No. 2) 2019, the Interactive Gambling Amendment (National Self-exclusion Register) Bill 2019 and the National Self-exclusion Register (Cost Recovery Levy) Bill 2019 being done together. We'd like to vote differently on the Australian Crime Commission Amendment ( Special Operations and Special Investigations) Bill 2019 and the Special Recreational Vessels Bill 2019.

Scott Ryan (President) | Hansard source

I will separate those two. I will put the motion with respect to the Farm Household Support Amendment (Relief Measures) Bill (No. 2) 2019, the Interactive Gambling Amendment (National Self-exclusion Register) Bill 2019 and the National Self-exclusion Register (Cost Recovery Levy) Bill 2019. So, the question is that the motion with respect to those three bills be agreed to.

Question agreed to.

I will now put the motion on the remaining two bills: the Australian Crime Commission Amendment ( Special Operations and Special Investigations) Bill 2019 and the Special Recreational Vessels Bill 2019. The question is that the motion with respect to those two bills be agreed to.
