Senate debates

Monday, 7 December 2020

Business

Senate Temporary Orders

4:25 pm

Larissa Waters (Australian Greens) | Hansard source

I move:

That paragraph (a) of the temporary order agreed to on 18 June 2020 relating to the consideration of formal motions be varied to provide that:

(a) minor party and independent senators are entitled to six motions per day;

(b) senators can make (or have made on their behalf) any number of requests for formality in any sitting week;

(c) orders for the production of documents are excluded from the restrictions under the temporary order; and

(d) motions not determined on the day that they are listed for consideration will be re-listed on the next sitting day and will be excluded from the restrictions under the temporary order.

Sue Lines (Deputy-President) | Hansard source

The question is that general business notice of motion No. 915, standing in the name of Senator Waters, be agreed to.
