Senate debates

Thursday, 18 March 2010

World Water Day

9:39 am

Rachel Siewert (Australian Greens) | Hansard source

I seek leave to amend in the terms circulated in the chamber general business notice of motion No. 757, standing in my name for today, relating to World Water Day .

Leave granted.

I ask that the amended motion be taken as a formal motion.

John Hogg (President) | Hansard source

Is there any objection to this motion as amended being taken as formal?

Kerry O'Brien (Australian Labor Party) | Hansard source

I have not seen the amended form. I apologise if it has been circulated and I have not got it. I wonder if this could be postponed so I can check it.

John Hogg (President) | Hansard source

We will defer consideration of this matter and come back to it.
