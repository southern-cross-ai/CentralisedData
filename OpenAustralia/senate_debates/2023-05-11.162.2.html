3:52 pm

Andrew McLachlan (Deputy-President) | Hansard source

That senators be discharged from and appointed to committees as follows:

Participating member: Senator Shoebridge

Rural and Regional Affairs and Tran sport Legislation Committee—

Appointed—

Substitute member: Senator Faruqi to replace Senator Whish-Wilson for the committee's inquiry into the Customs Legislation Amendment (Commercial Greyhound Export and Import Prohibition) Bill 2021

Participating member: Senator Whish-Wilson

Question agreed to.
