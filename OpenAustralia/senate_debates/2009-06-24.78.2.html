Senate debates

Wednesday, 24 June 2009

Parthenon Marbles

3:48 pm

Bob Brown (Australian Greens) | Hansard source

I move:

That the Senate calls on the Australian Government to make representations to the British Government to undertake the permanent return of the so-called ‘ Elgin Marbles ’ to the Greek Government and the people of Greece recognising that the marbles were part of the Parthenon at the Acropolis in Athens and should be returned to their historical home.

Nick Xenophon (Independent) | Hansard source

I seek leave to amend general business notice of motion No. 484 standing in the name of Senator Bob Brown by omitting the words ‘so-called Elgin Marbles ’ and substituting them with the words ‘the Parthenon Marbles ’.

Leave granted.

Bob Brown (Australian Greens) | Hansard source

by leave—I note this very reasonable amendment and Senator Xenophon’s Grecian earnestness. I am very happy to accept the amendment. I move the motion as amended:

That the Senate calls on the Australian Government to make representations to the British Government to undertake the permanent return of the Parthenon Marbles to the Greek Government and the people of Greece recognising that the marbles were part of the Parthenon at the Acropolis in Athens and should be returned to their historical home.

Barnaby Joyce (National Party) | Hansard source

I just want to say that I feel wonderful about the whole day!

Alan Ferguson (Deputy-President) | Hansard source

You need to seek leave to say anything, Senator Joyce, so let us not confuse the issue.

Question agreed to.
