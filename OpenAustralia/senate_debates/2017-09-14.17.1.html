Senate debates

Thursday, 14 September 2017

Parliamentary Zone

Approval of Works

11:11 am

James McGrath (Liberal National Party, Assistant Minister to the Prime Minister) | Hansard source

At the request of the Minister for Defence, Senator Payne, I move:

That, in accordance with section 5 of the Parliament Act 1974 , the Senate approves the proposal by the National Capital Authority for capital works within the Parliamentary Zone, relating to the demolition of the kiosk adjoining the Lobby restaurant in Parkes.

Gavin Marshall (Australian Labor Party) | Hansard source

The question is that the motion moved by Senator McGrath be agreed to. Those of that opinion say aye. The ayes have it.

Rachel Siewert (Australian Greens) | Hansard source

Madam Deputy President, I may have misheard you or didn't hear you call for the noes.

Gavin Marshall (Australian Labor Party) | Hansard source

I called the ayes have it.

Rachel Siewert (Australian Greens) | Hansard source

You called for the ayes but you didn't call for the noes, at least as I heard it, and I want to be able to call no.

Gavin Marshall (Australian Labor Party) | Hansard source

I will put government business notice of motion No. 1 again, the motion as moved by Senator McGrath. Those of that opinion say aye and against say no. The ayes have it.

Question agreed to.
