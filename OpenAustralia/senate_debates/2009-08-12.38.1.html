Senate debates

Wednesday, 12 August 2009

Questions without Notice: Additional Answers

Budget

3:01 pm

Stephen Conroy (Australian Labor Party, Deputy Leader of the Government in the Senate) | Hansard source

I have a response to the question Senator Joyce asked yesterday. Unfortunately, he directed it to the wrong portfolio. The Treasury have advised me that the answer to his question is that the current face value of all Commonwealth government securities on issue is $107.5 billion. I thank the Treasury for providing that information to me.

Barnaby Joyce (National Party) | Hansard source

I just want to clarify that. Did you say 107.5?

Stephen Conroy (Australian Labor Party, Deputy Leader of the Government in the Senate) | Hansard source

Yes. That is the answer they have given me.
