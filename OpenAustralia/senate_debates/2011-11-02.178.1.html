Senate debates

Wednesday, 2 November 2011

Questions on Notice

Social Housing and Homelessness (Question No. 1189)

Eric Abetz (Liberal Party, Shadow Minister for Employment and Workplace Relations) | Hansard source

asked the Minister for Social Housing and Homelessness, upon notice, on 13 September 2011:

With reference to the department and all agencies within the Minister's portfolio:

(1) What was the total cost of allowances for government employees or contractors working at sea for the 2010-11 financial year.

(2) What is the daily allowance for working at sea.

(3) How many days in total were spent at sea in the 2010-11 financial year.

Mark Arbib (Australian Labor Party, Minister for Social Housing and Homelessness) | Hansard source

The Minister for Social Housing and Homelessness has provided the following answer to the honourable senator's question:

(1) to (3) Please refer to my answer to Question No. 1169 on behalf of the Minister for Families, Housing, Community Services and Indigenous Affairs.
