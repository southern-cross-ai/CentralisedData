Senate debates

Thursday, 10 February 2022

Regulations and Determinations

Treasury Laws Amendment (Greater Transparency of Proxy Advice) Regulations 2021; Disallowance

11:47 am

Rex Patrick (Independent) | Hansard source

I ask that business of the Senate notice of motion No. 4 be taken as a formal motion.

Slade Brockman (President) | Hansard source

Is there any objection to this motion being taken as formal?

An honourable senator: Yes.

I'm sorry; formality has been denied, Senator Patrick.

Rex Patrick (Independent) | Hansard source

Pursuant to contingent notice of motion, I move:

That so much of the standing orders be suspended as would prevent the motion being moved immediately and determined without amendment or debate.

Slade Brockman (President) | Hansard source

The question is that the suspension of standing orders be agreed to.

11:54 am

Rex Patrick (Independent) | Hansard source

I, and also on behalf of Senator McKim, move:

That the Treasury Laws Amendment (Greater Transparency of Proxy Advice) Regulations 2021, made under the Corporations Act 2001 and the Superannuation Industry (Supervision) Act 1993 , be disallowed [F2021L01801].

Jonathon Duniam (Liberal Party, Assistant Minister for Forestry and Fisheries) | Hansard source

I seek leave to make a short statement.

Leave not granted.

I table the statement.

Slade Brockman (President) | Hansard source

The question is that the motion be agreed to.
