Tuesday, 28 November 2023

Committees

Public Works Joint Committee; Reference

4:25 pm

Katy Gallagher (Australian Labor Party, Minister for the Public Service) | Hansard source

I move general business notices of motion No. 1 and No. 2 together, and I table statements in relation to the works:

GENERAL BUSINESS NO TICE OF MOTION NO. 1

That, in accordance with the provisions of the Public Works Committee Act 1969 , the following proposed work be referred to the Parliamentary Standing Committee on Public Works for consideration and report as expeditiously as is practicable:

RAAF Base Learmonth Redevelopment Enabling KC -30A Operations, Exmouth, Western Australia .

GENERAL BUSINESS NOTICE OF MOTION NO. 2

That, in accordance with the provisions of the Public Works Committee Act 1969 , the following proposed work be referred to the Parliamentary Standing Committee on Public Works for consideration and report as expeditiously as is practicable:

Canberra Defence Precinct Tranche 1 Australian Defence Force Academy Living-in Accommodation Project.

Question agreed to.
