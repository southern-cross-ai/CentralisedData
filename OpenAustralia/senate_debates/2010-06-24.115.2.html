Thursday, 24 June 2010

Business

Consideration of Legislation

12:03 pm

Joe Ludwig (Australian Labor Party, Manager of Government Business in the Senate) | Hansard source

I move:

That the order of the Senate agreed to earlier today relating to the consideration of bills, be varied to add the following bills:No.18––Electoral and Referendum Amendment (How-to-Vote Cards and Other Measures) Bill 2010No. 15–– Social Security and Indigenous Legislation Amendment (Budget and Other Measures) Bill 2010No. 6––Veterans’ Affairs Legislation Amendment (2010 Budget Measures) Bill 2010 and the Veterans’ Entitlements Amendment (Income Support Measures) Bill 2010

I understand I have got agreement on that, but with all of these matters, and because we are moving at a clip, if there is a difficulty we will not proceed with it.

Question agreed to.
