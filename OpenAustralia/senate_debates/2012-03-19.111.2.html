Senate debates

Monday, 19 March 2012

Notices

Presentation

6:03 pm

Bob Brown (Australian Greens) | Hansard source

Mr Acting Deputy President, I seek leave to give notice of a motion.

Christopher Back (Liberal Party) | Hansard source

Could you explain the nature of it before I request whether leave is granted, Senator Bob Brown?

Bob Brown (Australian Greens) | Hansard source

Yes. It is a notice of motion for consideration on Thursday by the Senate.

Christopher Back (Liberal Party) | Hansard source

And the subject matter?

Bob Brown (Australian Greens) | Hansard source

The subject matter is related to the findings of the Committee of Privileges .

Christopher Back (Liberal Party) | Hansard source

Is leave granted?

Mathias Cormann (Liberal Party, Shadow Assistant Treasurer) | Hansard source

No.

Christopher Back (Liberal Party) | Hansard source

Leave is not granted.

Bob Brown (Australian Greens) | Hansard source

I seek leave on another matter.

Christopher Back (Liberal Party) | Hansard source

Could you explain that matter, Senator Bob Brown?

Bob Brown (Australian Greens) | Hansard source

I note that leave was not granted by the coalition. I seek leave to circulate an amendment to the bills before the Senate before 6.15 pm.

6:04 pm

Mathias Cormann (Liberal Party, Shadow Assistant Treasurer) | Hansard source

The coalition is not aware of the various matters that Senator Bob Brown is giving notices of motion about. In those circumstances, we are not in a position to make a decision to grant leave.

Leave not granted.

Christopher Back (Liberal Party) | Hansard source

Thank you, Senator Cormann. We are in the middle of a debate on the motion moved by Senator Fisher. I will call on Senator Marshall.
