Senate debates

Wednesday, 15 June 2011

Committees

Select and Joint Committees; Meeting

3:32 pm

Stephen Parry (Liberal Party) | Hansard source

I ask that notices of motion nos 256, 257, 258 and 259 be taken together and as formal.

Alan Ferguson (Deputy-President) | Hansard source

I s there any objection to these motions being taken together and as formal?

Anne McEwen (Australian Labor Party) | Hansard source

Yes. I note that, although we are not opposing formality of motion N o. 257, the government intends to oppose that motion.

Alan Ferguson (Deputy-President) | Hansard source

I will then separate the motions. I call Senator Parry.

Stephen Parry (Liberal Party) | Hansard source

I move:

That:

(a)   The Select Committee on the Reform of the Australian Federation be authorised to hold a private meeting otherwise than in accordance with standing order 33(1) during the sitting of the Senate on Thursday, 16 June 2011; and

(b)   That the Joint Standing Committee on Treaties be authorised to hold a public meeting during the sitting of the Senate on Monday, 20 June 2011, from 10 am to noon.

Question agreed to.
