Senate debates

Monday, 20 March 2017

Bills

Native Title Amendment (Indigenous Land Use Agreements) Bill 2017; Report of Legislation Committee

8:54 pm

David Fawcett (Liberal Party) | Hansard source

On behalf of the chair of the Legal and Constitutional Affairs Legislation Committee, I present the report of the committee on the provisions of the Native Title Amendment (Indigenous Land Use Agreements) Bill 2017, together with the Hansard record of proceedings and documents presented to the committee. I move:

That the report be printed.

8:55 pm

Sam Dastyari (Australian Labor Party) | Hansard source

Can I just ask for a point of clarification? This is the report that, earlier, when we got to business of the Senate, was not yet physically printed to be able to be tabled. Is that correct?

Stephen Parry (President) | Hansard source

That is correct.

Sam Dastyari (Australian Labor Party) | Hansard source

So this is just the earlier item as it appeared on the Notice Paper . It was not physically prepared at that point in time and now is happening at this time as a result of that.

Stephen Parry (President) | Hansard source

That is correct. The question is that the motion moved by Senator Fawcett be agreed to.

Question agreed to.

Ordered that the report be printed.
