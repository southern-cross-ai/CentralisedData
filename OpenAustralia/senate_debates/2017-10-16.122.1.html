Carol Brown (Australian Labor Party, Shadow Parliamentary Secretary for Families and Payments) | Hansard source

(a) notes that:

(i) 15 to 21 October 2017 is National Carers Week and that it is estimated that there are 2.7 million carers who provide care and support to a family member or friend with a disability, mental illness, chronic condition, terminal illness, or who is frail aged in Australia, and
