Senate debates

Wednesday, 12 October 2016

Questions without Notice

Attorney-General

2:49 pm

Katy Gallagher (Australian Labor Party) | Hansard source

My question is to the Minister representing the Prime Minister, Senator Brandis. Has the Prime Minister sought advice regarding the current conflict between Australia's first and second law officers from either the Department of the Prime Minister and Cabinet or the Attorney-General?

2:50 pm

George Brandis (Liberal Party, Attorney-General) | Hansard source

Not from me. As to PM &C, not that I am aware.

Stephen Parry (President) | Hansard source

Senator Gallagher, a supplementary question?

2:52 pm

Katy Gallagher (Australian Labor Party) | Hansard source

Has the Prime Minister sought any explanation from the Attorney-General himself regarding the ongoing dispute between the Attorney-General and the Solicitor-General?

2:50 pm

George Brandis (Liberal Party, Attorney-General) | Hansard source

Senator, I do not disclose—for reasons that you will well understand—conversations with ministerial colleagues.

Stephen Parry (President) | Hansard source

A final supplementary question, Senator Gallagher?

Katy Gallagher (Australian Labor Party) | Hansard source

Has the Prime Minister discussed the conflict between the Attorney-General and the Solicitor-General with the Solicitor-General, and has the Prime Minister expressed his confidence in the Solicitor-General?

2:51 pm

George Brandis (Liberal Party, Attorney-General) | Hansard source

You are the one who raises the issue, not me, Senator. As to the latter part of the question, you are the one who raises the issue, not me. As to the former part of the question, not so far as I am aware.
