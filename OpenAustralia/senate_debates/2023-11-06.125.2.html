Senate debates

Monday, 6 November 2023

Bills

Fair Work Legislation Amendment (Small Business Redundancy Exemption) Bill 2023, Fair Work Legislation Amendment (Strengthening Protections Against Discrimination) Bill 2023, Fair Work Legislation Amendment (Asbestos Safety and Eradication Agency) Bill 2023, Fair Work Legislation Amendment (First Responders) Bill 2023; First Reading

4:10 pm

Jacqui Lambie (Jacqui Lambie Network) | Hansard source

I, and on behalf of Senator David Pocock, move:

That the following bills be introduced:

A Bill for an Act to amend the law relating to workers' compensation and rehabilitation, and for related purposes.

A Bill for an Act to amend the law relating to workplace relations, and work health and safety, and for related purposes.

A Bill for an Act to amend the law relating to the Asbestos Safety and Eradication Agency, and for related purposes.

A Bill for an Act to amend the law relating to work health and safety, workers' compensation and rehabilitation, and for related purposes.

Question agreed to.

I present the bills and move:

That these bills may proceed without formalities, may be taken together and be now read a first time.

Question agreed to.

Bills read a first time.
